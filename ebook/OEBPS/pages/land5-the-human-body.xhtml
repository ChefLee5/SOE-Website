# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Head /HED/ Felix balances a book on top of his head. Bent hand touches temple, then chin
2 Forehead /FOR-hed/ Amara feels her forehead to check for a fever. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Temple /TEM-pul/ The temple is the flat area on the side of the forehead. Fingerspell TEMPLE
4 Eyebrow /EYE-brow/ Felix raises one eyebrow when he's curious. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Eyelid /EYE-lid/ The eyelid closes to protect the eye. Fingerspell EYELID
6 Eyelash /EYE-lash/ A tiny eyelash fell on Amara's cheek. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Eye /EYE/ Felix has bright brown eyes that see everything. Point to eye
8 Pupil /PYOO-pul/ The black pupil in the center of the eye gets bigger in the dark. Fingerspell PUPIL
9 Iris /EYE-ris/ The iris is the colorful part of the eye. Fingerspell IRIS
10 Nose /NOHZ/ Amara wrinkles her nose when she laughs. Point to nose
11 Nostril /NOS-trul/ Air flows in and out through each nostril. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Cheek /CHEEK/ Felix's cheeks turn red when he runs fast. Fingerspell CHEEK
13 Ear /EER/ The ear catches sound waves from every direction. Point to ear
14 Earlobe /EER-lohb/ The soft earlobe hangs at the bottom of the ear. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Mouth /MOWTH/ Open your mouth wide and say "Ahh." Point to mouth
16 Lip /LIP/ Amara puts balm on her dry lip. Fingerspell LIP
17 Tongue /TUNG/ The tongue helps you taste and speak. Point to tongue
18 Tooth /TOOTH/ Felix lost his first baby tooth last week. Fingerspell TOOTH
19 Gum /GUM/ The gums hold your teeth in place. Fingerspell GUM
20 Chin /CHIN/ Felix rests his chin on his hand while thinking. Fingerspell CHIN
21 Jaw /JAW/ The jaw moves up and down when you chew food. Fingerspell JAW
22 Neck /NEK/ Amara wraps a warm scarf around her neck in winter. Bent hand touches side of neck
23 Throat /THROHT/ A sore throat makes it hard to swallow. Fingerspell THROAT
24 Shoulder /SHOHL-der/ Felix puts his backpack over one shoulder. Palm taps opposite shoulder
25 Arm /ARM/ Amara stretches both arms above her head. Hand runs down opposite arm
26 Elbow /EL-boh/ The elbow is the joint in the middle of the arm. Fingerspell ELBOW
27 Forearm /FOR-arm/ The forearm connects the elbow to the wrist. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Wrist /RIST/ Amara wears a colorful bracelet on her wrist. Fingerspell WRIST
29 Hand /HAND/ Felix waves his hand to say hello. Flat hand chops opposite wrist
30 Palm /PAHM/ Place the seed in the palm of your hand. Fingerspell PALM
31 Finger /FING-ger/ Point your finger at the word and say it aloud. Point to and wiggle finger
32 Thumb /THUM/ The thumb helps you grip and hold things. Point to thumb
33 Knuckle /NUK-ul/ Each finger has three small knuckles. Fingerspell or sign each word
34 Fingernail /FING-ger-nayl/ Keep your fingernails clean and trimmed. Fingerspell or sign each word
35 Chest /CHEST/ Take a deep breath and feel your chest expand. Pat chest
36 Rib /RIB/ The ribs protect the heart and lungs inside the chest. Fingerspell RIB
37 Back /BAK/ Amara stretches her back after sitting for a long time. Pat back with hand over shoulder
38 Spine /SPYN/ The spine is the long bone that runs down the center of the back. Fingerspell SPINE
39 Waist /WAYST/ Felix ties a belt around his waist. Fingerspell WAIST
40 Hip /HIP/ The hip joint allows the leg to move in many directions. Fingerspell HIP
41 Abdomen /AB-doh-men/ The abdomen is the area between the chest and the hips. Fingerspell or sign each word
42 Thigh /THY/ The thigh is the strong upper part of the leg. Fingerspell THIGH
43 Knee /NEE/ Felix bends his knee when he stretches. Point to knee
44 Shin /SHIN/ The shin is the front part of the leg below the knee. Fingerspell SHIN
45 Calf /KAF/ The calf muscle at the back of the lower leg helps you jump. Fingerspell CALF
46 Ankle /ANG-kul/ The ankle connects the foot to the leg. Fingerspell ANKLE
47 Foot /FOOT/ Each foot has five toes at the end. Point to foot
48 Heel /HEEL/ The heel is the back part of the foot. Fingerspell HEEL
49 Toe /TOH/ Wiggle each toe to wake up your feet! Point to toe area
50 Skin /SKIN/ Skin is the largest organ of the body. Pinch skin on hand
