# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Inch /INCH/ One inch is about the width of your thumb. Index and thumb pinch close together
2 Foot /FOOT/ One foot equals twelve inches. Point to foot
3 Yard /YARD/ One yard equals three feet. Fingerspell YARD
4 Mile /MYL/ The school is one mile from Kwame's house. M-hand moves forward
5 Centimeter /SEN-tih-mee-ter/ A centimeter is a very small unit of length. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Meter /MEE-ter/ A meter is a little longer than a yard. Fingerspell METER
7 Kilometer /KIL-oh-mee-ter/ A kilometer is a thousand meters. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Ounce /OWNS/ An ounce is a small unit of weight. Fingerspell OUNCE
9 Pound /POWND/ One pound equals sixteen ounces. P-hand drops firmly
10 Ton /TUN/ An elephant weighs several tons. Fingerspell TON
11 Gram /GRAM/ A gram is a metric unit of weight. Fingerspell GRAM
12 Kilogram /KIL-oh-gram/ A kilogram equals about 2.2 pounds. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Cup /KUP/ Use one cup of flour for the recipe. C-hand taps opposite flat palm (cup on saucer)
14 Pint /PYNT/ A pint of ice cream is a small container. Fingerspell PINT
15 Quart /KWORT/ A quart of milk is a common size. Fingerspell QUART
16 Gallon /GAL-un/ A gallon is the biggest jug of milk. G-hand large volume shape
17 Liter /LEE-ter/ A liter is a metric unit of liquid. Fingerspell LITER
18 Tablespoon /TAY-bul-spoon/ A tablespoon holds three teaspoons. Fingerspell T-B-S or sign BIG + SPOON
19 Teaspoon /TEE-spoon/ Add one teaspoon of salt to the soup. Fingerspell T-S-P or sign SMALL + SPOON
20 Temperature /TEM-per-uh-chur/ Temperature is measured in degrees. Index slides up/down opposite index (thermometer)
21 Fahrenheit /FAIR-un-hyt/ Water boils at 212 degrees Fahrenheit. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Celsius /SEL-see-us/ Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Scale /SKAYL/ Step on the scale to measure your weight. Both flat hands balance like scale
24 Ruler /ROO-ler/ Use a ruler to measure length in inches. R-hands outline ruler
25 Tape measure /TAYP MEZH-er/ A tape measure is flexible and rolls up. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Length /LENGTH/ The length of the table is four feet. Fingerspell LENGTH
27 Width /WIDTH/ The width is how wide something is. Fingerspell WIDTH
28 Height /HYT/ Kwame's height is four feet and six inches. Fingerspell HEIGHT
29 Weight /WAYT/ The weight of the backpack is ten pounds. Fingerspell WEIGHT
30 Volume /VOL-yoom/ Volume measures how much space something fills. Fingerspell VOLUME
31 Distance /DIS-tuns/ The distance from home to school is two miles. A-hand pushes forward from opposite A-hand
32 Area /AIR-ee-uh/ The area of the room is one hundred square feet. Fingerspell AREA
33 Perimeter /per-IM-ih-ter/ The perimeter is the distance around the outside. Fingerspell or sign each word
34 Speed /SPEED/ The speed limit is thirty miles per hour. Both S-hands flick to 5-hands quickly
35 Measure /MEZH-er/ Measure the room before buying new furniture. Both Y-hands tap together (SAME SIZE)
