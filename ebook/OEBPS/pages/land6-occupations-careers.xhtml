# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Job /JOB/ A job is work you do to earn money. S-hand taps opposite wrist (WORK)
2 Career /kuh-REER/ A career is a long-term path in a field you love. Fingerspell CAREER
3 Boss /BOS/ The boss manages the workers in the office. B-hand taps shoulder (military stripes)
4 Employee /em-PLOY-ee/ An employee works for a company or business. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Employer /em-PLOY-er/ An employer is the person or company that hires you. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Salary /SAL-uh-ree/ A salary is the money paid for a full year of work. Flat hand scoops off opposite palm toward body
7 Wages /WAY-jez/ Wages are money paid by the hour. Fingerspell WAGES
8 Benefits /BEN-ih-fits/ Benefits may include health insurance and vacation days. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Resume /REZ-oo-may/ A resume lists your skills, education, and work experience. Fingerspell RESUME
10 Interview /IN-ter-vyoo/ Dress professionally for the job interview. I-hands alternate near mouth
11 Application /ap-lih-KAY-shun/ Fill out the application form to apply for the job. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Hire /HYR/ The company will hire two new employees next month. H-hand sweeps in toward body
13 Promotion /proh-MOH-shun/ A promotion means moving to a higher position. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Part-time /PART-tym/ A part-time job is fewer than forty hours a week. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Full-time /FOOL-tym/ A full-time job is usually forty hours per week. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Shift /SHIFT/ Ezra works the morning shift from six to two. Fingerspell SHIFT
17 Overtime /OH-ver-tym/ Working extra hours is called overtime. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Coworker /KOH-wur-ker/ A coworker is a person you work with. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Manager /MAN-uh-jer/ The manager assigns tasks to the team. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Retire /rih-TYR/ Grandfather plans to retire at age sixty-five. Both R-hands relax downward
21 Volunteer /vol-un-TEER/ A volunteer works without pay to help the community. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Skill /SKIL/ Typing is a useful skill for office work. Fingerspell SKILL
23 Experience /ek-SPEER-ee-ens/ Three years of experience is required for this position. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Qualification /kwol-ih-fih-KAY-shun/ A qualification proves you are trained for the job. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Deadline /DED-lyn/ The deadline for the project is Friday. Fingerspell or sign each word
