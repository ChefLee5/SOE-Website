Every action starts with a VERB. These are the words that make things happen!

A richly detailed scene depicting 'Body Movement Verbs' with numbered callout markers (1–25) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Run /RUN/ Run to the finish line as fast as you can. L-hands, index pulls on opposite thumb, move forward
2 Walk /WAWK/ Walk carefully on the wet floor. Both flat hands walk forward alternately
3 Jump /JUMP/ Jump over the puddle! Fingerspell JUMP
4 Sit /SIT/ Sit down in your chair, please. Bent H-hooks sit on opposite bent H
5 Stand /STAND/ Stand up when the teacher enters. V-fingers stand on opposite flat palm
6 Climb /KLYM/ Climb the stairs to the second floor. Fingerspell CLIMB
7 Crawl /KRAWL/ The baby can crawl across the room. Fingerspell CRAWL
8 Bend /BEND/ Bend your knees when you lift heavy things. Fingerspell BEND
9 Stretch /STRECH/ Stretch your arms above your head. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Push /PUSH/ Push the door open gently. Fingerspell PUSH
11 Pull /PUL/ Pull the handle to open the drawer. Fingerspell PULL
12 Lift /LIFT/ Lift the box with both hands. Fingerspell LIFT
13 Carry /KAIR-ee/ Carry the groceries into the kitchen. Fingerspell CARRY
14 Throw /THROH/ Throw the ball to your partner. Fingerspell THROW
15 Catch /KACH/ Catch the ball with two hands. Fingerspell CATCH
16 Kick /KIK/ Kick the soccer ball into the goal. Fingerspell KICK
17 Swim /SWIM/ I like to swim at the community pool. Fingerspell SWIM
18 Dance /DANS/ Dance to the rhythm of the music! Fingerspell DANCE
19 Clap /KLAP/ Clap your hands to the beat. Fingerspell CLAP
20 Wave /WAYV/ Wave goodbye to your friends. Open hand waves side to side
21 Point /POYNT/ Point to the correct answer on the board. Fingerspell POINT
22 Touch /TUCH/ Touch your toes without bending your knees. Fingerspell TOUCH
23 Hold /HOHLD/ Hold the baby carefully. Fingerspell HOLD
24 Drop /DROP/ Be careful not to drop the glass. Fingerspell DROP
25 Shake /SHAYK/ Shake hands when you meet someone new. Fingerspell SHAKE
