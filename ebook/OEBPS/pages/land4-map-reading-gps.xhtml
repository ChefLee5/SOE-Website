# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Map /MAP/ Unfold the map to find your location. M-hands trace on flat surface
2 GPS /JEE-PEE-ES/ The GPS recalculates when you make a wrong turn. Fingerspell GPS
3 North /NORTH/ The compass needle points north. N-hand moves up
4 South /SOWTH/ Texas is in the south of the United States. S-hand moves down
5 East /EEST/ The sun rises in the east. E-hand moves right
6 West /WEST/ The sun sets in the west. W-hand moves left
7 Landmark /LAND-mark/ The Statue of Liberty is a famous landmark. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Navigate /NAV-ih-gayt/ Navigate to the store using your phone. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Compass /KUM-pus/ A compass shows magnetic north. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Legend /LEJ-end/ The map legend explains the symbols. Fingerspell LEGEND
11 Scale /SKAYL/ The scale shows one inch equals one mile. Both flat hands balance like scale
12 Coordinate /koh-OR-dih-nit/ Enter the coordinates into the GPS. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Latitude /LAT-ih-tood/ Latitude measures position north or south. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Longitude /LON-jih-tood/ Longitude measures east and west position. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Destination /des-tih-NAY-shun/ Your destination is on the right. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Intersection /in-ter-SEK-shun/ Turn left at the next intersection. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Highway /HY-way/ Take the highway for a faster route. Both hands weave forward (busy road)
18 Exit /EK-sit/ Take exit twelve to reach the mall. Fingerspell EXIT
19 Detour /DEE-toor/ Follow the detour around the construction. Fingerspell DETOUR
20 U-turn /YOO-turn/ Make a U-turn at the next light. Fingerspell U-TURN
21 Roundabout /ROWND-uh-bowt/ Go around the roundabout and take the second exit. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 One-way /WUN-way/ This is a one-way street going east. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Dead end /DED END/ The road ends at a dead end. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Scenic route /SEE-nik ROOT/ Take the scenic route to enjoy the view. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Satellite /SAT-uh-lyt/ GPS uses satellite signals to locate you. Fingerspell or sign each word
