# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Continent /KON-tih-nent/ There are seven continents on the Earth. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Country /KUN-tree/ A country has its own government and borders. Y-hand circles elbow
3 Island /EYE-lund/ An island is land surrounded by water on all sides. I-hand circles on opposite palm
4 Peninsula /pen-IN-suh-luh/ A peninsula is land surrounded by water on three sides. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Mountain /MOWN-tun/ The snow-capped mountain rises high into the clouds. Flat hand taps fist (ROCK) + both hands peak up
6 Valley /VAL-ee/ The green valley lies between two tall mountains. Both flat hands dip down in center
7 Hill /HIL/ A hill is lower and rounder than a mountain. Flat hand shapes hill curve
8 Plain /PLAYN/ The flat plain stretches for miles with no trees. Fingerspell PLAIN
9 Plateau /pla-TOH/ A plateau is a flat area of land high above sea level. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Desert /DEZ-ert/ The desert is hot and dry with very little rain. Both flat hands push outward (dry, vast)
11 Forest /FOR-est/ A thick forest of trees covers the mountainside. Forearm trees, multiple, shift sideways
12 Jungle /JUNG-gul/ The tropical jungle has dense plants and tall trees. Fingerspell JUNGLE
13 River /RIV-er/ The river flows from the mountain to the sea. W-hand flows forward like water
14 Lake /LAYK/ The calm lake reflects the blue sky. L-hands trace circle shape
15 Pond /POND/ The small pond has ducks and lily pads. Fingerspell POND
16 Waterfall /WAW-ter-fawl/ The waterfall crashes down from the high cliff. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Stream /STREEM/ A narrow stream trickles through the forest. Fingerspell STREAM
18 Canal /kuh-NAL/ Ships travel through the canal between two bodies of water. Fingerspell CANAL
19 Bay /BAY/ The bay is a sheltered area of water along the coast. Fingerspell BAY
20 Coast /KOHST/ The coast is where the land meets the ocean. Fingerspell COAST
21 Beach /BEECH/ Nerissa walks along the sandy beach. Mime spreading towel, then waves
22 Cliff /KLIF/ The steep cliff drops straight down to the water. Fingerspell CLIFF
23 Cave /KAYV/ Ronan explores the dark cave near the shore. Fingerspell CAVE
24 Volcano /vol-KAY-noh/ A volcano can erupt with lava and ash. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Glacier /GLAY-sher/ A glacier is a massive river of ice. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Iceberg /EYS-burg/ Most of the iceberg is hidden below the water. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 North /NORTH/ The compass needle points north. N-hand moves up
28 South /SOWTH/ Travel south toward warmer weather. S-hand moves down
29 East /EEST/ The sun rises in the east every morning. E-hand moves right
30 West /WEST/ The sun sets in the west every evening. W-hand moves left
31 Equator /ih-KWAY-ter/ The equator divides the Earth into north and south halves. Fingerspell or sign each word
32 Hemisphere /HEM-ih-sfeer/ The Northern Hemisphere has different seasons than the Southern. Fingerspell or sign each word
33 Map /MAP/ Use a map to find your way to the destination. M-hands trace on flat surface
34 Compass /KUM-pus/ A compass shows which direction you are facing. Fingerspell or sign each word
35 Border /BOR-der/ A border is the line between two countries. Fingerspell BORDER
36 Capital /KAP-ih-tul/ Washington, D.C. is the capital of the United States. Fingerspell or sign each word
37 Population /pop-yoo-LAY-shun/ The population of the city is two million people. Fingerspell or sign each word
38 Region /REE-jun/ The southwest region is hot and dry. Fingerspell REGION
39 Terrain /tuh-RAYN/ The terrain changes from mountains to desert. Fingerspell or sign each word
40 Globe /GLOHB/ Spin the globe to find a new country. Fingerspell GLOBE
