# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Nutrition /noo-TRISH-un/ Good nutrition means eating foods that help your body. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Protein /PROH-teen/ Chicken, beans, and eggs are rich in protein. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Carbohydrate /kar-boh-HY-drayt/ Bread and rice are carbohydrates that give you energy. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Fiber /FY-ber/ Fruits, vegetables, and whole grains have lots of fiber. Fingerspell FIBER
5 Calcium /KAL-see-um/ Milk and cheese have calcium that makes bones strong. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Iron /EYE-urn/ Spinach and red meat contain iron for healthy blood. Fingerspell IRON
7 Vitamin /VY-tuh-min/ Oranges are full of Vitamin C. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Calorie /KAL-uh-ree/ A calorie is a unit that measures energy in food. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Sugar /SHOOG-er/ Too much sugar is bad for your teeth and body. Fingers brush down chin twice
10 Fat /FAT/ Healthy fats come from nuts, fish, and avocados. Fingerspell FAT
11 Dairy /DAIR-ee/ Milk, yogurt, and cheese belong to the dairy group. Fingerspell DAIRY
12 Grain /GRAYN/ Bread, oatmeal, and pasta are made from grains. Fingerspell GRAIN
13 Whole grain /HOHL GRAYN/ Whole grain bread is healthier than white bread. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Serving size /SUR-ving SYZ/ The serving size tells you how much to eat at one time. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Balanced meal /BAL-unst MEEL/ A balanced meal has protein, grains, and vegetables. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Sodium /SOH-dee-um/ Too much sodium, or salt, raises blood pressure. Fingerspell SODIUM
17 Organic /or-GAN-ik/ Organic vegetables are grown without pesticides. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Portion /POR-shun/ A small portion of rice is about the size of your fist. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Nutrient /NOO-tree-unt/ Every nutrient has a job inside the body. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Food label /FOOD LAY-bul/ Read the food label to know what's inside the package. Fingerspell or sign each word
