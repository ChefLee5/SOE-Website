# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 N /EN/ N is for Name, Night, and Number. Two fingers over thumb, fingers down
2 O /OH/ O is for Ocean, Open, and Orange. All fingers curve to make O shape
3 P /PEE/ P is for Please, Park, and People. K-hand points downward
4 Q /KYOO/ Q is for Question, Quick, and Quiet. G-hand points downward
5 R /AR/ R is for Read, River, and Respect. Cross index and middle fingers
6 S /ES/ S is for School, Smile, and Sun. Fist with thumb over fingers
7 T /TEE/ T is for Teacher, Time, and Tree. Thumb between index and middle
8 U /YOO/ U is for Uncle, Up, and Under. Index and middle up together
9 V /VEE/ V is for Vote, Visit, and Vegetable. Index and middle spread apart (peace sign)
10 W /DUB-ul-yoo/ W is for Water, Walk, and Welcome. Index, middle, ring spread apart
11 X /EKS/ X is for X-ray and eXcited. Index finger bent like a hook
12 Y /WY/ Y is for Yes, Year, and Yellow. Thumb and pinky out, rest closed
13 Z /ZEE/ Z is for Zero, Zoo, and Zone. Index draws letter Z in air
