Scene 13: Emergency Services

Safety is important in Aquaria. Ronan and Nerissa learn who to call and what to do in an emergency.

🖼️ Illustration: Emergency Services
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Emergency Services' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Emergency /ih-MUR-jen-see/ Call 911 in an emergency. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 911 /NYN-WUN-WUN/ Dial 911 for police, fire, or medical help. Fingerspell 911
3 Firefighter /FYR-fy-ter/ The brave firefighter climbs the ladder. B-hand at forehead (shield)
4 Paramedic /pair-uh-MED-ik/ The paramedic gives first aid at the scene. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Police officer /puh-LEES OF-ih-ser/ The police officer keeps the community safe. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 First aid /FURST AYD/ A first aid kit has bandages and antiseptic. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Bandage /BAN-dij/ Put a bandage on the cut to keep it clean. Mime wrapping bandage around hand
8 Fire extinguisher /FYR ek-STING-gwish-er/ The fire extinguisher is mounted on the wall. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Smoke detector /SMOHK dih-TEK-ter/ The smoke detector beeps when it senses smoke. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Escape route /es-KAYP ROOT/ Know the escape route in case of fire. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 CPR /SEE-PEE-AR/ CPR can save a life in a cardiac emergency. Fingerspell CPR
12 Stretcher /STRECH-er/ The paramedic carries the patient on a stretcher. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Siren /SY-ren/ The ambulance siren warns cars to move aside. Fingerspell SIREN
14 Safety /SAYF-tee/ Safety means protecting yourself and others. Fingerspell SAFETY
15 Hazard /HAZ-erd/ A wet floor is a slip hazard. Fingerspell HAZARD
16 Evacuate /ih-VAK-yoo-ayt/ Evacuate the building when the alarm sounds. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Alarm /uh-LARM/ Pull the alarm if you see fire or smoke. Index taps opposite palm rapidly (ringing)
18 Rescue /RES-kyoo/ The coast guard performs a water rescue. Fingerspell RESCUE
19 Dispatcher /dis-PACH-er/ The 911 dispatcher asks what the emergency is. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Safe place /SAYF PLAYS/ Go to a safe place during a tornado warning. Fingerspell or sign each word

Ronan's Tip:

"Memorize this sentence: 'In an emergency, I call 911.' Practice saying your full address out loud — the dispatcher will need it. Knowing these words could SAVE A LIFE."
