# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Speed /SPEED/ The speed limit is thirty-five miles per hour. Both S-hands flick to 5-hands quickly
2 Distance /DIS-tents/ The distance from home to school is two miles. A-hand pushes forward from opposite A-hand
3 Far /FAR/ How far is the nearest gas station? Fingerspell FAR
4 Near /NEER/ The park is near our house. Fingerspell NEAR
5 Close /KLOHS/ Please sit close to the front. B-hands apart, swing together
6 Fast /FAST/ The train moves very fast. L-hands pull back quickly (snapping)
7 Slow /SLOH/ Drive slow in the school zone. Hand drags slowly up opposite forearm
8 Rate /RAYT/ The rate of speed increased. Fingerspell RATE
9 Per /PUR/ The car travels sixty miles per hour. Fingerspell PER
10 Direction /dih-REK-shun/ Turn left at the next direction sign. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Route /ROOT/ Take the shortest route to the airport. Fingerspell ROUTE
12 Map /MAP/ Follow the map to find the treasure. M-hands trace on flat surface
13 GPS /JEE-PEE-ES/ GPS says we arrive in twenty minutes. Fingerspell GPS
14 Navigate /NAV-ih-gayt/ Navigate to the restaurant using your phone. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Step /STEP/ I walked ten thousand steps today. Fingerspell STEP
16 Lap /LAP/ She ran four laps around the track. Fingerspell LAP
17 Marathon /MAIR-uh-thon/ A marathon is twenty-six miles. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Accelerate /ak-SEL-er-ayt/ The car accelerates quickly. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Brake /BRAYK/ Press the brake to slow down. Fingerspell BRAKE
20 Timer /TY-mer/ Set the timer for ten minutes. T-hand taps wrist
21 Stopwatch /STOP-wotch/ Use the stopwatch to time the race. Both S-hands: one taps other (START + WATCH)
22 Record /REK-ord/ She broke the school record. Fingerspell RECORD
23 Average /AV-er-ij/ Average speed was forty-five mph. Flat hand levels across opposite hand
24 Maximum /MAK-sih-mum/ Maximum speed on the highway is sixty-five. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Minimum /MIN-ih-mum/ The minimum age to drive is sixteen. Fingerspell or sign each word
