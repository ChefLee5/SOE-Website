# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 School /SKOOL/ Aiko walks to school every morning at eight o'clock. Clap hands twice
2 Classroom /KLAS-room/ The classroom has desks, a whiteboard, and lots of books. Sign CLASS (C-hands circle) + ROOM
3 Teacher /TEE-cher/ The teacher writes the lesson on the board. Both flat hands at temples, move outward + PERSON
4 Student /STOO-dent/ Each student has a notebook and a pencil. Flat hand lifts from opposite palm + PERSON
5 Desk /DESK/ Kenji keeps his books inside his desk. Sign TABLE (forearms stacked, then flat)
6 Chair /CHAIR/ Push your chair in when you leave the desk. Two bent fingers sit on opposite two fingers
7 Whiteboard /WYT-bord/ The teacher draws a diagram on the whiteboard. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Marker /MAR-ker/ Use the blue marker to underline the key words. Fingerspell MARKER
9 Eraser /ih-RAY-ser/ The eraser removes mistakes from the whiteboard. Fingerspell ERASER
10 Pencil /PEN-sul/ Sharpen your pencil before the quiz. Thumb on lips, then write on palm
11 Pen /PEN/ Sign your paper with a black pen. Pinch dominant hand, write on opposite palm
12 Notebook /NOHT-book/ Aiko writes vocabulary words in her notebook. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Textbook /TEKST-book/ Open your textbook to page forty-two. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Paper /PAY-per/ Take a piece of paper for the drawing activity. Both flat hands brush past each other
15 Folder /FOHL-der/ Keep your homework in the green folder. Fingerspell FOLDER
16 Backpack /BAK-pak/ Kenji puts everything in his backpack. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Ruler /ROO-ler/ Use the ruler to draw a straight line. R-hands outline ruler
18 Scissors /SIZ-erz/ Cut along the dotted line with scissors. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Glue /GLOO/ Glue the picture onto the poster board. Fingerspell GLUE
20 Tape /TAYP/ Use tape to fix the torn page. Fingerspell TAPE
21 Calculator /KAL-kyoo-lay-ter/ The calculator helps you solve math problems quickly. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Computer /kum-PYOO-ter/ Students use the computer for research projects. C-hand bounces up opposite forearm
23 Laptop /LAP-top/ Aiko types her essay on a laptop. Fingerspell LAPTOP
24 Tablet /TAB-let/ The teacher shows a video on the tablet. Fingerspell TABLET
25 Projector /proh-JEK-ter/ The projector displays the lesson on the big screen. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Globe /GLOHB/ Spin the globe and point to a country. Fingerspell GLOBE
27 Map /MAP/ The map on the wall shows all seven continents. M-hands trace on flat surface
28 Clock /KLOK/ The clock on the wall says it's three fifteen. Point to wrist + mime clock hands
29 Bell /BEL/ The bell rings at the end of each class. Fingerspell BELL
30 Homework /HOHM-wurk/ Finish your homework before you watch television. Sign HOME + WORK
31 Test /TEST/ Study hard for the spelling test on Friday. X-hands draw question marks in air, then move down
32 Quiz /KWIZ/ The short quiz has only ten questions. Fingerspell QUIZ
33 Grade /GRAYD/ Kenji earned a good grade on his project. Fingerspell GRADE
34 Report card /rih-PORT KARD/ The report card shows your grades for the semester. Fingerspell or sign each word
35 Recess /REE-ses/ At recess, children play outside on the field. Fingerspell RECESS
36 Lunch period /LUNCH PEER-ee-ud/ The lunch period is thirty minutes long. Fingerspell or sign each word
37 Library /LY-brair-ee/ The library has thousands of books to borrow. L-hand circles in air
38 Gymnasium /jim-NAY-zee-um/ Students play basketball in the gymnasium. Fingerspell or sign each word
39 Cafeteria /kaf-uh-TEER-ee-uh/ Aiko eats lunch in the school cafeteria. Fingerspell or sign each word
40 Principal /PRIN-suh-pul/ The principal greets every student by name. Fingerspell or sign each word
41 Counselor /KOWN-sel-er/ The school counselor helps students with problems. Fingerspell or sign each word
42 Attendance /uh-TEN-duns/ The teacher takes attendance each morning. Fingerspell or sign each word
43 Absent /AB-sent/ Kenji was absent because he had a cold. Fingerspell ABSENT
44 Present /PREZ-ent/ "Present!" Aiko calls out when her name is called. Fingerspell or sign each word
45 Field trip /FEELD TRIP/ The class goes on a field trip to the museum. Fingerspell or sign each word
