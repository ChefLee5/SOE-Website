# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Letter /LET-er/ I wrote a letter to my grandmother. Fingerspell LETTER
2 Envelope /EN-vuh-lohp/ Put the letter inside the envelope. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Stamp /STAMP/ Buy a stamp to mail the letter. Fingerspell STAMP
4 Address /AD-res/ Write the address on the front of the envelope. A-hands move up chest (like suspenders)
5 Return address /rih-TURN AD-res/ The return address is in the top left corner. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Zip code /ZIP KOHD/ The zip code helps mail get to the right place. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Package /PAK-ij/ The package arrived in a brown cardboard box. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Mail carrier /MAYL KAIR-ee-er/ The mail carrier delivers letters to every house. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Post office /POHST OF-is/ Go to the post office to send a package. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Mailbox /MAYL-boks/ Drop the letter in the blue mailbox. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Postcard /POHST-kard/ Nerissa sends a postcard from every city she visits. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Delivery /dih-LIV-er-ee/ The delivery truck brings packages to the door. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Tracking number /TRAK-ing NUM-ber/ Use the tracking number to find your package online. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Fragile /FRAJ-ul/ Mark the box "Fragile" if it contains glass. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Priority mail /pry-OR-ih-tee MAYL/ Priority mail arrives faster than regular mail. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Express /ek-SPRES/ Express delivery gets the package there by tomorrow. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Scale /SKAYL/ The clerk weighs the package on the scale. Both flat hands balance like scale
18 Certified mail /SUR-tih-fyd MAYL/ Certified mail requires a signature when delivered. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 P.O. Box /PEE-OH BOKS/ Some people receive mail at a P.O. Box. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Sort /SORT/ Workers sort the mail by zip code. Fingerspell SORT
