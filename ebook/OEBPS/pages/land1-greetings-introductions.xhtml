# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Hello /heh-LOH/ Kenji says hello to every person who arrives. Flat hand, salute from forehead outward
2 Goodbye /good-BY/ Aiko waves goodbye as the visitors leave. Open hand, wave fingers down repeatedly
3 Good morning /good MOR-ning/ Good morning! The sun rises over Harmonia. Sign GOOD + MORNING (arm rises like sun)
4 Good afternoon /good af-ter-NOON/ Good afternoon! It's time for lunch. Sign GOOD + AFTERNOON (forearm tilts forward)
5 Good evening /good EEV-ning/ Good evening! The stars are coming out. Sign GOOD + EVENING (hand curves down like setting sun)
6 Good night /good NYT/ Good night! Time to rest and sleep. Sign GOOD + NIGHT (dominant hand drops over non-dominant)
7 Please /PLEEZ/ Please pass the water, Kenji says politely. Flat hand circles on chest
8 Thank you /THANK yoo/ Thank you for sharing, Aiko says with a smile. Flat hand from chin outward
9 You're welcome /yor WEL-kum/ You're welcome! It's nice to help a friend. Flat hand moves from mouth forward and down
10 Excuse me /ek-SKYOOZ mee/ Excuse me, may I sit here? Fingertips brush opposite palm forward twice
11 I'm sorry /eym SOR-ee/ I'm sorry, I didn't mean to bump into you. Fist circles on chest (sign SORRY)
12 Yes /YES/ Yes, I would like to learn a new word today. S-hand nods up and down
13 No /NOH/ No, thank you, I'm not hungry right now. Index and middle finger snap to thumb
14 Name /NAYM/ My name is Kenji. What is your name? Two H-fingers tap crosswise
15 First name /FURST NAYM/ Write your first name on this line. Sign FIRST (index touches thumb-side palm) + NAME
16 Last name /LAST NAYM/ Your last name is your family name. Sign LAST (pinky strikes opposite pinky) + NAME
17 Nice to meet you /NYS too MEET yoo/ Nice to meet you! Welcome to Harmonia. Both flat hands meet in middle
18 How are you? /HOW ar yoo/ How are you today? I am doing well. Curved hands on chest flip outward (HOW) + point
19 Fine /FYN/ I'm fine, thank you for asking. Thumb of 5-hand taps chest
20 Friend /FREND/ Aiko introduces her new friend to the group. Hook index fingers together, then reverse
21 Handshake /HAND-shayk/ A firm handshake shows respect. Clasp hands together, mime shaking
22 Wave /WAYV/ Kenji gives a friendly wave from across the garden. Open hand waves side to side
23 Bow /BOW/ In some cultures, people bow to greet each other. Bend at waist with hands at sides
24 Hug /HUG/ Aiko gives her grandmother a warm hug. Cross arms over chest, squeeze
25 Smile /SMYL/ A smile is a greeting that everyone understands. Index fingers draw smile on cheeks
26 Welcome /WEL-kum/ Welcome to our village! Come inside. Open hand sweeps inward toward body
27 Introduce /in-troh-DOOS/ Let me introduce you to my teacher. Both flat hands brought together from sides
28 Conversation /kon-ver-SAY-shun/ A good conversation has listening AND speaking. Both C-hands move alternately near mouth
29 Polite /poh-LYT/ It is polite to say "please" and "thank you." Thumb of 5-hand taps chest gently
30 Respect /rih-SPEKT/ Respect means treating others the way you want to be treated. R-hand arcs from forehead outward
