Aquaria cycles through all four seasons. Ronan and Nerissa watch nature transform every few months.

A richly detailed scene depicting 'Seasons & Nature Cycles' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Spring /SPRING/ Spring brings warm air and new flowers. Fingerspell SPRING
2 Summer /SUM-er/ Summer is the hottest season with the longest days. Fingerspell SUMMER
3 Fall /FAWL/ In fall, leaves turn red, orange, and gold. Fingerspell FALL
4 Autumn /AW-tum/ Autumn is another word for fall. Fingerspell AUTUMN
5 Winter /WIN-ter/ Winter is cold, and the days are shorter. Fingerspell WINTER
6 Bloom /BLOOM/ Flowers bloom in the warm spring sunshine. Fingerspell BLOOM
7 Harvest /HAR-vist/ The harvest is when farmers collect ripe crops. Curved hand mimes gathering crops
8 Migrate /MY-grayt/ Many birds migrate south for the winter. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Hibernate /HY-ber-nayt/ Bears hibernate through the cold winter months. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Bud /BUD/ A tiny green bud appears before the flower opens. Fingerspell BUD
11 Leaf /LEEF/ A single leaf falls from the tree. Index finger hangs from opposite wrist, sways
12 Branch /BRANCH/ The branch sways in the autumn wind. Fingerspell BRANCH
13 Root /ROOT/ The root anchors the tree deep underground. 5-hand pushes down through opposite C-hand
14 Seed /SEED/ Plant the seed in the soil and water it. Mime sprinkling seeds from pinched fingers
15 Soil /SOYL/ Rich dark soil helps plants grow. Fingerspell SOIL
16 Dew /DOO/ Morning dew sparkles on the grass. Fingerspell DEW
17 Frost /FROST/ Frost covers the windows on cold mornings. Fingerspell FROST
18 Thaw /THAW/ The ice begins to thaw when temperatures rise. Fingerspell THAW
19 Breeze /BREEZ/ A gentle breeze carries the smell of flowers. Fingerspell BREEZE
20 Sunshine /SUN-shyn/ Warm sunshine helps plants grow tall. Fingerspell or sign each word
