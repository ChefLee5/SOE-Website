Scene 3: Transportation

Ronan and Nerissa travel across Aquaria using every kind of vehicle — by land, sea, and air.

A richly detailed scene depicting 'Transportation' with numbered callout markers (1–35) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Car /KAR/ The car drives along the highway. Both C-hands mime steering wheel
2 Truck /TRUK/ The big truck carries goods across the country. Fingerspell TRUCK
3 Bus /BUS/ Take the bus to school and back home. B-hand mimes long vehicle
4 Van /VAN/ The family loads luggage into the van. Fingerspell VAN
5 Taxi /TAK-see/ Hail a taxi to get to the airport quickly. Fingerspell T-A-X-I or sign CAR + YELLOW
6 Motorcycle /MOH-ter-sy-kul/ The motorcycle weaves through city traffic. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Bicycle /BY-sih-kul/ Ronan rides his bicycle along the waterfront path. S-fists pedal in circles
8 Scooter /SKOO-ter/ The electric scooter is quiet and fast. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Train /TRAYN/ The train travels on tracks from city to city. H-hands slide along each other (tracks)
10 Subway /SUB-way/ Take the subway to travel underground across town. Y-hand slides under opposite flat palm
11 Airplane /AIR-playn/ The airplane flies above the clouds. Y-hand with index out, flies forward
12 Helicopter /HEL-ih-kop-ter/ The helicopter can hover in one place. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Boat /BOHT/ Nerissa rows the small boat across the lake. Both cupped hands form hull, bounce forward
14 Ship /SHIP/ The large ship carries passengers across the ocean. Fingerspell SHIP
15 Ferry /FAIR-ee/ The ferry takes cars and people across the bay. F-hands carry across space
16 Sailboat /SAYL-boht/ The sailboat uses wind to move through the water. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Canoe /kuh-NOO/ Ronan paddles the canoe down the calm river. Fingerspell CANOE
18 Kayak /KY-ak/ The kayak glides through the narrow waterway. Fingerspell KAYAK
19 Ambulance /AM-byoo-luns/ The ambulance rushes to the emergency with sirens on. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Fire truck /FYR TRUK/ The red fire truck races to the building. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Police car /puh-LEES KAR/ The police car patrols the neighborhood. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Tow truck /TOH TRUK/ The tow truck takes the broken car to the repair shop. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Crosswalk /KROS-wawk/ Always cross the street at the crosswalk. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Traffic light /TRAF-ik LYT/ Stop on red, go on green at the traffic light. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Stop sign /STOP SYN/ Every car must stop at the stop sign. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Speed limit /SPEED LIM-it/ The speed limit in a school zone is twenty miles per hour. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Parking lot /PAR-king LOT/ Find a space in the parking lot near the store. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Gas station /GAS STAY-shun/ Fill up the car's tank at the gas station. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Highway /HY-way/ The highway has multiple lanes for fast driving. Both hands weave forward (busy road)
30 Intersection /in-ter-SEK-shun/ Two roads meet at the intersection. Fingerspell or sign each word
31 Pedestrian /peh-DES-tree-un/ A pedestrian is a person walking on foot. Fingerspell or sign each word
32 Sidewalk /SYD-wawk/ Walk on the sidewalk, not on the road. Fingerspell or sign each word
33 Bridge /BRIJ/ The bridge crosses over the wide river. V-hand bridges under opposite forearm
34 Tunnel /TUN-ul/ The tunnel goes through the mountain. Fingerspell TUNNEL
35 Route /ROOT/ Which route is the fastest to the airport? Fingerspell ROUTE
