Scene 5: Safety & Emergencies

In the Sophia Safety Center, Ezra and Athena learn the words that can save a life.

🖼️ Illustration: Safety & Emergencies
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Safety & Emergencies' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Emergency /ih-MUR-jun-see/ In an emergency, call 911 immediately. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Danger /DAYN-jer/ The "Danger" sign warns you to stay away. Fingerspell DANGER
3 Caution /KAW-shun/ Use caution when crossing the busy street. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Warning /WOR-ning/ The warning light flashes when there is a problem. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Exit /EK-sit/ Find the nearest exit in case of emergency. Fingerspell EXIT
6 Fire escape /FYR es-KAYP/ Use the fire escape if the stairs are blocked. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Fire extinguisher /FYR ek-STING-gwish-er/ The fire extinguisher puts out small fires. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Smoke detector /SMOHK dih-TEK-ter/ The smoke detector beeps loudly when it senses smoke. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 First aid /FURST AYD/ First aid is immediate help before the doctor arrives. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 CPR /SEE-PEE-AR/ CPR can save a life by keeping blood flowing. Fingerspell CPR
11 Poison /POY-zun/ Keep poison and chemicals away from children. Fingerspell POISON
12 Hazard /HAZ-erd/ A wet floor is a slipping hazard. Fingerspell HAZARD
13 Evacuate /ih-VAK-yoo-ayt/ Evacuate the building calmly and quickly. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Rescue /RES-kyoo/ Firefighters rescue people from burning buildings. Fingerspell RESCUE
15 Safe /SAYF/ Stay in a safe place during a storm. S-hands cross at wrists, then pull apart
16 Helmet /HEL-met/ Wear a helmet when riding a bicycle. Both C-hands come down on head
17 Seat belt /SEET BELT/ Always buckle your seat belt in the car. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Life jacket /LYF JAK-it/ Wear a life jacket when you are on a boat. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Crosswalk /KROS-wawk/ Use the crosswalk to cross the street safely. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Stop, drop, and roll /STOP DROP and ROHL/ If your clothes catch fire: stop, drop, and roll. Fingerspell or sign each word

Ezra's Tip:

"Safety words can SAVE YOUR LIFE. Memorize your address and phone number in English. Practice: 'My address is ___. My phone number is ___. I need HELP.' You are always safer when you can communicate."
