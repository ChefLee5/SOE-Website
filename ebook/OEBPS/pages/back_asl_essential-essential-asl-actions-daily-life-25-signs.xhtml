# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Eat /EET/ Time to eat breakfast. Flat-O hand taps mouth
2 Drink /DRINK/ Drink some water. C-hand tilts to mouth
3 Sleep /SLEEP/ I need to sleep. 5-hand draws down face, closing
4 Go /GOH/ Let's go to school. Both index fingers flick forward
5 Come /KUM/ Come here, please. Both index fingers beckon toward body
6 Want /WONT/ I want to learn. Both claw hands pull toward body
7 Need /NEED/ I need help. X-hand bends down firmly
8 Like /LYK/ I like this book. Thumb and middle pull from chest
9 Know /NOH/ I know the answer. Flat hand taps side of forehead
10 Learn /LURN/ I want to learn ASL. Flat hand from palm to forehead (absorbing)
11 Work /WURK/ Time to work hard. S-fist taps on opposite S-fist
12 Play /PLAY/ Let's play a game. Y-hands shake in front of body
13 Read /REED/ Read the book carefully. V-fingers scan down opposite palm
14 Write /RYT/ Write your name here. Mime writing on opposite palm
15 Walk /WAWK/ Walk to school every day. Both flat hands walk forward
16 Run /RUN/ Run fast to the finish line. L-hands pull forward quickly
17 Sit /SIT/ Please sit down. Bent V sits on opposite bent V
18 Stand /STAND/ Stand up straight. V-fingers stand on opposite palm
19 Open /OH-pen/ Open the door. B-hands together, swing apart
20 Close /KLOHS/ Close the window. B-hands apart, swing together
21 Give /GIV/ Give me the book. Flat-O hands push forward, opening
22 Take /TAYK/ Take this with you. 5-hand grabs, pulls to S-fist
23 Look /LOOK/ Look at the picture. V-hand from eyes points outward
24 Listen /LIS-en/ Listen carefully to the teacher. Cup hand at ear
25 Think /THINK/ Think about it first. Index finger circles at temple
