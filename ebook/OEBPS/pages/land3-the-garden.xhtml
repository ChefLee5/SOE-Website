Scene 4: The Garden

Behind Silas and Vesta's cottage, a beautiful garden grows with vegetables, herbs, and flowers year-round.

# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Soil /SOYL/ Silas digs his hands into the dark, rich soil. Fingerspell SOIL
2 Rake /RAYK/ Vesta uses the rake to smooth the dirt. Fingerspell RAKE
3 Hoe /HOH/ The hoe breaks up hard ground for planting. Fingerspell HOE
4 Shovel /SHUV-ul/ Silas digs a hole with the shovel. Fingerspell SHOVEL
5 Watering can /WAW-ter-ing KAN/ Vesta pours water from the watering can onto the seedlings. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Hose /HOHZ/ The long green hose reaches every corner of the garden. Fingerspell HOSE
7 Compost /KOM-pohst/ Old leaves and food scraps become rich compost. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Wheelbarrow /WEEL-bair-oh/ Silas pushes the wheelbarrow full of soil. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Seed /SEED/ Vesta plants a tiny seed and covers it with soil. Mime sprinkling seeds from pinched fingers
10 Seedling /SEED-ling/ A green seedling pushes up through the dirt. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Sprout /SPROWT/ The first sprout appears after five days of rain. Fingerspell SPROUT
12 Root /ROOT/ The root grows down deep into the earth. 5-hand pushes down through opposite C-hand
13 Stem /STEM/ Water travels up through the stem to the leaves. Fingerspell STEM
14 Leaf /LEEF/ Each green leaf catches sunlight for the plant. Index finger hangs from opposite wrist, sways
15 Bud /BUD/ A tiny bud will open into a beautiful flower. Fingerspell BUD
16 Bloom /BLOOM/ The roses bloom in the warm summer months. Fingerspell BLOOM
17 Petal /PET-ul/ Each petal is soft like silk. Fingerspell PETAL
18 Thorn /THORN/ Be careful of the sharp thorn on the rose bush. Fingerspell THORN
19 Vine /VYN/ The vine climbs up the old stone wall. Fingerspell VINE
20 Weed /WEED/ Silas pulls up every weed so the vegetables can grow. Fingerspell WEED
21 Mulch /MULCH/ Vesta spreads mulch around the plants to keep the soil moist. Fingerspell MULCH
22 Fertilizer /FUR-tuh-ly-zer/ Fertilizer helps the plants grow strong and tall. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Garden bed /GAR-den BED/ Each garden bed is planted with a different vegetable. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Row /ROH/ The carrots grow in a straight row. Fingerspell ROW
25 Trellis /TREL-is/ The bean plants climb up the wooden trellis. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Pot /POT/ Herbs grow in a clay pot on the windowsill. P-hands mime stirring in pot
27 Planter /PLAN-ter/ The large planter is full of colorful flowers. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Greenhouse /GREEN-hows/ The greenhouse keeps plants warm during the winter. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Pruning shears /PROO-ning SHEERZ/ Vesta trims the bushes with her pruning shears. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Gloves /GLUVZ/ Silas wears thick gloves to protect his hands. One hand slides over other like putting on glove
31 Bucket /BUK-it/ Vesta fills a bucket with fresh vegetables from the garden. Fingerspell BUCKET
32 Sunflower /SUN-flow-er/ The tall sunflower turns its face toward the sun. Fingerspell or sign each word
33 Rose /ROHZ/ A red rose grows beside the garden gate. Fingerspell ROSE
34 Tulip /TOO-lip/ Purple tulips bloom in the spring. Fingerspell TULIP
35 Daisy /DAY-zee/ Vesta picks a white daisy and puts it in her hair. Fingerspell DAISY
