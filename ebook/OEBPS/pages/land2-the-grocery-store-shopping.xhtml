# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Store /STOR/ The store opens at eight in the morning. Both flat O-hands at chest flick outward (SELL)
2 Supermarket /SOO-per-mar-kit/ The supermarket has aisles of food and supplies. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Aisle /EYL/ The cereal is in aisle three. Fingerspell AISLE
4 Cart /KART/ Push the shopping cart down the aisle. Fingerspell CART
5 Basket /BAS-kit/ Use a basket if you only need a few things. Fingerspell BASKET
6 Cashier /ka-SHEER/ The cashier scans each item at the register. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Register /REJ-ih-ster/ Pay at the register when you are finished shopping. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Receipt /rih-SEET/ The receipt lists every item you purchased. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Coupon /KOO-pon/ Use the coupon to get one dollar off. Fingerspell COUPON
10 Shopping list /SHOP-ing LIST/ Write a shopping list before you go to the store. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Bag /BAG/ The cashier puts the groceries in a bag. Fingerspell BAG
12 Brand /BRAND/ Choose the store brand to save money. Fingerspell BRAND
13 Label /LAY-bul/ Read the label to check the ingredients. Fingerspell LABEL
14 Expiration date /ek-spuh-RAY-shun DAYT/ The expiration date tells you when food is no longer safe. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Organic /or-GAN-ik/ Organic food is grown without chemicals. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Frozen /FROH-zen/ Frozen vegetables are in the freezer section. Fingerspell FROZEN
17 Dairy /DAIR-ee/ Milk, cheese, and yogurt are in the dairy section. Fingerspell DAIRY
18 Bakery /BAY-ker-ee/ Fresh bread comes from the supermarket bakery. Fingerspell BAKERY
19 Deli /DEL-ee/ Order sliced turkey at the deli counter. Fingerspell DELI
20 Produce /PROH-doos/ Fresh fruits and vegetables are in the produce section. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Checkout /CHEK-owt/ Go to the checkout line when you're ready to pay. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Self-checkout /SELF-CHEK-owt/ The self-checkout lets you scan items yourself. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Return /rih-TURN/ Return an item if it is damaged or wrong. Fingerspell RETURN
24 Exchange /eks-CHAYNJ/ Exchange the shirt for a different size. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Refund /REE-fund/ Ask for a refund if the product doesn't work. Fingerspell REFUND
26 Sale /SAYL/ The sale sign says buy one, get one free. Fingerspell SALE
27 Price tag /PRYS TAG/ Check the price tag before putting it in the cart. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Customer /KUS-tuh-mer/ The customer asks the cashier a question. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Employee /em-PLOY-ee/ The store employee stocks the shelves. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Manager /MAN-uh-jer/ Ask for the manager if you have a complaint. Fingerspell or sign each word
