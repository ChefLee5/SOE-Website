The people in your life — learn to sign them all.

# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Mother /MUTH-er/ My mother takes care of me. 5-hand, thumb taps chin
2 Father /FAH-ther/ My father works hard. 5-hand, thumb taps forehead
3 Sister /SIS-ter/ My sister is my best friend. A-hand at chin drops to L-hand
4 Brother /BRUTH-er/ My brother helps me study. A-hand at forehead drops to L-hand
5 Baby /BAY-bee/ The baby is sleeping. Rock arms like cradling
6 Family /FAM-uh-lee/ Family is everything. F-hands circle outward from center
7 Boy /BOY/ The boy plays in the park. Flat hand snaps at forehead
8 Girl /GURL/ The girl reads her book. Thumb traces down jawline
9 Man /MAN/ The man wears a hat. 5-hand forehead to chest center
10 Woman /WOO-mun/ The woman drives the bus. 5-hand chin to chest center
11 Teacher /TEECH-er/ The teacher helps us learn. Flat-O hands at temples move out + PERSON
12 Student /STOO-dent/ Every student works hard. Flat hand from opposite palm to forehead
13 Doctor /DOK-ter/ The doctor checks my health. M or D-hand taps opposite wrist
14 Police /poh-LEES/ The police keep us safe. C-hand taps left chest like badge
15 Neighbor /NAY-ber/ My neighbor waves hello. Flat hand near other flat hand, both move
16 Person /PUR-sun/ Every person is important. P-hands drop down sides of body
17 Children /CHIL-dren/ Children love to play. Flat hands pat down repeatedly (heights)
18 Grandma /GRAN-mah/ Grandma tells great stories. 5 at chin, arc forward twice
19 Grandpa /GRAN-pah/ Grandpa has wise advice. 5 at forehead, arc forward twice
20 Husband /HUZ-band/ Her husband cooks dinner. Clasp hands (MARRY), sign at forehead
21 Wife /WYF/ His wife is a teacher. Clasp hands (MARRY), sign at chin
22 Friend /FREND/ Friends help each other. Linked index fingers swap
23 People /PEE-pul/ All people deserve respect. P-hands circle alternately in front
24 Deaf /DEF/ Deaf culture is rich and beautiful. Index touches ear, then chin (or reverse)
25 Hearing /HEER-ing/ Hearing and Deaf people communicate together. Index circles away from mouth
