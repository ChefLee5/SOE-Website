# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Earth /URTH/ Earth is our home planet. Fingerspell EARTH
2 Core /KOR/ The core is the hot center of the Earth. Fingerspell CORE
3 Crust /KRUST/ The crust is the thin outer layer we walk on. Fingerspell CRUST
4 Mantle /MAN-tul/ The mantle is the thick layer between the crust and the core. Fingerspell MANTLE
5 Tectonic plate /tek-TON-ik PLAYT/ Tectonic plates are huge slabs of rock that move slowly. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Continent /KON-tih-nent/ There are seven continents on the Earth. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Ocean /OH-shun/ Oceans cover about 71 percent of the Earth's surface. Both hands make waves, push forward
8 Mountain /MOWN-tun/ Mount Everest is the tallest mountain on Earth. Flat hand taps fist (ROCK) + both hands peak up
9 Volcano /vol-KAY-noh/ A volcano erupts when hot lava bursts through the crust. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Earthquake /URTH-kwayk/ An earthquake happens when tectonic plates shift. S-hands grab air and shake
11 Lava /LAH-vuh/ Hot lava flows from the opening of the volcano. Fingerspell LAVA
12 Magma /MAG-muh/ Magma is melted rock beneath the Earth's surface. Fingerspell MAGMA
13 Fossil /FOS-ul/ A fossil is the preserved remains of an ancient plant or animal. Fingerspell FOSSIL
14 Mineral /MIN-er-ul/ A mineral is a natural solid found in rock. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Rock /ROK/ There are three types of rock: igneous, sedimentary, and metamorphic. Fingerspell ROCK
16 Erosion /ih-ROH-zhun/ Erosion wears away land by water, wind, or ice. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Soil /SOYL/ Healthy soil has nutrients for plants to grow. Fingerspell SOIL
18 Natural resource /NACH-er-ul REE-sors/ Water, air, and sunlight are natural resources. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Renewable /rih-NOO-uh-bul/ Solar and wind energy are renewable resources. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Nonrenewable /non-rih-NOO-uh-bul/ Coal and oil are nonrenewable resources that can run out. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Pollution /puh-LOO-shun/ Pollution harms the air, water, and land. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Recycle /ree-SY-kul/ Recycle paper, plastic, and glass to reduce waste. R-hand circles (going around again)
23 Conservation /kon-ser-VAY-shun/ Conservation means using resources wisely to protect nature. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Ecosystem /EE-koh-sis-tem/ An ecosystem is a community of living things and their environment. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Habitat /HAB-ih-tat/ The forest is a natural habitat for many animals. Fingerspell or sign each word
