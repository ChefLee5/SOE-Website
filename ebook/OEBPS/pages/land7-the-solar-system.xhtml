# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Sun /SUN/ The sun is the star at the center of our solar system. C-hand above, opens to 5-hand (radiating)
2 Moon /MOON/ The moon orbits the Earth once every 27 days. Modified C at temple, arcs up
3 Star /STAR/ A star is a ball of burning gas far away in space. Both index fingers brush up alternately
4 Planet /PLAN-it/ Earth is the third planet from the sun. Fingerspell PLANET
5 Mercury /MUR-kyoo-ree/ Mercury is the closest planet to the sun. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Venus /VEE-nus/ Venus is the hottest planet because of its thick atmosphere. Fingerspell VENUS
7 Earth /URTH/ Earth is the only planet known to support life. Fingerspell EARTH
8 Mars /MARZ/ Mars is called the Red Planet. Fingerspell MARS
9 Jupiter /JOO-pih-ter/ Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Saturn /SAT-urn/ Saturn has beautiful rings made of ice and rock. Fingerspell SATURN
11 Uranus /YOOR-uh-nus/ Uranus rotates on its side, unlike other planets. Fingerspell URANUS
12 Neptune /NEP-toon/ Neptune is the farthest planet from the sun. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Orbit /OR-bit/ The Earth takes one year to orbit the sun. Fingerspell ORBIT
14 Rotate /ROH-tayt/ The Earth rotates once every twenty-four hours. Fingerspell ROTATE
15 Galaxy /GAL-uk-see/ Our sun is one of billions of stars in the Milky Way galaxy. Fingerspell GALAXY
16 Constellation /kon-steh-LAY-shun/ A constellation is a group of stars forming a pattern. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Comet /KOM-it/ A comet leaves a glowing tail as it flies past the sun. Fingerspell COMET
18 Asteroid /AS-ter-oyd/ An asteroid is a rocky object that orbits the sun. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Meteor /MEE-tee-or/ A meteor streaks across the night sky like a shooting star. Fingerspell METEOR
20 Telescope /TEL-ih-skohp/ Selene looks at the moon through the telescope. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Astronaut /AS-troh-nawt/ An astronaut travels to space in a rocket. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Rocket /ROK-it/ The rocket launches from the pad with a roar. Fingerspell ROCKET
23 Space station /SPAYS STAY-shun/ Astronauts live and work on the space station. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Gravity /GRAV-ih-tee/ Gravity pulls everything toward the ground. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Lunar /LOO-ner/ A lunar eclipse happens when Earth blocks the sun's light on the moon. Fingerspell LUNAR
26 Solar /SOH-ler/ A solar eclipse happens when the moon blocks the sun. Fingerspell SOLAR
27 Satellite /SAT-eh-lyt/ A satellite orbits Earth and sends information to us. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Light-year /LYT-yeer/ A light-year is the distance light travels in one year. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Universe /YOO-nih-vers/ The universe contains everything that exists. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Atmosphere /AT-muh-sfeer/ Earth's atmosphere protects us from the sun's harmful rays. Fingerspell or sign each word
