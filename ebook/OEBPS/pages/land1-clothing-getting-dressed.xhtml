Scene 6: Clothing & Getting Dressed

Every morning, Kenji and Aiko get dressed in their Harmonia outfits. Every piece of clothing has a name.

🖼️ Illustration: Clothing & Getting Dressed
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Clothing & Getting Dressed' with numbered callout markers (1–40) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Shirt /SHURT/ Kenji puts on a clean white shirt. Pinch and pull shirt fabric at chest
2 T-shirt /TEE-shurt/ Aiko wears a purple T-shirt on casual days. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Blouse /BLOWZ/ The blouse has small buttons down the front. Fingerspell BLOUSE
4 Pants /PANTS/ Kenji pulls on a pair of dark blue pants. Both hands pat thighs, then pull up at waist
5 Jeans /JEENZ/ Aiko's jeans are comfortable for running and playing. Fingerspell JEANS
6 Shorts /SHORTS/ In summer, Kenji wears shorts to stay cool. Bent hand pushes down (repeated)
7 Skirt /SKURT/ Aiko twirls in her colorful skirt. 5-hands brush down from waist
8 Dress /DRES/ The flowered dress is for special occasions. 5-hands brush down chest (showing garment)
9 Sweater /SWET-er/ A warm sweater keeps you cozy on chilly days. Claw hands mime pulling sweater on
10 Jacket /JAK-it/ Kenji zips up his jacket before going outside. Sign COAT (same motion)
11 Coat /KOHT/ The long winter coat keeps out the cold wind. A-hands trace lapels to shoulders
12 Raincoat /RAYN-koht/ Aiko grabs her yellow raincoat when it's raining. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Hoodie /HOO-dee/ Kenji pulls the hoodie over his head. Fingerspell HOODIE
14 Vest /VEST/ The vest adds a layer without long sleeves. Fingerspell VEST
15 Suit /SOOT/ Father wears a suit to important meetings. Fingerspell SUIT
16 Tie /TY/ The colorful tie goes around the collar. Mime tying a necktie
17 Uniform /YOO-nih-form/ Students at Harmonia School wear a uniform. U-hands brush down chest
18 Underwear /UN-der-wair/ Put on clean underwear first thing every morning. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Socks /SOKS/ The socks keep your feet warm inside your shoes. Index fingers rub together pointing down
20 Shoes /SHOOZ/ Tie your shoes before you go outside. Both S-fists tap together sideways
21 Sneakers /SNEE-kerz/ Aiko runs fastest in her new sneakers. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Boots /BOOTS/ Rain boots protect your feet from puddles. B-hands stamp alternately
23 Sandals /SAN-dulz/ Sandals are open shoes for warm weather. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Slippers /SLIP-erz/ Wear slippers inside the house. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Hat /HAT/ Kenji wears a hat to shade his face from the sun. Pat top of head
26 Cap /KAP/ The baseball cap goes forwards or backwards. Fingerspell CAP
27 Scarf /SKARF/ A scarf wraps around the neck to stay warm. Mime wrapping scarf around neck
28 Gloves /GLUVZ/ Warm gloves protect your fingers from the cold. One hand slides over other like putting on glove
29 Belt /BELT/ The belt keeps Kenji's pants from falling down. Both hands trace belt around waist
30 Button /BUT-un/ Button your shirt from the bottom up. F-hand taps down chest in spots
31 Zipper /ZIP-er/ Zip the zipper all the way up on cold days. Mime pulling zipper up chest
32 Pocket /POK-it/ Kenji keeps his key in his front pocket. Fingerspell POCKET
33 Sleeve /SLEEV/ Roll up your sleeves when you wash dishes. Fingerspell SLEEVE
34 Collar /KOL-er/ The collar of the shirt goes around the neck. Fingerspell COLLAR
35 Laundry /LAWN-dree/ Put your dirty clothing in the laundry basket. Fingerspell or sign each word
36 Iron /EYE-urn/ Iron the wrinkled shirt until it's smooth. Fingerspell IRON
37 Fold /FOHLD/ Fold the clean clothes and put them away. Fingerspell FOLD
38 Hanger /HANG-er/ Hang the jacket on a hanger in the closet. Fingerspell HANGER
39 Size /SYZ/ What size shirt do you wear? Small, medium, or large? Fingerspell SIZE
40 Try on /TRY ON/ Try on the shoes to see if they fit. Fingerspell TRY ON

Aiko's Tip:
