# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Barn /BARN/ Silas opens the big red barn door each morning. Fingerspell BARN
2 Tractor /TRAK-ter/ Vesta drives the tractor across the green field. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Hay /HAY/ The hay is golden and soft for the animals to sleep on. Fingerspell HAY
4 Silo /SY-loh/ The tall silo stores grain for the winter months. Fingerspell SILO
5 Fence /FENS/ A wooden fence keeps the animals safe inside the farm. Fingerspell FENCE
6 Rooster /ROO-ster/ The rooster crows loudly when the sun comes up. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Cow /KOW/ The brown cow gives fresh milk every day. Y-hand thumb at temple, twist (horn)
8 Calf /KAF/ The little calf stays close to its mother. Fingerspell CALF
9 Goat /GOHT/ The goat climbs on rocks and eats everything! Fingerspell GOAT
10 Pig /PIG/ The pig rolls happily in the cool mud. Flat hand flaps under chin
11 Piglet /PIG-let/ Three tiny piglets follow the mother pig. Fingerspell PIGLET
12 Horse /HORS/ Silas rides the strong horse through the meadow. Thumb at temple, two fingers flap (ear)
13 Pony /POH-nee/ Vesta brushes the pony's soft mane. Fingerspell PONY
14 Donkey /DONG-kee/ The donkey carries heavy baskets up the hill. Fingerspell DONKEY
15 Sheep /SHEEP/ The fluffy sheep graze on the hillside. V-fingers on opposite forearm, snip up (shearing)
16 Lamb /LAM/ The lamb has soft white wool. Fingerspell LAMB
17 Hen /HEN/ The hen sits on her nest of eggs. Fingerspell HEN
18 Chick /CHIK/ The yellow chick follows the hen everywhere. Fingerspell CHICK
19 Duck /DUK/ The duck waddles to the pond for a swim. Hand at mouth opens/closes (quacking)
20 Duckling /DUK-ling/ Five ducklings swim behind their mother. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Turkey /TUR-kee/ The turkey spreads its colorful tail feathers. Fingerspell TURKEY
22 Goose /GOOS/ The white goose honks loudly at visitors. Fingerspell GOOSE
23 Stable /STAY-bul/ The horses sleep inside the warm stable at night. Fingerspell STABLE
24 Pen /PEN/ The pigs live in a pen behind the barn. Pinch dominant hand, write on opposite palm
25 Coop /KOOP/ The chickens go inside the coop when it gets dark. Fingerspell COOP
26 Trough /TRAWF/ Silas fills the trough with water for the animals. Fingerspell TROUGH
27 Feed /FEED/ Vesta carries a bucket of feed to the chickens. Fingerspell FEED
28 Saddle /SAD-ul/ Silas puts the leather saddle on the horse. Fingerspell SADDLE
29 Bridle /BRY-dul/ The bridle helps Silas steer the horse gently. Fingerspell BRIDLE
30 Pasture /PAS-chur/ The cows walk slowly through the green pasture. Fingerspell or sign each word
31 Corral /kuh-RAL/ The rancher brings the horses into the corral. Fingerspell CORRAL
32 Hoof /HOOF/ Vesta checks the horse's hoof for stones. Fingerspell HOOF
33 Mane /MAYN/ The horse's mane blows in the wind. Fingerspell MANE
34 Tail /TAYL/ The cow swishes its tail to keep flies away. Fingerspell TAIL
35 Wool /WOOL/ The sheep's wool is sheared in the spring. Fingerspell WOOL
36 Egg /EG/ The hen laid a brown egg in the nest. H-fingers crack apart
37 Milk /MILK/ Fresh milk comes from the cows on Silas's farm. C-hand squeezes (milking motion)
38 Hay bale /HAY BAYL/ Vesta stacks the hay bales inside the barn. Fingerspell or sign each word
39 Scarecrow /SKAIR-kroh/ A funny scarecrow stands in the middle of the field. Fingerspell or sign each word
40 Windmill /WIND-mil/ The old windmill turns slowly in the breeze. Fingerspell or sign each word
