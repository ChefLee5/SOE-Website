# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Bank /BANGK/ A bank is a safe place to keep your money. B-hand taps opposite palm repeatedly (stacking money)
2 Account /uh-KOWNT/ Open an account at the bank to save money. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Savings account /SAY-vingz uh-KOWNT/ A savings account helps your money grow. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Checking account /CHEK-ing uh-KOWNT/ Use a checking account to pay bills. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Deposit /dih-POZ-it/ Deposit your paycheck into your bank account. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Withdraw /with-DRAW/ Withdraw cash from the ATM when you need it. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 ATM /AY-TEE-EM/ The ATM machine gives cash 24 hours a day. Fingerspell ATM
8 Balance /BAL-uns/ Your balance is how much money is in your account. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Interest /IN-ter-est/ Interest is money the bank pays you for saving. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Loan /LOHN/ A loan is money you borrow and pay back over time. Fingerspell LOAN
11 Payment /PAY-ment/ Make your car payment on time each month. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Transfer /TRANS-fur/ Transfer money from savings to checking online. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Statement /STAYT-ment/ The bank statement shows all your transactions. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 PIN /PIN/ Enter your four-digit PIN to access your account. Fingerspell PIN
15 Direct deposit /dih-REKT dih-POZ-it/ Direct deposit puts your paycheck in the bank automatically. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Wire transfer /WYR TRANS-fur/ A wire transfer sends money to another bank quickly. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Online banking /on-LYN BANG-king/ Online banking lets you manage money from home. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Teller /TEL-er/ The bank teller helps customers at the counter. Fingerspell TELLER
19 Safe /SAYF/ The bank safe protects valuable items. S-hands cross at wrists, then pull apart
20 Mortgage /MOR-gij/ A mortgage is a loan to buy a house. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Overdraft /OH-ver-draft/ An overdraft happens when you spend more than your balance. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Routing number /ROO-ting NUM-ber/ The routing number identifies your bank. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Credit score /KRED-it SKOR/ A good credit score helps you get loans. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Fraud /FRAWD/ Report fraud if someone steals your account information. Fingerspell FRAUD
25 Financial aid /fy-NAN-shul AYD/ Apply for financial aid to help pay for school. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Investment account /in-VEST-ment uh-KOWNT/ An investment account helps your money grow over time. Fingerspell or sign each word

Octavia's Tip:

"Knowing banking words protects you! Practice: 'I want to DEPOSIT money. I want to WITHDRAW money. What is my BALANCE?' These three sentences give you power at any bank."
