Scene 18: Celebrations & Traditions

In Harmonia, every culture brings its own special celebrations. Kenji and Aiko celebrate them all!

# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Holiday /HOL-ih-day/ Our favorite holiday is Thanksgiving. Thumbs at armpits, wiggle fingers (VACATION)
2 Tradition /truh-DISH-un/ A tradition is something families do year after year. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Celebration /sel-eh-BRAY-shun/ The celebration includes food, music, and dancing. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Festival /FES-tih-vul/ The community festival has games and live music. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Parade /puh-RAYD/ Marching bands walk in the parade down Main Street. Fingerspell PARADE
6 Fireworks /FYR-wurks/ Colorful fireworks light up the sky at midnight. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Candle /KAN-dul/ Light a candle on the birthday cake. Fingerspell CANDLE
8 Decoration /dek-oh-RAY-shun/ Hang the decoration on the wall for the party. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Costume /KOS-toom/ Children wear a costume on Halloween. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Gift /GIFT/ A gift is something you give to show you care. Fingerspell GIFT
11 Wrap /RAP/ Wrap the gift in colorful paper. Fingerspell WRAP
12 Card /KARD/ Write a birthday card for your friend. Fingerspell CARD
13 Feast /FEEST/ The family prepared a feast for the holiday meal. Fingerspell FEAST
14 Toast /TOHST/ Raise your glass and make a toast to the new year. Fingerspell TOAST
15 Reunion /ree-YOON-yun/ The family reunion brings everyone together. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Ceremony /SAIR-eh-moh-nee/ A wedding ceremony celebrates love. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Custom /KUS-tum/ Every country has its own customs and practices. Fingerspell CUSTOM
18 Heritage /HAIR-ih-tij/ Share your cultural heritage with your classmates. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Feast day /FEEST DAY/ A feast day is a special meal for a holiday. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Ancestor /AN-ses-ter/ An ancestor is a family member from long ago. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Gathering /GATH-er-ing/ A gathering is when people come together. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Potluck /POT-luk/ Everyone brings a dish to the potluck dinner. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Invitation /in-vih-TAY-shun/ Send an invitation to your friends for the party. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Guest /GEST/ A guest is someone you invite to your home. Fingerspell GUEST
25 Host /HOHST/ The host welcomes everyone to the party. Fingerspell HOST
