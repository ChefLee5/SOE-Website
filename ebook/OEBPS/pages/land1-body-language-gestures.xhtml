# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Nod /NOD/ Nod your head up and down to mean "yes." Fingerspell NOD
2 Shake head /SHAYK HED/ Shake your head side to side to mean "no." Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Point /POYNT/ Point to the picture you want to talk about. Fingerspell POINT
4 Shrug /SHRUG/ Shrug your shoulders to say "I don't know." Fingerspell SHRUG
5 Thumbs up /THUMZ UP/ A thumbs up means "good job" or "yes." Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Thumbs down /THUMZ DOWN/ A thumbs down means "no" or "I don't like it." Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Clap /KLAP/ Clap your hands to show you enjoyed the show. Fingerspell CLAP
8 Gesture /JES-chur/ A gesture is a movement that communicates meaning. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Eye contact /EYE KON-takt/ Make eye contact when someone is talking to you. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Facial expression /FAY-shul ek-SPRESH-un/ A smile is a facial expression that means "I'm happy." Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Frown /FROWN/ A frown shows you are unhappy or worried. Fingerspell FROWN
12 Wink /WINK/ A friendly wink means "I'm joking with you." Fingerspell WINK
13 Yawn /YAWN/ If you yawn, cover your mouth with your hand. Fingerspell YAWN
14 Stretch /STRECH/ Stretch your arms after sitting for a long time. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Cross arms /KROS ARMZ/ Crossing your arms can mean you are cold or upset. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 High five /HY FYV/ Give your teammate a high five after a great play. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Fist bump /FIST BUMP/ A fist bump is a friendly greeting between friends. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Shake hands /SHAYK HANDZ/ Shake hands firmly when meeting someone new. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Bow /BOW/ In many cultures, a bow is a sign of respect. Bend at waist with hands at sides
20 Tone of voice /TOHN ov VOYS/ Your tone of voice tells people how you feel. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Whisper /WIS-per/ Whisper quietly in the library. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Shout /SHOWT/ Shout for help if you are in danger. Fingerspell SHOUT
23 Body language /BOD-ee LANG-gwij/ Body language speaks louder than words sometimes. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Personal space /PUR-sun-ul SPAYS/ Respect other people's personal space. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Posture /POS-chur/ Good posture means sitting and standing up straight. Fingerspell or sign each word
