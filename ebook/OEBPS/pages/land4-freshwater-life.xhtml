# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Freshwater /FRESH-waw-ter/ Lakes and rivers are freshwater — not salty. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Trout /TROWT/ The rainbow trout swims in the cold mountain stream. Fingerspell TROUT
3 Bass /BAS/ The largemouth bass hides near the lily pads. Fingerspell BASS
4 Catfish /KAT-fish/ The catfish has long whiskers near its mouth. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Salmon /SAM-un/ Salmon swim upstream to lay their eggs. Fingerspell SALMON
6 Tadpole /TAD-pohl/ A tadpole will grow into a frog. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Crayfish /KRAY-fish/ The crayfish looks like a tiny lobster. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Pond weed /POND WEED/ Pond weed gives shelter to small fish. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Lily pad /LIL-ee PAD/ The frog sits on a wide green lily pad. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Cattail /KAT-tayl/ Tall cattails grow at the edge of the marsh. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Dragonfly /DRAG-un-fly/ A blue dragonfly darts over the water. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Heron /HAIR-un/ The great blue heron stands still in the shallows. Fingerspell HERON
13 Beaver dam /BEE-ver DAM/ The beaver dam blocks the stream and makes a pond. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Minnow /MIN-oh/ Tiny minnows swim in a school near the bank. Fingerspell MINNOW
15 Algae /AL-jee/ Green algae grows on rocks in the sunlight. Fingerspell ALGAE
16 Fishing rod /FISH-ing ROD/ Ronan casts the fishing rod into the lake. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Bait /BAYT/ Use a worm as bait to catch fish. Fingerspell BAIT
18 Hook /HOOK/ The hook holds the bait for the fish. Fingerspell HOOK
19 Net /NET/ Nerissa scoops up minnows with a small net. Fingerspell NET
20 Dock /DOK/ The wooden dock stretches out over the lake. Fingerspell DOCK
