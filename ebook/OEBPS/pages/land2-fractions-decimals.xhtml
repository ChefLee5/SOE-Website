# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Fraction /FRAK-shun/ A fraction shows a part of a whole. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Numerator /NOO-mer-ay-ter/ The numerator is the top number in a fraction. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Denominator /dih-NOM-ih-nay-ter/ The denominator is the bottom number in a fraction. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 One half /WUN HAF/ One half means one of two equal parts. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 One third /WUN THURD/ One third of a pie is a small slice. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 One quarter /WUN KWOR-ter/ One quarter is the same as one fourth. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Three quarters /THREE KWOR-terz/ Three quarters of the tank is almost full. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Whole /HOHL/ A whole means all the parts together. Fingerspell WHOLE
9 Part /PART/ Each part of the pie is the same size. Fingerspell PART
10 Equal parts /EE-kwul PARTS/ Divide the chocolate bar into equal parts. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Decimal /DES-ih-mul/ A decimal uses a dot to show values less than one. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Decimal point /DES-ih-mul POYNT/ Put the decimal point between dollars and cents. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Tenth /TENTH/ 0.1 is one tenth. Fingerspell TENTH
14 Hundredth /HUN-dredth/ 0.01 is one hundredth. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Percent /per-SENT/ Fifty percent means half of something. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Percentage /per-SEN-tij/ The percentage of students who passed is ninety. Draw percent sign in air (O / O)
17 Equivalent /ih-KWIV-uh-lent/ One half and two quarters are equivalent fractions. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Mixed number /MIKSD NUM-ber/ Two and a half is a mixed number. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Improper fraction /im-PROP-er FRAK-shun/ Five thirds is an improper fraction. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Reduce /rih-DOOS/ Reduce two fourths to one half. Fingerspell REDUCE
21 Simplify /SIM-plih-fy/ Simplify the fraction to its smallest form. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Convert /kun-VERT/ Convert the fraction to a decimal. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Ratio /RAY-shee-oh/ The ratio of boys to girls is three to two. Fingerspell RATIO
24 Proportion /proh-POR-shun/ A proportion shows two equal ratios. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Pie /PY/ Cut the pie into eight equal slices. Fingerspell PIE

Kwame's Tip:

"Fractions are REAL LIFE! When you share a pizza equally with a friend, each person gets ONE HALF. That's 1/2. Now share with three friends — each gets ONE QUARTER. That's 1/4!"
