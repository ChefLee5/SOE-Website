# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Pharmacy /FAR-muh-see/ The pharmacy is where you pick up prescribed medicine. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Pharmacist /FAR-muh-sist/ The pharmacist explains how to take the medicine safely. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Prescription /prih-SKRIP-shun/ Felix hands the prescription to the pharmacist. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Medicine /MED-ih-sin/ Take your medicine at the same time every day. Middle finger rocks on opposite palm
5 Pill /PIL/ Felix swallows the small pill with a glass of water. Mime popping pill into mouth with thumb
6 Tablet /TAB-let/ The chewable tablet tastes like grape. Fingerspell TABLET
7 Capsule /KAP-sul/ The capsule has powder inside a smooth shell. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Liquid medicine /LIK-wid MED-ih-sin/ Liquid medicine is measured with a tiny cup. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Dosage /DOH-sij/ The label tells you the correct dosage for your age. Fingerspell DOSAGE
10 Side effect /SYD ih-FEKT/ Drowsiness is a common side effect of this medicine. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Vitamins /VY-tuh-minz/ Amara takes her daily vitamins each morning. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Bandage /BAN-dij/ A clean bandage protects a cut from infection. Mime wrapping bandage around hand
13 Adhesive tape /ad-HEE-siv TAYP/ Adhesive tape holds gauze in place over a wound. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Gauze /GAWZ/ Clean gauze pads absorb blood from a small cut. Fingerspell GAUZE
15 Antiseptic /an-tih-SEP-tik/ Apply antiseptic to a scrape to prevent infection. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Ointment /OYNT-ment/ The ointment soothes the rash and helps it heal. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Cough syrup /KAWF SIR-up/ Cough syrup helps calm a persistent cough. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Eye drops /EYE DROPS/ Eye drops relieve dry, itchy eyes. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Inhaler /in-HAY-ler/ An inhaler helps people with asthma breathe easier. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Thermometer /ther-MOM-ih-ter/ Use a thermometer to check your temperature at home. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Ice pack /EYS PAK/ An ice pack reduces swelling on a sore ankle. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Heating pad /HEE-ting PAD/ A heating pad soothes sore, tight muscles. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Sling /SLING/ The sling holds a hurt arm still so it can heal. Fingerspell SLING
24 Over-the-counter /OH-ver thuh KOWN-ter/ Some medicines are over-the-counter and don't need a prescription. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Refill /REE-fil/ Amara calls the pharmacy for a refill on her vitamins. Fingerspell REFILL
