# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Add /AD/ Add three and four to get seven. Flat O-hands come together (combining)
2 Addition /uh-DISH-un/ Addition means putting numbers together. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Plus /PLUS/ Three plus two equals five. Fingerspell PLUS
4 Subtract /sub-TRAKT/ Subtract five from ten to get five. Bent hand swipes across opposite palm down
5 Subtraction /sub-TRAK-shun/ Subtraction means taking a number away. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Minus /MY-nus/ Ten minus three equals seven. Fingerspell MINUS
7 Multiply /MUL-tih-ply/ Multiply four times three to get twelve. V-hands cross in front
8 Multiplication /mul-tih-plih-KAY-shun/ Multiplication is repeated addition. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Times /TYMZ/ Six times two equals twelve. Index taps wrist (watch location) (repeated)
10 Divide /dih-VYD/ Divide twelve by three to get four. Flat hands slide apart diagonally
11 Division /dih-VIZH-un/ Division splits a number into equal groups. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Equals /EE-kwulz/ Two plus two equals four. Both bent hands tap together evenly (repeated)
13 Sum /SUM/ The sum of five and three is eight. Fingerspell SUM
14 Difference /DIF-er-ens/ The difference between ten and six is four. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Product /PROD-ukt/ The product of three and four is twelve. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Quotient /KWOH-shunt/ The quotient of twenty divided by five is four. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Remainder /rih-MAYN-der/ When you divide seven by two, the remainder is one. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Fraction /FRAK-shun/ One half is a fraction written as 1/2. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Decimal /DES-ih-mul/ The number 3.5 has a decimal point. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Percent /per-SENT/ Fifty percent means half. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Equation /ih-KWAY-zhun/ An equation shows that two sides are equal. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Problem /PROB-lem/ Kwame solves one math problem at a time. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Answer /AN-ser/ The answer to five plus five is ten. Index from chin points forward
24 Estimate /ES-tih-mayt/ Estimate how many marbles are in the jar. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Calculate /KAL-kyoo-layt/ Octavia can calculate the total in her head. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Greater than /GRAY-ter THAN/ Ten is greater than five. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Less than /LES THAN/ Three is less than seven. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Equal to /EE-kwul TOO/ Six is equal to three plus three. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Average /AV-er-ij/ The average of two, four, and six is four. Flat hand levels across opposite hand
30 Total /TOH-tul/ The total cost of the three items is fifteen dollars. Fingerspell TOTAL

Octavia's Tip:

"Math has its own vocabulary! Learn these words and math class gets easier. Say: 'I ADD. I SUBTRACT. I MULTIPLY. I DIVIDE.' Now say it with numbers: 'Two PLUS three EQUALS five!"
