# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Data /DAY-tuh/ Collect data by counting and measuring. D-hand moves along opposite palm (reading data)
2 Survey /SUR-vay/ Take a survey to find opinions. Fingerspell SURVEY
3 Result /rih-ZULT/ The results were surprising. Fingerspell RESULT
4 Percentage /per-SEN-tij/ Eighty percent of students passed. Draw percent sign in air (O / O)
5 Increase /in-KREES/ Temperature increased today. H-hand rises up opposite index
6 Decrease /dee-KREES/ Rainfall decreased this month. H-hand drops down opposite index
7 Average /AV-er-ij/ The average score was eighty-five. Flat hand levels across opposite hand
8 Rank /RANK/ Our school ranks third. Fingerspell RANK
9 Score /SKOR/ What was your math score? Both hands tally in air
10 Total /TOH-tul/ Total number of students is two hundred. Fingerspell TOTAL
11 Half /HAF/ Half of the class speaks Spanish. Fingerspell HALF
12 Double /DUB-ul/ Double the recipe for more people. Fingerspell DOUBLE
13 Triple /TRIP-ul/ Sales tripled during the holidays. Fingerspell TRIPLE
14 Majority /muh-JOR-ih-tee/ The majority voted yes. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Minority /my-NOR-ih-tee/ A minority chose option B. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Frequent /FREE-kwent/ Rain is frequent in April. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Rare /RAIR/ Snow in Florida is very rare. Fingerspell RARE
18 Compare /kum-PAIR/ Compare last month to this month. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Predict /prih-DIKT/ Can you predict tomorrow's weather? Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Trend /TREND/ The trend shows sales going up. Fingerspell TREND
21 Chart /CHART/ Draw a bar chart to show results. Index draws chart lines on opposite palm
22 Graph /GRAF/ The line graph shows temperature. 4-hand moves along opposite palm stepping up
23 Table /TAY-bul/ Organize data in a table. Both forearms stack, form flat surface
24 Row /ROH/ Each row shows one student. Fingerspell ROW
25 Column /KOL-um/ The first column shows the names. Fingerspell COLUMN
