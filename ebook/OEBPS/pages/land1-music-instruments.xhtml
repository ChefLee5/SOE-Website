# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Piano /pee-AN-oh/ She plays the piano beautifully. Fingerspell PIANO
2 Guitar /gih-TAR/ Kenji strums the guitar and sings a song. Fingerspell GUITAR
3 Drum /DRUM/ Beat the drum to keep the rhythm. Fingerspell DRUM
4 Violin /vy-oh-LIN/ The violin makes a sweet, high sound. Fingerspell VIOLIN
5 Flute /FLOOT/ The flute is a wind instrument made of metal. Fingerspell FLUTE
6 Trumpet /TRUM-pit/ The trumpet plays a bright, bold melody. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Tambourine /TAM-buh-reen/ Shake the tambourine to add rhythm. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Xylophone /ZY-loh-fohn/ Each bar of the xylophone plays a different note. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Melody /MEL-oh-dee/ A melody is the main tune of a song. Fingerspell MELODY
10 Rhythm /RITH-um/ Clap the rhythm with your hands. R-hand bounces forward rhythmically
11 Beat /BEET/ The beat is the steady pulse of the music. Fist taps chest rhythmically
12 Lyrics /LEER-iks/ The lyrics are the words of a song. Fingerspell LYRICS
13 Choir /KWY-er/ The school choir sings in four-part harmony. Fingerspell CHOIR
14 Orchestra /OR-kes-truh/ An orchestra has many different instruments. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Concert /KON-sert/ We went to a concert in the park last night. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Note /NOHT/ Each note has a different pitch and length. Fingerspell NOTE
17 Tempo /TEM-poh/ The tempo tells you how fast to play. Index bounces at varying speeds
18 Volume /VOL-yoom/ Turn down the volume — it's too loud. Fingerspell VOLUME
19 Harmony /HAR-moh-nee/ Harmony is when different notes sound good together. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Microphone /MY-kroh-fohn/ Speak into the microphone so everyone can hear. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Headphones /HED-fohnz/ Put on your headphones to listen to the recording. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Record /rih-KORD/ Let's record our song and share it. Fingerspell RECORD
23 Practice /PRAK-tis/ Practice the piano for thirty minutes every day. A-hand brushes back and forth on opposite index
24 Performer /per-FOR-mer/ The performer bowed after the standing ovation. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Audience /AW-dee-ents/ The audience clapped at the end of the concert. Fingerspell or sign each word
