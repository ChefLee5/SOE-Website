# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Happy /HAP-ee/ I am so happy today. Flat hand brushes up chest twice
2 Sad /SAD/ The movie made me sad. Open hands slide down face
3 Angry /ANG-ree/ Don't get angry, stay calm. Claw hand pulls from face
4 Scared /SKAIRD/ The thunder made me scared. Fists open outward at chest (startled)
5 Tired /TYRD/ I am very tired tonight. Bent hands at chest fall forward
6 Sick /SIK/ I feel sick today. Middle finger on forehead + stomach
7 Hungry /HUNG-ree/ I am hungry for lunch. C-hand slides down chest
8 Thirsty /THUR-stee/ I am thirsty for water. Index drags down throat
9 Food /FOOD/ The food is delicious. Flat-O taps mouth
10 Water /WOT-er/ Drink plenty of water. W-hand taps chin
11 Milk /MILK/ Pour some milk in the glass. C-hand squeezes (milking)
12 Bread /BRED/ Slice the fresh bread. Slice back of hand with opposite
13 Apple /AP-ul/ Eat an apple every day. X-knuckle twists on cheek
14 Home /HOHM/ There is no place like home. Flat-O touches near mouth, then cheek
15 School /SKOOL/ I go to school every day. Clap hands twice
16 Store /STOR/ Let's go to the store. Flat-O hands flick outward from chest
17 Hospital /HOS-pih-tul/ The hospital is nearby. H-hand draws cross on upper arm
18 Library /LY-brair-ee/ Read books at the library. L-hand circles in air
19 Park /PARK/ Play at the park after school. P-hand taps S-hand
20 Car /KAR/ Drive the car to work. Both C-hands mime steering
21 Bus /BUS/ Take the bus to school. Mime holding overhead bus strap
22 Bathroom /BATH-room/ Where is the bathroom? T-hand shakes between fists
23 Phone /FOHN/ Call me on the phone. Y-hand at ear
24 Book /BOOK/ Open the book and read. Palms together, open like book
25 Money /MUN-ee/ Save your money wisely. Flat-O taps opposite palm twice
