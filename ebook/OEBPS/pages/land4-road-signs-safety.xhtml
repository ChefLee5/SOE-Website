Scene 14: Road Signs & Safety

Walking or driving through Aquaria, Ronan and Nerissa learn to read the signs that keep everyone safe.

A richly detailed scene depicting 'Road Signs & Safety' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Stop /STOP/ The red stop sign means come to a complete halt. Flat hand chops down on opposite palm
2 Yield /YEELD/ Yield means slow down and let others go first. Fingerspell YIELD
3 One way /WUN WAY/ This is a one way street — traffic goes in one direction. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Do not enter /DOO NOT EN-ter/ The "Do Not Enter" sign means you cannot go that way. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Speed limit /SPEED LIM-it/ The speed limit is 25 miles per hour in a school zone. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 School zone /SKOOL ZOHN/ Drive slowly through the school zone. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Crosswalk /KROS-wawk/ Pedestrians cross at the crosswalk. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Pedestrian crossing /peh-DES-tree-un KROS-ing/ Watch for the pedestrian crossing sign. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Railroad crossing /RAYL-rohd KROS-ing/ Stop and look both ways at a railroad crossing. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Merge /MURJ/ The merge sign means two lanes become one. Fingerspell MERGE
11 Exit /EG-zit/ Take the next exit off the highway. Fingerspell EXIT
12 Detour /DEE-toor/ Follow the detour signs around road construction. Fingerspell DETOUR
13 No parking /NOH PAR-king/ You will get a ticket if you park in a no parking zone. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Handicapped parking /HAN-dee-kapt PAR-king/ Handicapped parking spaces are near the entrance. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Caution /KAW-shun/ The caution sign warns of danger ahead. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Construction zone /kun-STRUK-shun ZOHN/ Workers are ahead in the construction zone. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Dead end /DED END/ The road ends at a dead end. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 U-turn /YOO-TURN/ Make a U-turn to go back the other way. Fingerspell U-TURN
19 Curve /KURV/ Slow down at the sharp curve ahead. Fingerspell CURVE
20 Warning sign /WORN-ing SYN/ A warning sign alerts you to possible danger. Fingerspell or sign each word
