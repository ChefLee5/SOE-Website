A richly detailed scene depicting 'Ages & Life Stages' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Baby /BAY-bee/ A baby is a child from birth to about one year old. Rock arms like cradling baby
2 Toddler /TOD-ler/ A toddler is learning to walk and talk. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Child /CHYLD/ A child is a young person between two and twelve. Pat downward in air (showing height)
4 Teenager /TEEN-ay-jer/ A teenager is between thirteen and nineteen years old. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Adult /uh-DULT/ An adult is a fully grown person, usually eighteen or older. A-hand moves from forehead to chest
6 Senior /SEEN-yer/ A senior citizen is over sixty-five years old. Fingerspell SENIOR
7 Age /AYJ/ What is your age? I am thirty-two years old. Pull chin down with C-hand
8 Birthday /BURTH-day/ Your birthday is the day you were born. Middle finger touches chin, then chest
9 Anniversary /an-ih-VUR-suh-ree/ An anniversary marks years since a special event. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Generation /jen-er-AY-shun/ A generation is about twenty-five to thirty years. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Youth /YOOTH/ Youth is the time of life when you are young. Fingerspell YOUTH
12 Elderly /EL-der-lee/ The elderly deserve our respect and care. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Grow up /GROH UP/ What do you want to be when you grow up? Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Milestone /MYL-stohn/ A first word is a milestone in every baby's life. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Lifespan /LYF-span/ The average human lifespan is about seventy-five years. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Mature /muh-CHOOR/ To mature means to grow older and wiser. Fingerspell MATURE
17 Infant /IN-funt/ An infant needs constant care from a parent. Fingerspell INFANT
18 Middle-aged /MID-ul AYJD/ A middle-aged person is usually between forty and sixty. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Aging /AY-jing/ Aging is a natural part of every living thing. Fingerspell AGING
20 Memory /MEM-or-ee/ A memory is something you remember from the past. Fingerspell MEMORY
