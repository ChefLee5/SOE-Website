# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Breakfast /BREK-fust/ Breakfast gives your body energy to start the day. Flat O to mouth + arm rises (EAT + MORNING)
2 Lunch /LUNCH/ Felix eats lunch at noon in the village square. Flat O to mouth + L-arm (EAT + NOON)
3 Dinner /DIN-er/ Dinner brings the whole family together at the table. Flat O to mouth + hand curves down (EAT + EVENING)
4 Snack /SNAK/ An apple is a healthy snack between meals. Fingerspell SNACK
5 Plate /PLAYT/ Each person has a clean plate at their place. Both hands outline circle (plate shape)
6 Bowl /BOHL/ Amara ladles soup into a deep bowl. Both cupped hands form bowl shape
7 Cup /KUP/ Felix drinks water from a tall glass cup. C-hand taps opposite flat palm (cup on saucer)
8 Glass /GLAS/ The glass of juice is cold and refreshing. C-hand rises from opposite palm (tall glass)
9 Mug /MUG/ Amara wraps her hands around a warm mug of tea. Fingerspell MUG
10 Fork /FORK/ Use the fork to pick up pieces of food. V-hand stabs opposite palm
11 Knife /NYF/ The butter knife spreads jam on the toast. Index finger slides along opposite index (cutting)
12 Spoon /SPOON/ Stir your tea with a small spoon. Curved H scoops from opposite palm to mouth
13 Chopsticks /CHOP-stiks/ Felix practices eating rice with chopsticks. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Napkin /NAP-kin/ Place the napkin on your lap before eating. Fingerspell NAPKIN
15 Placemat /PLAYS-mat/ The colorful placemat protects the table. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Bread /BRED/ Warm bread with butter melts in your mouth. Slice back of hand with opposite hand
17 Rice /RYS/ Fluffy white rice goes with many dishes. Both cupped hands scoop up
18 Pasta /PAH-stuh/ Long strands of pasta twirl around the fork. Fingerspell PASTA
19 Soup /SOOP/ The hot soup warms you up on a cold day. Curved hand scoops up to mouth
20 Salad /SAL-ud/ A fresh green salad starts the meal. Both claw hands toss upward (mixing)
21 Sandwich /SAND-wich/ Felix makes a sandwich with turkey and lettuce. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Eggs /EGZ/ Scrambled eggs are Felix's favorite breakfast. H-fingers crack apart (repeated)
23 Cheese /CHEEZ/ A slice of yellow cheese tops the burger. Palms press and twist together
24 Chicken /CHIK-en/ Grilled chicken is a good source of protein. G-hand pecks from mouth
25 Fish /FISH/ Fish is healthy for the brain and the body. Flat hand swims forward like fish
26 Beef /BEEF/ Beef stew cooks slowly in the big pot. Fingerspell BEEF
27 Beans /BEENZ/ Black beans are rich in protein and fiber. Fingerspell BEANS
28 Tortilla /tor-TEE-yuh/ Amara wraps beans and rice in a warm tortilla. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Cereal /SEER-ee-ul/ Felix pours cereal and milk for a quick breakfast. Fingerspell CEREAL
30 Yogurt /YOH-gurt/ Yogurt with fruit is a healthy snack. Fingerspell YOGURT
31 Juice /JOOS/ Fresh-squeezed orange juice is full of vitamin C. J-hand + DRINK motion
32 Water /WAW-ter/ Drink plenty of water throughout the day. W-hand taps chin
33 Milk /MILK/ Cold milk goes perfectly with warm cookies. C-hand squeezes (milking motion)
34 Tea /TEE/ Chamomile tea helps you relax before bed. F-hand stirs in opposite C-hand (stirring cup)
35 Dessert /dih-ZURT/ Fruit salad is a light and sweet dessert. Fingerspell or sign each word
