# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Polite /poh-LYT/ Polite words open doors that rudeness cannot. Thumb of 5-hand taps chest gently
2 Rude /ROOD/ Speaking rudely hurts people's feelings. Fingerspell RUDE
3 Manners /MAN-erz/ Good manners show respect for others. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 May I /MAY eye/ May I please borrow your pencil? Fingerspell MAY I
5 Pardon /PAR-dun/ Pardon me, could you repeat that? Fingerspell PARDON
6 After you /AF-ter yoo/ After you — please go first through the door. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Bless you /BLES yoo/ Bless you! Kenji says after Aiko sneezes. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Congratulations /kun-grat-yoo-LAY-shunz/ Congratulations on winning the spelling bee! Fingerspell or sign each word
9 I appreciate it /eye uh-PREE-shee-ayt it/ I appreciate your help with my homework. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 No problem /noh PROB-lum/ No problem! I'm happy to help you. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Certainly /SUR-ten-lee/ Certainly! I can show you the way. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Of course /ov KORS/ Of course you can sit with us at lunch. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Patience /PAY-shunss/ Patience means waiting without getting upset. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Kind /KYND/ A kind word can change someone's whole day. Fingerspell KIND
15 Gentle /JEN-tul/ Speak in a gentle voice when someone is sad. Fingerspell GENTLE
16 Share /SHAIR/ Kenji shares his crayons with the new student. Flat hand slides between opposite thumb and index
17 Take turns /TAYK TURNZ/ Take turns so everyone gets a chance to play. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Interrupt /in-ter-UPT/ Don't interrupt when someone else is talking. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Listen /LIS-en/ Listen carefully when your teacher speaks. Cup hand behind ear
20 Apologize /uh-POL-oh-jyz/ Apologize sincerely when you make a mistake. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Forgive /for-GIV/ Forgive your friend and start fresh. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Compliment /KOM-plih-ment/ Give a compliment: "I like your drawing!" Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Gratitude /GRAT-ih-tood/ Gratitude means being thankful for what you have. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Considerate /kun-SID-er-it/ Being considerate means thinking about how others feel. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Courteous /KUR-tee-us/ A courteous person holds the door open for others. Fingerspell or sign each word
