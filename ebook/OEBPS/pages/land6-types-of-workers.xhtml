Scene 2: Types of Workers

In every corner of Sophia, people work to keep the community running. Athena lists them all.

🖼️ Illustration: Types of Workers
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Types of Workers' with numbered callout markers (1–30) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Doctor /DOK-ter/ A doctor diagnoses and treats illnesses. D-hand or M-hand taps opposite wrist
2 Nurse /NURS/ A nurse cares for patients in the hospital. N-hand taps opposite wrist
3 Teacher /TEE-cher/ A teacher helps students learn new things. Both flat hands at temples, move outward + PERSON
4 Firefighter /FYR-fy-ter/ A firefighter rescues people and puts out fires. B-hand at forehead (shield)
5 Police officer /puh-LEES OF-ih-ser/ A police officer protects and serves the community. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Lawyer /LOY-er/ A lawyer helps people understand and follow the law. Fingerspell LAWYER
7 Chef /SHEF/ A chef creates delicious meals in a restaurant kitchen. Flip hand on opposite palm (COOK) + PERSON
8 Mechanic /meh-KAN-ik/ A mechanic fixes cars and engines. S-fists twist opposite directions (wrench)
9 Electrician /ee-lek-TRISH-un/ An electrician installs and repairs electrical wiring. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Plumber /PLUM-er/ A plumber fixes pipes, sinks, and toilets. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Carpenter /KAR-pen-ter/ A carpenter builds and repairs things made of wood. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Painter /PAYN-ter/ A painter covers walls with fresh coats of paint. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Farmer /FAR-mer/ A farmer grows crops and raises animals. Fingerspell FARMER
14 Bus driver /BUS DRY-ver/ The bus driver safely takes passengers to their stops. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Postal worker /POHS-tul WUR-ker/ The postal worker delivers mail and packages. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Cashier /ka-SHEER/ The cashier rings up purchases at the store. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Janitor /JAN-ih-ter/ The janitor keeps the building clean and tidy. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Accountant /uh-KOWN-tant/ An accountant manages money and financial records. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Pharmacist /FAR-muh-sist/ A pharmacist prepares and gives out medicine. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Dentist /DEN-tist/ A dentist takes care of your teeth and gums. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Veterinarian /vet-er-ih-NAIR-ee-un/ A veterinarian is a doctor for animals. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Engineer /en-juh-NEER/ An engineer designs buildings, roads, and machines. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Architect /AR-kih-tekt/ An architect designs the plans for new buildings. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Programmer /PROH-gram-er/ A programmer writes code for computers and apps. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Artist /AR-tist/ An artist creates paintings, sculptures, or music. Fingerspell ARTIST
26 Journalist /JUR-nuh-list/ A journalist writes news stories for the newspaper. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Translator /TRANS-lay-ter/ A translator converts words from one language to another. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Social worker /SOH-shul WUR-ker/ A social worker helps families in difficult situations. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Paramedic /pair-uh-MED-ik/ A paramedic provides emergency medical care. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Scientist /SY-en-tist/ A scientist studies the world through experiments. Fingerspell or sign each word
