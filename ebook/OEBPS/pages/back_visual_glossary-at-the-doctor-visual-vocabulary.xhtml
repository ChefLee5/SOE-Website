Scene 4: At the Doctor — Visual Vocabulary

Knowing medical words helps you take care of yourself and your family.

🖼️ Illustration: At the Doctor — Visual Vocabulary
A richly detailed scene depicting 'At the Doctor — Visual Vocabulary' with numbered callout markers (1–25) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Waiting room /WAY-ting ROOM/ Please sit in the waiting room until the doctor calls you. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Appointment /uh-POYNT-ment/ I have a doctor's appointment at two o'clock. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Insurance card /in-SHOOR-ents KARD/ Show your insurance card at the front desk. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Clipboard /KLIP-bord/ Fill out the forms on the clipboard. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Blood pressure /BLUD PRESH-er/ The nurse checks your blood pressure first. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Stethoscope /STETH-oh-skohp/ The doctor listens to your heart with a stethoscope. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Thermometer /ther-MOM-ih-ter/ The thermometer shows your body temperature. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Scale /SKAYL/ Step on the scale to check your weight. Both flat hands balance like scale
9 Exam table /eg-ZAM TAY-bul/ Sit on the exam table and wait for the doctor. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Prescription /prih-SKRIP-shun/ The doctor writes a prescription for your medicine. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Syringe /sih-RINJ/ The nurse uses a syringe to give the injection. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Bandage /BAN-dij/ Put a bandage on the cut to keep it clean. Mime wrapping bandage around hand
13 X-ray /EKS-ray/ The X-ray shows the bones inside your body. Fingerspell X-RAY
14 Symptom /SIMP-tum/ A cough is a symptom of a cold. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Diagnosis /dy-ug-NOH-sis/ The diagnosis tells you what illness you have. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Treatment /TREET-ment/ The treatment includes rest and medicine. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Follow-up /FOL-oh-UP/ Schedule a follow-up visit in two weeks. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Referral /rih-FUR-ul/ The doctor gave me a referral to see a specialist. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Co-pay /KOH-pay/ The co-pay for each visit is twenty dollars. Fingerspell CO-PAY
20 Pharmacy /FAR-muh-see/ Pick up your medicine at the pharmacy. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Dosage /DOH-sij/ Take the correct dosage as directed on the label. Fingerspell DOSAGE
22 Side effect /SYD ih-FEKT/ Drowsiness is a common side effect of this medicine. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Allergic /uh-LUR-jik/ Tell the doctor if you are allergic to any medicine. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Emergency /ee-MUR-jen-see/ Call 911 in an emergency. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Recovery /rih-KUV-er-ee/ Recovery from surgery takes several weeks. Fingerspell or sign each word
