# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Sunny /SUN-ee/ It is sunny and warm outside today. Fingerspell SUNNY
2 Cloudy /KLOWD-ee/ The sky is gray and cloudy this afternoon. Fingerspell CLOUDY
3 Rainy /RAYN-ee/ Bring an umbrella — it's rainy today. Fingerspell RAINY
4 Windy /WIN-dee/ The windy day blows the leaves off the trees. Fingerspell WINDY
5 Snowy /SNOH-ee/ Everything looks white and beautiful on a snowy day. Fingerspell SNOWY
6 Stormy /STORM-ee/ Thunder and lightning make the night stormy. Fingerspell STORMY
7 Hot /HOT/ It is very hot in July and August. C-hand at mouth, flick away sharply
8 Cold /KOHLD/ Wear a coat because it is cold outside. Both fists shake near body (shivering)
9 Warm /WORM/ Spring days are warm but not hot. C-hand at mouth, slowly open (gentle breath)
10 Cool /KOOL/ Fall mornings are cool and crisp. Both open hands fan at body
11 Fog /FOG/ Thick fog makes it hard to see the road. Fingerspell FOG
12 Hail /HAYL/ Small balls of ice called hail fell from the sky. Fingerspell HAIL
13 Thunder /THUN-der/ The loud thunder shakes the windows. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Lightning /LYT-ning/ A flash of lightning lights up the sky. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Rainbow /RAYN-boh/ A rainbow appears after the rain stops. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Umbrella /um-BREL-uh/ Vesta opens her umbrella when it starts to rain. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Thermometer /thur-MOM-ih-ter/ The thermometer shows the temperature outside. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Forecast /FOR-kast/ The weather forecast says it will rain tomorrow. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Spring /SPRING/ Flowers bloom and baby animals are born in spring. Fingerspell SPRING
20 Summer /SUM-er/ Summer has the longest and warmest days. Fingerspell SUMMER
21 Fall /FAWL/ Leaves change color and fall from the trees in autumn. Fingerspell FALL
22 Winter /WIN-ter/ Winter brings snow, ice, and short cold days. Fingerspell WINTER
23 Breeze /BREEZ/ A gentle breeze cools us on a warm day. Fingerspell BREEZE
24 Frost /FROST/ White frost covers the grass early in the morning. Fingerspell FROST
25 Humidity /hyoo-MID-ih-tee/ High humidity makes the air feel thick and sticky. Fingerspell or sign each word

Silas's Tip:
