# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Wake up /WAYK UP/ Kenji wakes up at six o'clock every morning. Pinch at eyes, flick open
2 Get up /GET UP/ Get up when the alarm rings. Fingerspell GET UP
3 Brush teeth /BRUSH TEETH/ Brush your teeth for two minutes morning and night. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Wash face /WASH FAYS/ Aiko washes her face with warm water. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Comb hair /KOHM HAIR/ Comb your hair before you leave the house. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Get dressed /GET DRESD/ Get dressed and put on your shoes. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Eat breakfast /EET BREK-fust/ Eat a healthy breakfast to start the day. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Pack lunch /PAK LUNCH/ Pack your lunch in a bag with an ice pack. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Leave home /LEEV HOHM/ Kenji leaves home at seven thirty. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Walk to school /WAWK too SKOOL/ Aiko walks to school with her neighbor. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Arrive /uh-RYV/ The students arrive before the bell rings. Fingerspell ARRIVE
12 Study /STUD-ee/ Study your vocabulary words every day. 5-hand wiggles fingers at opposite palm
13 Take a break /TAYK uh BRAYK/ Take a break and stretch your legs. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Eat lunch /EET LUNCH/ Students eat lunch in the cafeteria at noon. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Come home /KUM HOHM/ Kenji comes home at three thirty. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Do homework /DOO HOHM-wurk/ Do your homework before you play outside. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Practice /PRAK-tis/ Aiko practices piano for thirty minutes. A-hand brushes back and forth on opposite index
18 Play /PLAY/ Children play in the yard after school. Y-hands shake in front
19 Eat dinner /EET DIN-er/ The family eats dinner together at six o'clock. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Watch TV /WOCH TEE-VEE/ Kenji watches TV for one hour before bed. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Take a bath /TAYK uh BATH/ Take a warm bath before bedtime. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Read a book /REED uh BOOK/ Read a book in bed to relax your mind. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Say goodnight /SAY GOOD-nyt/ Say goodnight to everyone in the house. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Turn off the light /TURN OFF thuh LYT/ Turn off the light and close your eyes. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Go to sleep /GOH too SLEEP/ Aiko goes to sleep at nine o'clock. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Alarm clock /uh-LARM KLOK/ The alarm clock rings at six every morning. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Routine /roo-TEEN/ A routine is a set of things you do every day. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Schedule /SKED-yool/ Follow your schedule so you have time for everything. 4-hand slides down, then across opposite palm
29 On time /ON TYM/ Being on time shows you respect other people. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Late /LAYT/ If you are late, say "I'm sorry I'm late." Hand waves downward beside hip
31 Early /UR-lee/ Aiko likes to arrive early to help set up. Middle finger slides over fist knuckle
32 Habit /HAB-it/ A good habit makes your life easier and better. Fingerspell HABIT
33 Chores /CHORZ/ Everyone in the family helps with chores. Fingerspell CHORES
34 Make the bed /MAYK thuh BED/ Make the bed first thing when you wake up. Fingerspell or sign each word
35 Set the table /SET thuh TAY-bul/ Set the table with plates, forks, and napkins. Fingerspell or sign each word
36 Clean up /KLEEN UP/ Clean up your room before going outside. Fingerspell or sign each word
37 Take out trash /TAYK OWT TRASH/ Take out the trash on Tuesday nights. Fingerspell or sign each word
38 Feed the pet /FEED thuh PET/ Feed the pet before you eat your own breakfast. Fingerspell or sign each word
39 Water plants /WAW-ter PLANTS/ Water the plants every other day. Fingerspell or sign each word
40 Bedtime /BED-tym/ Bedtime is at nine o'clock on school nights. Fingerspell or sign each word
