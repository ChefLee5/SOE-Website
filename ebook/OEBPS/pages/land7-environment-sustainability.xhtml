# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Environment /en-VY-run-ment/ The environment is everything around us — air, water, land. E-hand circles around index (AROUND + E)
2 Climate change /KLY-mit CHAYNJ/ Climate change is the long-term shift in weather patterns. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Global warming /GLOH-bul WOR-ming/ Global warming is the gradual increase in Earth's temperature. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Greenhouse gas /GREEN-hows GAS/ Carbon dioxide is a greenhouse gas that traps heat. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Carbon footprint /KAR-bun FOOT-print/ Your carbon footprint is the amount of CO2 your activities produce. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Recycle /ree-SY-kul/ Recycle cans, bottles, and paper. R-hand circles (going around again)
7 Reuse /ree-YOOZ/ Reuse bags and containers instead of throwing them away. Fingerspell REUSE
8 Reduce /rih-DOOS/ Reduce waste by buying only what you need. Fingerspell REDUCE
9 Compost /KOM-pohst/ Compost food scraps to make rich soil for the garden. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Biodegradable /by-oh-dih-GRAY-duh-bul/ Biodegradable materials break down naturally. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Endangered /en-DAYN-jerd/ Endangered species are animals at risk of disappearing. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Extinct /ek-STINGKT/ Dinosaurs are extinct — they no longer exist. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Deforestation /dee-for-es-TAY-shun/ Deforestation is cutting down forests in large areas. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Renewable energy /rih-NOO-uh-bul EN-er-jee/ Solar and wind are types of renewable energy. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Sustainability /sus-tayn-uh-BIL-ih-tee/ Sustainability means meeting today's needs without harming the future. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Organic /or-GAN-ik/ Organic farming avoids harmful chemicals. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Pollution /puh-LOO-shun/ Air pollution comes from cars and factories. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Clean energy /KLEEN EN-er-jee/ Clean energy does not produce pollution. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Conservation /kon-ser-VAY-shun/ Conservation protects natural habitats and wildlife. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Ecosystem /EE-koh-sis-tem/ Every ecosystem depends on balance to survive. Fingerspell or sign each word

Selene's Tip:

"YOU can help the planet! Learn and say these THREE words every day: REDUCE, REUSE, RECYCLE. Now teach them to someone else — that's how change spreads!"
