Scene 20: Seeds & Growing

In Vesta's Greenhouse, every seed tells a story

🖼️ Illustration: Seeds & Growing
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Seeds & Growing' with numbered callout markers (1–25) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Seed /SEED/ Plant the seed in the soil and water it. Mime sprinkling seeds from pinched fingers
2 Sprout /SPROWT/ The sprout pushes up through the soil. Fingerspell SPROUT
3 Seedling /SEED-ling/ The seedling needs sunlight to grow strong. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Root /ROOT/ Roots grow underground and drink water. 5-hand pushes down through opposite C-hand
5 Stem /STEM/ The stem holds the flower up toward the sun. Fingerspell STEM
6 Leaf /LEEF/ Each leaf catches sunlight. Index finger hangs from opposite wrist, sways
7 Bud /BUD/ A bud is a flower before it opens. Fingerspell BUD
8 Bloom /BLOOM/ The flowers bloom in spring. Fingerspell BLOOM
9 Petal /PET-ul/ Each petal of the rose is soft and colorful. Fingerspell PETAL
10 Pollen /POL-en/ Bees carry pollen from flower to flower. Fingerspell POLLEN
11 Compost /KOM-pohst/ Add compost to make soil richer. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Fertilizer /FUR-tih-ly-zer/ Fertilizer helps plants grow bigger. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Greenhouse /GREEN-hows/ Grow plants year-round in the greenhouse. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Water /WOT-er/ Water the plants every morning. W-hand taps chin
15 Sunlight /SUN-lyt/ Plants need sunlight to make food. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Soil /SOYL/ Healthy soil is dark and rich. Fingerspell SOIL
17 Pot /POT/ Plant the herb in a small clay pot. P-hands mime stirring in pot
18 Planter /PLAN-ter/ The window planter is full of flowers. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Weed /WEED/ Pull the weeds so they do not steal nutrients. Fingerspell WEED
20 Harvest /HAR-vest/ Harvest the tomatoes when they turn red. Curved hand mimes gathering crops
21 Vine /VYN/ The vine climbs up the garden fence. Fingerspell VINE
22 Branch /BRANCH/ The bird sits on a branch of the oak tree. Fingerspell BRANCH
23 Trunk /TRUNK/ The trunk of the tree is thick and strong. Fingerspell TRUNK
24 Bark /BARK/ The bark protects the tree like a coat. Fingerspell BARK
25 Growth /GROHTH/ The growth of the plant was amazing. Fingerspell GROWTH
