Scene 3: The Workplace

Ezra shows the inside of a typical Sophia office. Everything has a name and a function.

A richly detailed scene depicting 'The Workplace' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Office /OF-is/ The office has desks, computers, and a copier. O-hands outline box shape
2 Desk /DESK/ Ezra keeps his files organized on his desk. Sign TABLE (forearms stacked, then flat)
3 Computer /kum-PYOO-ter/ The computer is the main tool for most office work. C-hand bounces up opposite forearm
4 Printer /PRIN-ter/ The printer makes paper copies of digital documents. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Copier /KOP-ee-er/ Use the copier to make duplicates of the report. Fingerspell COPIER
6 File cabinet /FYL KAB-ih-net/ Old documents are stored in the file cabinet. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Stapler /STAY-pler/ Use the stapler to hold pages together. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Paper clip /PAY-per KLIP/ A paper clip holds a few pages together. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Sticky notes /STIK-ee NOHTS/ Write reminders on sticky notes and post them on your monitor. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Meeting /MEE-ting/ The team meeting is at ten o'clock in the conference room. Both 5-hands come together, fingertips meet
11 Conference room /KON-fer-ens ROOM/ The conference room has a long table and a projector. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Break room /BRAYK ROOM/ Eat lunch and relax in the break room. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Cubicle /KYOO-bih-kul/ Each worker has a cubicle with walls for privacy. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Supervisor /SOO-per-vy-zer/ The supervisor checks in with the team each morning. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Project /PROJ-ekt/ The team is working on a big project this month. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Report /rih-PORT/ Write a report at the end of every week. Fingerspell REPORT
17 Email /EE-mayl/ Send the document by email. Index shoots into opposite C-hand (mail + computer)
18 Schedule /SKED-yool/ Check the schedule for today's meetings. 4-hand slides down, then across opposite palm
19 Paycheck /PAY-chek/ Ezra receives his paycheck every two weeks. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Time card /TYM KARD/ Swipe your time card to clock in and out. Fingerspell or sign each word

Ezra's Tip:
