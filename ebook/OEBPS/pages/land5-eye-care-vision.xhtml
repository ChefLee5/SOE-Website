11 Pupil /PYOO-pul/ The pupil gets bigger in the dark. Fingerspell PUPIL

22 Blind /BLYND/ Braille helps blind people read. Fingerspell BLIND
23 Color blind /KUL-er BLYND/ Color blind people have trouble seeing certain colors. Fingerspell or sign each word
