Scene 6: The Environment

Terrasol has every kind of landscape — from meadows to deserts, from wetlands to coral reefs. Silas and Vesta are its caretakers.

🖼️ Illustration: The Environment
A richly detailed scene depicting 'The Environment' with numbered callout markers (1–30) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Meadow /MED-oh/ Wildflowers dot the green meadow in springtime. Fingerspell MEADOW
2 Desert /DEZ-ert/ The desert is hot and dry with very little rain. Both flat hands push outward (dry, vast)
3 Wetland /WET-land/ The wetland is full of birds, frogs, and tall reeds. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Pond /POND/ Dragonflies hover over the still pond. Fingerspell POND
5 Stream /STREEM/ A clear stream flows over smooth, round stones. Fingerspell STREAM
6 Hill /HIL/ Vesta runs up the grassy hill to see the sunrise. Flat hand shapes hill curve
7 Valley /VAL-ee/ The green valley lies between two mountain ridges. Both flat hands dip down in center
8 Cliff /KLIF/ The cliff drops steeply to the ocean below. Fingerspell CLIFF
9 Cave /KAYV/ Bats sleep inside the dark, cool cave. Fingerspell CAVE
10 Prairie /PRAIR-ee/ The prairie stretches flat as far as the eye can see. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Marsh /MARSH/ The marsh is wet and muddy with cattails growing tall. Fingerspell MARSH
12 Jungle /JUNG-gul/ The thick jungle is warm, wet, and full of life. Fingerspell JUNGLE
13 Rainforest /RAYN-for-est/ The rainforest gets more rain than any other place. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Tundra /TUN-druh/ The frozen tundra has very few trees. Fingerspell TUNDRA
15 Coral reef /KOR-ul REEF/ The coral reef is a colorful underwater city for fish. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Volcano /vol-KAY-noh/ The volcano has been quiet for a hundred years. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Island /EYE-lund/ A small island sits in the middle of the blue lake. I-hand circles on opposite palm
18 Peninsula /pen-IN-suh-luh/ The peninsula is land surrounded by water on three sides. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Canyon /KAN-yun/ The deep canyon was carved by a river over millions of years. Fingerspell CANYON
20 Glacier /GLAY-sher/ The ancient glacier moves slowly down the mountain. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Ecosystem /EE-koh-sis-tem/ Every plant and animal is part of the ecosystem. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Habitat /HAB-ih-tat/ A habitat is the natural home of an animal or plant. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Conservation /kon-ser-VAY-shun/ Conservation means protecting nature for future generations. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Pollution /puh-LOO-shun/ Pollution harms the air, water, and land. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Recycle /ree-SY-kul/ Silas and Vesta recycle paper, plastic, and glass. R-hand circles (going around again)
26 Endangered /en-DAYN-jerd/ Endangered animals need our help to survive. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Extinct /ek-STINKT/ Dinosaurs are extinct — they no longer live on Earth. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Biodiversity /by-oh-dy-VUR-sih-tee/ Biodiversity means many different kinds of living things. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Renewable /rih-NOO-uh-bul/ Solar power and wind are renewable sources of energy. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Climate /KLY-mit/ The climate in Terrasol changes from season to season. Fingerspell or sign each word
