Scene 8: The Home

Kenji and Aiko give a tour of their home in Harmonia. Every room has a purpose, and every object has a name.

🖼️ Illustration: The Home
A richly detailed scene depicting 'The Home' with numbered callout markers (1–45) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 House /HOWS/ The house has a front yard and a back door. Both hands form roof peak, then walls
2 Apartment /uh-PART-ment/ An apartment is a home inside a larger building. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Room /ROOM/ Each room in the house has a different purpose. R-hands form box shape
4 Kitchen /KICH-en/ The kitchen is where the family cooks meals. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Living room /LIV-ing ROOM/ The living room has a sofa, a TV, and bookshelves. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Bedroom /BED-room/ Kenji reads books in his bedroom before sleep. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Bathroom /BATH-room/ Wash your hands in the bathroom after playing. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Dining room /DY-ning ROOM/ The family eats dinner together in the dining room. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Garage /guh-RAHJ/ The car is parked in the garage. Fingerspell GARAGE
10 Basement /BAYS-ment/ The basement is the room below the ground floor. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Attic /AT-ik/ Old photo albums are stored in the attic. Fingerspell ATTIC
12 Hallway /HAWL-way/ The hallway connects the bedrooms to the living room. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Door /DOR/ Close the door quietly when someone is sleeping. Both B-hands: one swings open from other
14 Window /WIN-doh/ Open the window to let in fresh air. Both flat hands stack, top slides open
15 Roof /ROOF/ The roof keeps rain and snow out of the house. Fingerspell ROOF
16 Floor /FLOR/ Sweep the floor before setting the table. Both flat hands horizontal, slide apart
17 Ceiling /SEE-ling/ The ceiling fan cools the room in summer. Flat hand above, pats up
18 Wall /WAWL/ Aiko hangs her paintings on the wall. Both flat hands vertical, parallel
19 Stairs /STAIRZ/ Walk carefully going up and down the stairs. Fingerspell STAIRS
20 Closet /KLOZ-it/ Keep your coats and shoes in the closet. Fingerspell CLOSET
21 Furniture /FUR-nih-chur/ A table, chairs, and a bed are all furniture. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Sofa /SOH-fuh/ The whole family sits on the sofa for movie night. Fingerspell SOFA
23 Table /TAY-bul/ Set the table with plates, cups, and napkins. Both forearms stack, form flat surface
24 Bed /BED/ Make your bed every morning when you wake up. Fingerspell BED
25 Pillow /PIL-oh/ Rest your head on the soft pillow. Fingerspell PILLOW
26 Blanket /BLANG-kit/ Pull the warm blanket up to your chin. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Lamp /LAMP/ Turn on the lamp to read at night. Fingerspell LAMP
28 Mirror /MIR-er/ Kenji checks his hair in the mirror. Fingerspell MIRROR
29 Curtains /KUR-tunz/ Close the curtains to block the sunlight. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Rug /RUG/ The soft rug covers the cold floor. Fingerspell RUG
31 Sink /SINK/ Wash the dishes in the kitchen sink. Fingerspell SINK
32 Toilet /TOY-let/ Flush the toilet after each use. Fingerspell TOILET
33 Bathtub /BATH-tub/ The bathtub fills with warm water for bath time. Fingerspell or sign each word
34 Shower /SHOW-er/ Kenji takes a quick shower before school. 5-hand above head opens/closes (water spraying)
35 Refrigerator /rih-FRIJ-er-ay-ter/ Store milk and vegetables in the refrigerator. Both hands shiver with R-hands
36 Microwave /MY-kroh-wayv/ Heat the soup in the microwave for two minutes. Fingerspell or sign each word
37 Dishwasher /DISH-wash-er/ Load the dirty dishes into the dishwasher. Fingerspell or sign each word
38 Washing machine /WASH-ing muh-SHEEN/ The washing machine cleans clothes with soap and water. Fingerspell or sign each word
39 Dryer /DRY-er/ The dryer makes wet clothes warm and dry. Fingerspell DRYER
40 Key /KEE/ Always carry your house key in a safe place. Fingerspell KEY
41 Lock /LOK/ Lock the door at night for safety. Fingerspell LOCK
42 Doorbell /DOR-bel/ Ring the doorbell and wait for someone to answer. Fingerspell or sign each word
43 Mailbox /MAYL-boks/ Aiko checks the mailbox for letters. Fingerspell or sign each word
44 Yard /YARD/ The children play in the front yard. Fingerspell YARD
45 Porch /PORCH/ Grandfather sits on the porch in his rocking chair. Fingerspell PORCH
