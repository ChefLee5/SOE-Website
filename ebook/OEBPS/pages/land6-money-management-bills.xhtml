# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Bill /BIL/ Pay the electricity bill before the due date. Fingerspell BILL
2 Rent /RENT/ Rent is the monthly payment for your apartment. Fingerspell RENT
3 Mortgage /MOR-gij/ A mortgage is a monthly payment for a house. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Utilities /yoo-TIL-ih-teez/ Utilities include water, gas, and electricity. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Electricity /ee-lek-TRIS-ih-tee/ The electricity bill goes up in the summer. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Water bill /WAW-ter BIL/ The water bill is paid every two months. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Gas bill /GAS BIL/ The gas bill is higher in the winter for heating. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Insurance /in-SHOOR-uns/ Health insurance helps pay for doctor visits. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Car insurance /KAR in-SHOOR-uns/ Car insurance is required by law in most states. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Due date /DOO DAYT/ Pay before the due date to avoid a late fee. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Late fee /LAYT FEE/ A late fee is charged if you pay after the due date. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Income /IN-kum/ Income is the total money you earn. Hand scoops money toward body
13 Expense /ek-SPENS/ Rent is the biggest monthly expense. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Budget /BUJ-it/ A budget tracks what you earn and what you spend. B-hands on opposite palm (MONEY + PLAN)
15 Debt /DET/ Pay off your debt as quickly as you can. Fingerspell DEBT
16 Credit score /KRED-it SKOR/ A high credit score helps you get better loan rates. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Minimum payment /MIN-ih-mum PAY-ment/ Always pay at least the minimum payment on time. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Paycheck /PAY-chek/ The paycheck arrives every two weeks. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Direct deposit /dih-REKT dih-POZ-it/ Direct deposit puts money straight into your bank. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Savings /SAY-vingz/ Put ten percent of every paycheck into savings. Both hands scoop toward body (gathering)
21 Auto-pay /AW-toh-PAY/ Set up auto-pay so bills are never late. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Overdraft protection /OH-ver-draft proh-TEK-shun/ Overdraft protection stops your account from going negative. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Co-pay /KOH-pay/ Pay a co-pay each time you visit the doctor. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Subscription /sub-SKRIP-shun/ A streaming subscription charges a monthly fee. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Emergency fund /ih-MUR-jen-see FUND/ An emergency fund covers unexpected expenses. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Net pay /NET PAY/ Net pay is what you take home after taxes. Fingerspell or sign each word
