# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Sunday /SUN-day/ Sunday is the first day of the week. Fingerspell SUNDAY
2 Monday /MUN-day/ Monday is when the school week begins. Fingerspell MONDAY
3 Tuesday /TOOZ-day/ Kenji has music practice every Tuesday. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Wednesday /WENZ-day/ Wednesday is in the middle of the week. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Thursday /THURZ-day/ Aiko visits the library every Thursday. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Friday /FRY-day/ Friday is the last school day of the week! Fingerspell FRIDAY
7 Saturday /SAT-er-day/ Saturday is for rest and play. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 January /JAN-yoo-air-ee/ January is the first month of the new year. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 February /FEB-roo-air-ee/ February has only twenty-eight or twenty-nine days. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 March /MARCH/ Spring flowers start to bloom in March. Fingerspell MARCH
11 April /AY-prul/ April showers bring May flowers. Fingerspell APRIL
12 May /MAY/ May is the last full month of spring. Fingerspell MAY
13 June /JOON/ School usually ends in June. Fingerspell JUNE
14 July /joo-LY/ July is the hottest month of summer. Fingerspell JULY
15 August /AW-gust/ In August, families prepare for back-to-school. Fingerspell AUGUST
16 September /sep-TEM-ber/ September is the start of fall. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 October /ok-TOH-ber/ The leaves change color in October. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 November /noh-VEM-ber/ In November, families gather for Thanksgiving. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 December /dih-SEM-ber/ December is the last month of the year. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Today /too-DAY/ Today is a great day to learn something new. Both Y-hands drop in front of body
21 Tomorrow /too-MOR-oh/ Tomorrow we will practice our greetings. Thumb of A-hand bounces forward from cheek
22 Yesterday /YES-ter-day/ Yesterday Kenji learned ten new words. Y-hand or A-thumb touches cheek, then ear
23 Week /WEEK/ There are seven days in one week. Index moves across opposite flat palm (7 days)
24 Month /MUNTH/ A month has about thirty days. Index slides down opposite index once
25 Year /YEER/ A year has twelve months and 365 days. S-fists circle each other, then stack
26 Calendar /KAL-en-der/ Mark important dates on the calendar. C-hands mime turning page
27 Birthday /BURTH-day/ Today is Aiko's birthday! She is eleven. Middle finger touches chin, then chest
28 Holiday /HOL-ih-day/ A holiday is a special day of celebration. Thumbs at armpits, wiggle fingers (VACATION)
29 Date /DAYT/ What is the date today? It is March second. Fingerspell DATE
30 Season /SEE-zun/ There are four seasons: spring, summer, fall, winter. Fingerspell SEASON

Kenji's Tip:

"Sing the days of the week! Sunday, Monday, Tuesday... make it a song with a rhythm. Now sing the months! January, February, March... music makes memorization EASY."
