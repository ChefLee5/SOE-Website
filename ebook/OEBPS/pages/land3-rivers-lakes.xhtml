# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 River /RIV-er/ The river flows from mountains to sea. W-hand flows forward like water
2 Stream /STREEM/ A small stream runs through the forest. Fingerspell STREAM
3 Creek /KREEK/ The children played by the creek. Fingerspell CREEK
4 Lake /LAYK/ The lake is calm and perfect for fishing. L-hands trace circle shape
5 Pond /POND/ Ducks swim in the pond at the park. Fingerspell POND
6 Waterfall /WOT-er-fall/ The waterfall drops into the pool below. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Dam /DAM/ The dam holds back the water. Fingerspell DAM
8 Bridge /BRIJ/ Walk across the bridge to the other side. V-hand bridges under opposite forearm
9 Bank /BANK/ Sit on the river bank and watch the water. B-hand taps opposite palm repeatedly (stacking money)
10 Current /KUR-ent/ The river current carries leaves downstream. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Upstream /UP-streem/ Salmon swim upstream to lay their eggs. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Downstream /DOWN-streem/ The boat floated downstream. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Canal /kuh-NAL/ The canal connects two rivers. Fingerspell CANAL
14 Rapids /RAP-idz/ The white-water rapids are exciting. Fingerspell RAPIDS
15 Kayak /KY-ak/ Paddle the kayak down the calm river. Fingerspell KAYAK
16 Canoe /kuh-NOO/ Two people can paddle a canoe together. Fingerspell CANOE
17 Fishing rod /FISH-ing ROD/ Cast the fishing rod into the deep part. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Trout /TROWT/ The trout jumps out of the stream. Fingerspell TROUT
19 Frog /FROG/ The frog sits on a lily pad. Fist under chin, flick two fingers out
20 Turtle /TUR-tul/ The turtle suns itself on a log. A-hand under opposite cupped hand, thumb peeks
21 Beaver /BEE-ver/ The beaver builds a dam with sticks. Fingerspell BEAVER
22 Marsh /MARSH/ A marsh is a wetland with tall grasses. Fingerspell MARSH
23 Swamp /SWOMP/ The swamp is wet and muddy. Fingerspell SWAMP
24 Flood /FLUD/ Heavy rain caused a flood in the valley. W-hands rise up from below
25 Fresh water /FRESH WOT-er/ Fresh water from rivers is safe to drink. Fingerspell or sign each word
