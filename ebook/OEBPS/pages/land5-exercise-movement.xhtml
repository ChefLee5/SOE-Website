# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Exercise /EK-ser-syz/ Daily exercise keeps the body strong and the mind sharp. Both A-fists push up (like lifting weights)
2 Stretch /STRECH/ Stretch your muscles before and after exercise. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Warm-up /WORM-up/ A warm-up gets your body ready for harder movements. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Cool-down /KOOL-down/ A cool-down helps your heart rate return to normal. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Jump /JUMP/ Felix can jump higher than anyone in Vitalis. Fingerspell JUMP
6 Run /RUN/ Amara runs three laps around the training field. L-hands, index pulls on opposite thumb, move forward
7 Walk /WAWK/ Walking is a gentle exercise anyone can do. Both flat hands walk forward alternately
8 Skip /SKIP/ Felix skips along the path, swinging his arms. Fingerspell SKIP
9 Hop /HOP/ Hop on one foot, then switch to the other! Fingerspell HOP
10 Crawl /KRAWL/ The baby crawls quickly across the soft mat. Fingerspell CRAWL
11 Climb /KLYM/ Amara climbs the rope all the way to the top. Fingerspell CLIMB
12 Bend /BEND/ Bend at the waist and touch your toes. Fingerspell BEND
13 Twist /TWIST/ Twist your body to the left, then to the right. Fingerspell TWIST
14 Kick /KIK/ Felix kicks the ball across the field. Fingerspell KICK
15 Throw /THROH/ Throw the ball in a high arc. Fingerspell THROW
16 Catch /KACH/ Catch the ball with both hands. Fingerspell CATCH
17 Push /POOSH/ Push-ups make your arms and chest strong. Fingerspell PUSH
18 Pull /POOL/ Pull the resistance band slowly and steadily. Fingerspell PULL
19 Lift /LIFT/ Lift the light weight above your head. Fingerspell LIFT
20 Squat /SKWOT/ Squat down low and then stand back up. Fingerspell SQUAT
21 Balance /BAL-uns/ Stand on one foot to practice your balance. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Dance /DANS/ Amara loves to dance to the rhythm of the drums. Fingerspell DANCE
23 Yoga /YOH-guh/ Yoga combines breathing, stretching, and balance. Fingerspell YOGA
24 Breathe /BREETH/ Breathe in deeply through your nose, out through your mouth. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Inhale /in-HAYL/ Inhale slowly as you raise your arms. Fingerspell INHALE
26 Exhale /eks-HAYL/ Exhale gently as you lower your arms back down. Fingerspell EXHALE
27 Flexibility /flek-sih-BIL-ih-tee/ Stretching every day improves your flexibility. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Strength /STRENGTH/ Felix builds strength by doing exercises every day. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Endurance /en-DOOR-uns/ Running long distances builds endurance. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Coordination /koh-or-dih-NAY-shun/ Catching a ball while running requires coordination. Fingerspell or sign each word
