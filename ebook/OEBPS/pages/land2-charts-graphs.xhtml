# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Graph /GRAF/ A graph shows information visually. 4-hand moves along opposite palm stepping up
2 Bar graph /BAR GRAF/ The bar graph shows how many books each student read. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Pie chart /PY CHART/ The pie chart shows what percentage of students prefer each color. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Line graph /LYN GRAF/ The line graph shows temperature changes over a week. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Data /DAY-tuh/ Data is information collected from research or observation. D-hand moves along opposite palm (reading data)
6 Table /TAY-bul/ The table organizes data into rows and columns. Both forearms stack, form flat surface
7 Row /ROH/ Read across the row to find the answer. Fingerspell ROW
8 Column /KOL-um/ Each column represents a different category. Fingerspell COLUMN
9 Title /TY-tul/ The title of the graph tells you what it's about. Fingerspell TITLE
10 Label /LAY-bul/ Each axis needs a label so readers understand. Fingerspell LABEL
11 Axis /AK-sis/ The x-axis goes left to right, the y-axis goes up and down. Fingerspell AXIS
12 Survey /SUR-vay/ Octavia conducts a survey to find everyone's favorite fruit. Fingerspell SURVEY
13 Result /rih-ZULT/ The survey result shows that apples are most popular. Fingerspell RESULT
14 Compare /kum-PAIR/ Compare the two bars to see which is taller. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Increase /in-KREES/ The graph shows an increase in temperature. H-hand rises up opposite index
16 Decrease /dih-KREES/ There was a decrease in rainfall this month. H-hand drops down opposite index
17 Pattern /PAT-ern/ Can you find a pattern in these numbers? Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Predict /prih-DIKT/ Use the data to predict what will happen next. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Tally /TAL-ee/ Make a tally mark for each person's answer. Fingerspell TALLY
20 Chart /CHART/ The chart makes the information easy to understand. Index draws chart lines on opposite palm
