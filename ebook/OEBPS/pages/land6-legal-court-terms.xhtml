Scene 14: Legal & Court Terms

Ezra and Athena visit the Sophia Courthouse to learn the vocabulary of justice, rights, and the legal system.

🖼️ Illustration: Legal & Court Terms
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Legal & Court Terms' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Attorney /uh-TUR-nee/ An attorney is a person trained in the law. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Plaintiff /PLAYN-tif/ The plaintiff is the person who brings a case to court. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Defendant /dih-FEN-dunt/ The defendant is the person accused in court. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Evidence /EV-ih-dens/ Evidence includes facts and objects that prove a point. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Witness /WIT-nes/ A witness tells the court what they saw or heard. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Testimony /TES-tih-moh-nee/ The witness gives testimony under oath. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Verdict /VUR-dikt/ The verdict is the jury's decision — guilty or not guilty. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Sentence /SEN-tens/ The judge gives a sentence after a guilty verdict. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Fine /FYN/ A fine is money you pay as a penalty. Thumb of 5-hand taps chest
10 Bail /BAYL/ Bail is money paid to be released before trial. Fingerspell BAIL
11 Guilty /GIL-tee/ Guilty means the person committed the crime. Fingerspell GUILTY
12 Not guilty /NOT GIL-tee/ Not guilty means the person is found innocent. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Oath /OHTH/ An oath is a promise to tell the truth. Fingerspell OATH
14 Summons /SUM-unz/ A summons orders you to appear in court. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Subpoena /suh-PEE-nuh/ A subpoena requires a person to testify or bring documents. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Arraignment /uh-RAYN-ment/ The arraignment is the first court appearance. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Misdemeanor /mis-dih-MEE-ner/ A misdemeanor is a minor crime with lighter punishment. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Felony /FEL-uh-nee/ A felony is a serious crime with heavy punishment. Fingerspell FELONY
19 Probation /proh-BAY-shun/ Probation means supervision instead of prison time. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Legal aid /LEE-gul AYD/ Legal aid provides free lawyers for people who can't afford one. Fingerspell or sign each word
