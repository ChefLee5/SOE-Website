# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Ocean /OH-shun/ The ocean covers more than seventy percent of the Earth. Both hands make waves, push forward
2 Sea /SEE/ The sea is calm and blue near the shore. Fingerspell SEA
3 Wave /WAYV/ Big waves crash against the rocky cliff. Open hand waves side to side
4 Tide /TYD/ The tide comes in and goes out twice each day. Fingerspell TIDE
5 Current /KUR-ent/ A strong current carries water across the ocean. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Coral /KOR-ul/ Colorful coral grows in warm, shallow water. Fingerspell CORAL
7 Coral reef /KOR-ul REEF/ The coral reef is home to thousands of fish. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Fish /FISH/ Tropical fish have bright stripes and spots. Flat hand swims forward like fish
9 Shark /SHARK/ The shark glides through the deep, dark water. Fingerspell SHARK
10 Dolphin /DOL-fin/ The friendly dolphin leaps beside the boat. B-hand dips and rises forward
11 Whale /WAYL/ The blue whale is the largest animal on Earth. W-hand dips and rises (swimming)
12 Octopus /OK-tuh-pus/ The octopus has eight long arms. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Jellyfish /JEL-ee-fish/ The transparent jellyfish floats gently in the current. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Sea turtle /SEE TUR-tul/ The sea turtle swims thousands of miles to lay eggs. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Seahorse /SEE-hors/ The tiny seahorse wraps its tail around the seaweed. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Starfish /STAR-fish/ The starfish has five arms and moves slowly on the rocks. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Crab /KRAB/ The crab walks sideways along the sandy bottom. Both V-hands snap open and close (pincers)
18 Lobster /LOB-ster/ The lobster hides among the rocks at night. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Shrimp /SHRIMP/ Tiny shrimp swim in groups near the reef. Fingerspell SHRIMP
20 Clam /KLAM/ The clam opens and closes its shell for food. Fingerspell CLAM
21 Oyster /OY-ster/ An oyster can make a pearl inside its shell. Fingerspell OYSTER
22 Seaweed /SEE-weed/ Long strands of seaweed sway in the tide. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Sand /SAND/ The sand on the ocean floor is white and soft. Fingerspell SAND
24 Shell /SHEL/ Nerissa collects a beautiful shell from the beach. Fingerspell SHELL
25 Anchor /ANG-ker/ The anchor keeps the boat from drifting away. Fingerspell ANCHOR
26 Submarine /SUB-muh-reen/ A submarine can travel deep under the sea. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Scuba diver /SKOO-buh DY-ver/ The scuba diver explores the reef up close. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Snorkel /SNOR-kul/ Ronan breathes through a snorkel near the surface. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Wetsuit /WET-soot/ The wetsuit keeps you warm in cold ocean water. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Plankton /PLANGK-tun/ Tiny plankton is food for whales and small fish. Fingerspell or sign each word
31 Trench /TRENCH/ The ocean trench is the deepest part of the sea. Fingerspell TRENCH
32 Shipwreck /SHIP-rek/ An old shipwreck lies on the ocean floor. Fingerspell or sign each word
33 Reef /REEF/ The reef is alive with color and movement. Fingerspell REEF
34 Saltwater /SAWLT-waw-ter/ The ocean is made of saltwater. Fingerspell or sign each word
35 Marine /muh-REEN/ Marine life includes all plants and animals in the sea. Fingerspell MARINE
