# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Phone /FOHN/ Aiko calls her grandmother on the phone every week. Y-hand at ear (phone shape)
2 Cell phone /SEL FOHN/ A cell phone fits in your pocket. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Call /KAWL/ I will call you at five o'clock. Fingerspell CALL
4 Text message /TEKST MES-ij/ Send a text message if you can't call. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Internet /IN-ter-net/ The internet connects people around the world. 5-hands, middle fingers touch, twist
6 Website /WEB-syt/ Visit the school website for announcements. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Password /PAS-wurd/ Keep your password private and secure. P-hand taps opposite palm (SECRET + WORD)
8 Username /YOO-zer-naym/ Your username identifies you online. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Download /DOWN-lohd/ Download the file to your computer. Claw hand pulls down to S-fist
10 Upload /UP-lohd/ Upload your photo to the class album. S-fist pushes up, opens to claw
11 Search /SURCH/ Search the internet for the answer. Fingerspell SEARCH
12 Type /TYP/ Kenji types quickly on the keyboard. Fingerspell TYPE
13 Click /KLIK/ Click the blue button to open the page. Fingerspell CLICK
14 Screen /SKREEN/ The screen shows the video clearly. Both flat hands outline rectangle
15 Keyboard /KEE-bord/ The keyboard has letters, numbers, and symbols. Fingers mime typing
16 Mouse /MOWS/ Move the mouse to select the correct answer. Mime clicking mouse button
17 Printer /PRIN-ter/ The printer makes paper copies of documents. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Charger /CHAR-jer/ Plug in the charger when the battery is low. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Headphones /HED-fohnz/ Wear headphones to listen without disturbing others. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Speaker /SPEE-ker/ The speaker plays music for everyone to hear. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Camera /KAM-er-uh/ Aiko takes a photo with her camera. Both L-hands frame box at eye, click
22 Video /VID-ee-oh/ The video shows step-by-step instructions. 5-hand at temple, mime projecting
23 App /AP/ Open the dictionary app on your tablet. Fingerspell APP
24 Wi-Fi /WY-fy/ Connect to the Wi-Fi to go online. Fingerspell WI-FI
25 Letter /LET-er/ Kenji writes a letter to his pen pal in Land 4. Fingerspell LETTER
26 Envelope /EN-veh-lohp/ Put the letter in the envelope and seal it. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Stamp /STAMP/ Stick a stamp on the envelope before mailing. Fingerspell STAMP
28 Post office /POHST OF-is/ Take the package to the post office to mail it. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Newspaper /NOOZ-pay-per/ Grandfather reads the newspaper every morning. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Magazine /mag-uh-ZEEN/ The magazine has pictures and articles about nature. Fingerspell or sign each word

Aiko's Tip:

"Technology words are EVERYWHERE! The next time you use a phone, tablet, or computer, say the English word for what you are doing: 'I click. I type. I search. I download.' You are learning English every time you use technology!"
