Scene 1: Classroom Language & Commands

These are the words teachers say every day. Knowing them helps you follow along in any classroom.

🖼️ Illustration: Classroom Language & Commands
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Classroom Language & Commands' with numbered callout markers (1–30) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Attendance /uh-TEN-dents/ The teacher takes attendance every morning. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Assignment /uh-SYN-ment/ Turn in your assignment by Friday. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Homework /HOHM-wurk/ Do your homework before watching TV. Sign HOME + WORK
4 Quiz /KWIZ/ We have a spelling quiz tomorrow. Fingerspell QUIZ
5 Test /TEST/ Study hard for the math test. X-hands draw question marks in air, then move down
6 Grade /GRAYD/ She got a good grade on her report card. Fingerspell GRADE
7 Report card /rih-PORT KARD/ Report cards are sent home every quarter. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Permission slip /per-MISH-un SLIP/ Bring a signed permission slip for the field trip. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Absence /AB-sents/ An excused absence requires a note from a parent. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Tardy /TAR-dee/ Students who arrive late are marked tardy. Fingerspell TARDY
11 Raise your hand /RAYZ yor HAND/ Raise your hand before speaking in class. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Pay attention /PAY uh-TEN-shun/ Please pay attention to the instructions. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Turn to page /TURN too PAYJ/ Turn to page forty-seven in your textbook. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Copy /KOP-ee/ Copy the notes from the whiteboard. Fingerspell COPY
15 Repeat /rih-PEET/ Please repeat the question slowly. Fingerspell REPEAT
16 Pronounce /proh-NOWNS/ How do you pronounce this word? Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Spell /SPEL/ Can you spell your last name for me? Fingerspell SPELL
18 Define /dih-FYN/ Define the word in your own words. Fingerspell DEFINE
19 Explain /ek-SPLAYN/ Can you explain the answer in English? Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Translate /TRANS-layt/ Translate the sentence into your home language. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Review /rih-VYOO/ Let's review yesterday's vocabulary. Fingerspell REVIEW
22 Practice /PRAK-tis/ Practice reading aloud for ten minutes. A-hand brushes back and forth on opposite index
23 Correct /kuh-REKT/ Check your work and correct any mistakes. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Submit /sub-MIT/ Submit your essay by the end of the day. Fingerspell SUBMIT
25 Participate /par-TIS-ih-payt/ Everyone must participate in the group activity. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Enroll /en-ROHL/ Enroll in the ESL class at the community center. Fingerspell ENROLL
27 Register /REJ-ih-ster/ Register for classes before the deadline. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Tutor /TOO-ter/ A tutor can help you with reading and writing. Fingerspell TUTOR
29 Conference /KON-fer-ens/ The parent-teacher conference is next Thursday. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Graduate /GRAJ-oo-ayt/ She will graduate from the program in June. Fingerspell or sign each word
