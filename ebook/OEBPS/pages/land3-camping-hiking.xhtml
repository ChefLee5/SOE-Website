# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Tent /TENT/ Set up the tent before dark. Fingerspell TENT
2 Sleeping bag /SLEEP-ing BAG/ Zip up inside your sleeping bag to stay warm. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Campfire /KAMP-fyr/ Gather around the campfire and tell stories. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Flashlight /FLASH-lyt/ Shine the flashlight to see in the dark. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Backpack /BAK-pak/ Carry food and water in your backpack. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Trail /TRAYL/ Follow the trail through the woods. Fingerspell TRAIL
7 Hike /HYK/ We hiked five miles to the mountain top. Fingerspell HIKE
8 Compass /KUM-pus/ Use the compass to navigate the forest. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Binoculars /buh-NOK-yoo-lerz/ Look through the binoculars to see the eagle. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Firewood /FYR-wood/ Collect firewood for the campfire. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Marshmallow /MARSH-mal-oh/ Roast a marshmallow over the campfire. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Canoe /kuh-NOO/ Paddle the canoe across the calm lake. Fingerspell CANOE
13 Cliff /KLIF/ Be careful near the edge of the cliff. Fingerspell CLIFF
14 Cabin /KAB-in/ The log cabin sits at the forest edge. Fingerspell CABIN
15 Wildlife /WYLD-lyf/ We saw a lot of wildlife on the trail. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Map /MAP/ Study the map before choosing your trail. M-hands trace on flat surface
17 Boots /BOOTS/ Wear sturdy boots on the rocky trail. B-hands stamp alternately
18 Insect repellent /IN-sekt rih-PEL-ent/ Spray insect repellent to keep mosquitoes away. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 First aid kit /FURST AYD KIT/ Always bring a first aid kit camping. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Constellation /kon-stuh-LAY-shun/ Find the Big Dipper in the night sky. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Bear /BAIR/ Store food properly so bears cannot find it. Crossed arms scratch chest
22 Deer /DEER/ A deer quietly crossed the trail. Open 5-hands at temples (antlers)
23 Owl /OWL/ The owl hoots in the dark forest. Fingerspell OWL
24 Nature /NAY-chur/ Nature is the greatest teacher of all. N-hand circles then settles (NATURAL)
25 Wilderness /WIL-der-nes/ The wilderness is untouched natural land. Fingerspell or sign each word
