# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Insect /IN-sekt/ An insect has six legs and three body parts. Fingerspell INSECT
2 Ant /ANT/ The ant carries a crumb ten times its own weight. Fingerspell ANT
3 Bee /BEE/ The bee buzzes from flower to flower collecting pollen. Fingerspell BEE
4 Butterfly /BUT-er-fly/ The colorful butterfly was once a caterpillar. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Caterpillar /KAT-er-pil-er/ The caterpillar eats leaves before spinning a cocoon. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Ladybug /LAY-dee-bug/ The red ladybug has small black spots. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Dragonfly /DRAG-un-fly/ The dragonfly hovers over the pond. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Grasshopper /GRAS-hop-er/ The grasshopper jumps through the tall grass. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Cricket /KRIK-it/ The cricket chirps loudly on summer nights. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Spider /SPY-der/ The spider is not an insect — it has eight legs. Fingerspell SPIDER
11 Web /WEB/ The spider's web sparkles with morning dew. Fingerspell WEB
12 Mosquito /mus-KEE-toh/ The mosquito buzzes near your ear at night. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Fly /FLY/ A fly landed on the picnic table. Y-hand with index, soar through air
14 Beetle /BEE-tul/ The shiny beetle crawls along the branch. Fingerspell BEETLE
15 Worm /WURM/ The earthworm helps make the soil healthy. Fingerspell WORM
16 Cocoon /kuh-KOON/ Inside the cocoon, the caterpillar transforms. Fingerspell COCOON
17 Hive /HYV/ Thousands of bees live together in a hive. Fingerspell HIVE
18 Honey /HUN-ee/ Sweet honey comes from the bees' hard work. Fingerspell HONEY
19 Colony /KOL-uh-nee/ The ant colony works together underground. Fingerspell COLONY
20 Pest /PEST/ A pest is an insect that damages crops. Fingerspell PEST
21 Pollinate /POL-ih-nayt/ Bees pollinate flowers so that fruit can grow. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Larvae /LAR-vee/ The larvae of the butterfly look like tiny worms. Fingerspell LARVAE
23 Antenna /an-TEN-uh/ The butterfly uses its antenna to sense the world. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Wing /WING/ Each wing of the butterfly is painted with color. Fingerspell WING
25 Magnifying glass /MAG-nih-fy-ing GLAS/ Use a magnifying glass to see the tiny details. Fingerspell or sign each word
