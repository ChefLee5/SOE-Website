# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Lion /LY-un/ The lion rests under the shade of a big tree. Claw hand rakes back over head (mane)
2 Tiger /TY-ger/ An orange tiger has beautiful black stripes. Fingerspell TIGER
3 Bear /BAIR/ The brown bear catches fish in the cold river. Crossed arms scratch chest
4 Wolf /WOOLF/ The wolf howls at the full moon in Terrasol. Fingerspell WOLF
5 Fox /FOKS/ A clever red fox hides behind the bushes. Fingerspell FOX
6 Deer /DEER/ The gentle deer drinks water from the stream. Open 5-hands at temples (antlers)
7 Elk /ELK/ The elk has huge antlers on top of its head. Fingerspell ELK
8 Moose /MOOS/ A moose stands in the shallow lake eating plants. Fingerspell MOOSE
9 Zebra /ZEE-bruh/ Each zebra has its own unique pattern of stripes. Fingerspell ZEBRA
10 Giraffe /juh-RAF/ The giraffe stretches its long neck to eat leaves. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Elephant /EL-uh-funt/ The elephant sprays water with its trunk. Trace trunk from nose curving down
12 Rhinoceros /ry-NOS-er-us/ The rhinoceros has a strong horn on its nose. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Hippopotamus /hip-oh-POT-uh-mus/ The hippopotamus stays cool in the muddy river. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Gorilla /guh-RIL-uh/ The gorilla beats its chest in the jungle. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Monkey /MUNG-kee/ The monkey swings from branch to branch. Scratch sides of body going up
16 Chimpanzee /chim-pan-ZEE/ The chimpanzee uses a stick to find food. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Kangaroo /kang-guh-ROO/ The kangaroo carries its baby in a pouch. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Koala /koh-AH-luh/ The koala sleeps in its favorite eucalyptus tree. Fingerspell KOALA
19 Panda /PAN-duh/ The panda eats bamboo all day long. Fingerspell PANDA
20 Leopard /LEP-erd/ The leopard has spots and runs very fast. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Cheetah /CHEE-tuh/ The cheetah is the fastest animal on land. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Hyena /hy-EE-nuh/ The hyena laughs loudly across the savanna. Fingerspell HYENA
23 Crocodile /KROK-uh-dyl/ The crocodile waits quietly in the water. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Alligator /AL-ih-gay-ter/ The alligator has a wide, flat snout. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Snake /SNAYK/ The snake slithers silently through the tall grass. V-hand slithers forward (like fangs)
26 Lizard /LIZ-erd/ A green lizard suns itself on a warm rock. Fingerspell LIZARD

35 Flamingo /fluh-MING-oh/ The pink flamingo stands on one leg. Fingerspell or sign each word
36 Bat /BAT/ The bat hangs upside down in a dark cave. Fingerspell BAT
37 Squirrel /SKWIR-ul/ The squirrel hides acorns in the ground for winter. Fingerspell or sign each word
38 Raccoon /ra-KOON/ The raccoon has a black mask around its eyes. Fingerspell or sign each word
39 Skunk /SKUNGK/ The skunk has a white stripe down its back. Fingerspell SKUNK
