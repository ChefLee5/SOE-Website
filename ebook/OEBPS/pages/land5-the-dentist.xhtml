# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Dentist /DEN-tist/ The dentist examines Amara's teeth carefully. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Dental hygienist /DEN-tul hy-JEE-nist/ The dental hygienist cleans Amara's teeth with special tools. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Dental chair /DEN-tul CHAIR/ Amara leans back in the comfortable dental chair. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Tooth /TOOTH/ Each tooth has a different shape and job. Fingerspell TOOTH
5 Molar /MOH-ler/ The flat molar in the back grinds food into small pieces. Fingerspell MOLAR
6 Canine /KAY-nyn/ The pointed canine tooth tears food apart. Fingerspell CANINE
7 Incisor /in-SY-zer/ The sharp incisor at the front bites into an apple. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Gum /GUM/ Healthy gums are pink and firm around each tooth. Fingerspell GUM
9 Cavity /KAV-ih-tee/ A cavity is a small hole caused by sugar and plaque. Fingerspell CAVITY
10 Filling /FIL-ing/ The dentist puts a filling in the cavity to fix it. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Crown /KROWN/ A crown covers and protects a damaged tooth. Fingerspell CROWN
12 Braces /BRAY-siz/ Braces straighten teeth slowly over time. Fingerspell BRACES
13 Retainer /rih-TAY-ner/ A retainer keeps teeth in their new position after braces. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Dental X-ray /DEN-tul EKS-ray/ The dental X-ray shows what's happening under the gums. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Fluoride /FLOR-eyed/ Fluoride treatment makes teeth stronger. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Plaque /PLAK/ Plaque is a sticky film of bacteria on the teeth. Fingerspell PLAQUE
17 Floss /FLOS/ Use floss to clean between your teeth every day. Fingerspell FLOSS
18 Toothbrush /TOOTH-brush/ Amara brushes with a soft toothbrush twice a day. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Toothpaste /TOOTH-payst/ Mint toothpaste makes your mouth feel fresh and clean. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Mouthwash /MOWTH-wash/ Swish mouthwash around for thirty seconds, then spit. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Dental mirror /DEN-tul MIR-er/ The dental mirror lets the dentist see the back teeth. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Drill /DRIL/ The small drill removes the decayed part of the tooth. Fingerspell DRILL
23 Numbing gel /NUM-ing JEL/ Numbing gel prevents pain during the procedure. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Rinse /RINS/ Rinse your mouth with water after the cleaning. Fingerspell RINSE
25 Wisdom tooth /WIZ-dum TOOTH/ Wisdom teeth usually appear in the late teens. Fingerspell or sign each word
