9 Order /OR-der/ Put the numbers in order from smallest to largest. Fingerspell ORDER
10 Classify /KLAS-ih-fy/ Classify the objects as living or nonliving. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Compare /kum-PAIR/ Compare the two circles — which is bigger? Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Contrast /kun-TRAST/ Contrast the summer and winter temperatures. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Cause /KAWZ/ The cause of the flood was heavy rain. Fingerspell CAUSE

16 Reason /REE-zun/ Give a reason for your answer. Fingerspell REASON
17 Evidence /EV-ih-dents/ Show evidence from the text to support your answer. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Conclusion /kun-KLOO-zhun/ The conclusion summarizes what you learned. Fingerspell or sign each word
