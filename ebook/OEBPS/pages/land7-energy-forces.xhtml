# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Energy /EN-er-jee/ Energy is the ability to do work or cause change. E-hands flex at biceps (STRONG + E)
2 Heat /HEET/ Heat energy makes things warmer. Fingerspell HEAT
3 Light /LYT/ Light energy lets us see the world around us. Both flat hands rise up easily
4 Sound /SOWND/ Sound energy travels in waves through the air. Fingerspell SOUND
5 Electricity /ee-lek-TRIS-ih-tee/ Electricity powers lights, computers, and machines. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Solar energy /SOH-ler EN-er-jee/ Solar energy comes from the sun. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Wind energy /WIND EN-er-jee/ Wind energy is captured by windmills and turbines. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Battery /BAT-er-ee/ A battery stores electrical energy. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Magnet /MAG-net/ A magnet attracts objects made of iron or steel. Fingerspell MAGNET
10 Force /FORS/ A force is a push or a pull on an object. Fingerspell FORCE
11 Friction /FRIK-shun/ Friction slows things down when they rub together. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Motion /MOH-shun/ An object in motion keeps moving until a force stops it. Fingerspell MOTION
13 Speed /SPEED/ Speed is how fast something moves. Both S-hands flick to 5-hands quickly
14 Acceleration /ak-sel-er-AY-shun/ Acceleration is the rate of getting faster. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Weight /WAYT/ Weight is the measure of gravity pulling on an object. Fingerspell WEIGHT
16 Mass /MAS/ Mass is the amount of matter in an object. Fingerspell MASS
17 Matter /MAT-er/ Everything you can touch or see is made of matter. Fingerspell MATTER
18 Solid /SOL-id/ Ice is water in its solid form. Fingerspell SOLID
19 Liquid /LIK-wid/ Water is a liquid that flows and takes the shape of its container. Fingerspell LIQUID
20 Gas /GAS/ Steam is water in its gas form. Fingerspell GAS
21 Evaporation /ih-vap-or-AY-shun/ Evaporation turns liquid water into water vapor. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Condensation /kon-den-SAY-shun/ Condensation turns water vapor back into liquid drops. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Precipitation /prih-sip-ih-TAY-shun/ Rain, snow, and hail are all forms of precipitation. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Water cycle /WAW-ter SY-kul/ The water cycle is how water moves from Earth to sky and back. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Temperature /TEM-per-uh-chur/ Temperature measures how hot or cold something is. Index slides up/down opposite index (thermometer)
