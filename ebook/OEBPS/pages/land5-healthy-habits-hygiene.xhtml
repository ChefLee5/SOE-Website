# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Hygiene /HY-jeen/ Good hygiene keeps your body clean and healthy. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Wash /WOSH/ Wash your hands with soap for twenty seconds. Fists rub together (scrubbing)
3 Soap /SOHP/ Soap kills germs on your skin. Bend fingers on opposite palm
4 Shampoo /sham-POO/ Amara works the shampoo into her hair and rinses it out. Both claw hands scrub on head
5 Conditioner /kun-DISH-un-er/ Conditioner makes hair soft and easy to comb. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Shower /SHOW-er/ Felix takes a warm shower every morning. 5-hand above head opens/closes (water spraying)
7 Bath /BATH/ A long bath with warm water relaxes tired muscles. Both A-fists scrub up and down chest
8 Towel /TOW-ul/ Dry off with a clean towel after every shower. Flat hands rub back and forth at sides
9 Deodorant /dee-OH-der-unt/ Deodorant keeps you smelling fresh all day. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Toothbrush /TOOTH-brush/ Brush your teeth for two full minutes. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Toothpaste /TOOTH-payst/ Squeeze a pea-sized amount of toothpaste on the brush. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Floss /FLOS/ Floss reaches places the toothbrush cannot. Fingerspell FLOSS
13 Comb /KOHM/ Amara runs a wide-tooth comb through her hair. Fingerspell COMB
14 Brush /BRUSH/ A soft brush detangles hair without pulling. Mime brushing teeth
15 Nail clipper /NAYL KLIP-er/ Use a nail clipper to trim your fingernails and toenails. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Sunscreen /SUN-skreen/ Apply sunscreen before going outside in the sun. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Lotion /LOH-shun/ Lotion keeps skin soft and prevents dryness. Fingerspell LOTION
18 Tissue /TISH-oo/ Cover your nose with a tissue when you sneeze. Fingerspell TISSUE
19 Hand sanitizer /HAND SAN-ih-ty-zer/ Use hand sanitizer when soap and water aren't available. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Sleep /SLEEP/ Eight to ten hours of sleep helps children grow strong. Open hand draws down face, eyes close
21 Rest /REST/ Your body needs rest after a long day of activity. Arms cross over chest (resting)
22 Stretch /STRECH/ Stretch your arms and legs when you wake up. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Posture /POS-chur/ Good posture means sitting and standing up straight. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Hydrate /HY-drayt/ Drink water throughout the day to stay hydrated. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Germs /JURMZ/ Germs are tiny organisms that can make you sick. Fingerspell GERMS
26 Immune system /ih-MYOON SIS-tem/ The immune system protects the body from illness. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Wellness /WEL-nes/ Wellness means taking care of your body AND your mind. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Routine /roo-TEEN/ A daily routine helps you build healthy habits. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Laundry /LAWN-dree/ Put dirty clothes in the laundry basket to be washed. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Clean /KLEEN/ A clean home keeps everyone in the family healthy. Flat hand slides across opposite palm
