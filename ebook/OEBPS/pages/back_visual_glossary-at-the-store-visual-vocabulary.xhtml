Every shopping trip is a vocabulary lesson. Name what you see!

# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Cart /KART/ Put your groceries in the shopping cart. Fingerspell CART
2 Basket /BAS-kit/ Use a basket if you only need a few items. Fingerspell BASKET
3 Aisle /YL/ The cereal is in aisle five. Fingerspell AISLE
4 Checkout /CHEK-owt/ Go to the checkout counter to pay. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Receipt /rih-SEET/ Keep your receipt in case you need to return something. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Coupon /KOO-pon/ Use the coupon to save two dollars. Fingerspell COUPON
7 Cashier /kash-EER/ The cashier scans your items and tells you the total. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Sale /SAYL/ These shoes are on sale — twenty percent off. Fingerspell SALE
9 Brand /BRAND/ Which brand of toothpaste do you prefer? Fingerspell BRAND
10 Label /LAY-bul/ Read the label to check the ingredients. Fingerspell LABEL
11 Expiration /ek-spih-RAY-shun/ Check the expiration date before buying milk. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Price tag /PRYS TAG/ The price tag shows how much the item costs. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Refund /REE-fund/ Ask for a refund if the product is defective. Fingerspell REFUND
14 Exchange /eks-CHAYNJ/ You can exchange the shirt for a different size. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Barcode /BAR-kohd/ The cashier scans the barcode to ring up the item. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Produce section /PROH-doos SEK-shun/ Fresh fruits and vegetables are in the produce section. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Frozen food /FROH-zen FOOD/ Pizza and ice cream are in the frozen food section. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Dairy /DAIR-ee/ Milk, cheese, and yogurt are dairy products. Fingerspell DAIRY
19 Bakery /BAY-ker-ee/ Fresh bread is available at the bakery counter. Fingerspell BAKERY
20 Deli /DEL-ee/ Order sliced turkey at the deli counter. Fingerspell DELI
21 Bag /BAG/ Would you like paper or plastic bags? Fingerspell BAG
22 Scale /SKAYL/ Weigh the apples on the scale. Both flat hands balance like scale
23 Sample /SAM-pul/ Try a free sample before you buy. Fingerspell SAMPLE
24 Register /REJ-ih-ster/ The register shows the total amount due. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Manager /MAN-ih-jer/ Ask the manager if you have a complaint. Fingerspell or sign each word
