# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 A /AY/ The letter A starts the alphabet. Fist with thumb at side
2 B /BEE/ B is for Book, Bus, and Banana. Four fingers up, thumb across palm
3 C /SEE/ C is for Cat, City, and Cup. Curved hand like the letter C
4 D /DEE/ D is for Dog, Day, and Door. Index up, other fingers touch thumb
5 E /EE/ E is for Eat, Eye, and Energy. Fingertips curled to thumb, thumb tucked
6 F /EF/ F is for Family, Fish, and Flower. Three fingers up, index and thumb circle
7 G /JEE/ G is for Good, Green, and Garden. Index and thumb point sideways
8 H /AYCH/ H is for Happy, Home, and Help. Index and middle point sideways, stacked
9 I /EYE/ I is for I, Ice, and Important. Pinky finger up, rest closed
10 J /JAY/ J is for Juice, Job, and January. Pinky up, trace letter J in air
11 K /KAY/ K is for Kitchen, Key, and Kind. Index and middle up, thumb between
12 L /EL/ L is for Love, Lake, and Library. L-shape: index up, thumb out
13 M /EM/ M is for Mother, Map, and Music. Three fingers over thumb, fingers down
