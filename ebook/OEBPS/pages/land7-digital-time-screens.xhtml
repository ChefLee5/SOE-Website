# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Digital clock /DIJ-ih-tul KLOK/ The digital clock shows numbers instead of hands. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Timer /TY-mer/ Set the timer for ten minutes. T-hand taps wrist
3 Alarm /uh-LARM/ The alarm rings at six thirty every morning. Index taps opposite palm rapidly (ringing)
4 Countdown /KOWNT-down/ The countdown starts at ten, nine, eight... Fingers count down 5-4-3-2-1
5 Stopwatch /STOP-wotch/ Use the stopwatch to time the race. Both S-hands: one taps other (START + WATCH)
6 Snooze /SNOOZ/ Press snooze for five more minutes of sleep. Fingerspell SNOOZE
7 Reminder /rih-MYN-der/ Set a reminder for your doctor appointment. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Calendar app /KAL-en-der AP/ Add events to your calendar app. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Notification /noh-tih-fih-KAY-shun/ A notification pops up on your screen. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Screen time /SKREEN TYM/ Limit screen time to two hours per day. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Streaming /STREEM-ing/ Streaming a movie takes about two hours. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Loading /LOH-ding/ The page is still loading. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Buffering /BUF-er-ing/ The video keeps buffering because the internet is slow. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Download /DOWN-lohd/ Download the file in about three minutes. Claw hand pulls down to S-fist
15 Upload /UP-lohd/ Upload the photo to the cloud. S-fist pushes up, opens to claw
16 Update /up-DAYT/ The software update takes fifteen minutes. Fingerspell UPDATE
17 Real-time /REEL-tym/ GPS gives real-time directions. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Instant /IN-stunt/ The message was delivered in an instant. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Lag /LAG/ A lag means there is a delay in the connection. Fingerspell LAG
20 Sync /SINK/ Sync your watch with your phone for accuracy. Fingerspell SYNC
21 Timestamp /TYM-stamp/ The timestamp shows when the photo was taken. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Log in /LOG IN/ Log in to your account to check the schedule. Fingerspell LOG IN
23 Schedule /SKEJ-ool/ Schedule the meeting for tomorrow at two. 4-hand slides down, then across opposite palm
24 Time zone /TYM ZOHN/ Adjust for the time zone when calling overseas. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 24-hour clock /TWEN-tee-for OW-er KLOK/ The military uses a 24-hour clock. Fingerspell or sign each word
