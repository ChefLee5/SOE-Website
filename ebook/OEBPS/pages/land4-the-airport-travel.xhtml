# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Airport /AIR-port/ The airport is where airplanes take off and land. Y-hand with index: airplane flies
2 Terminal /TUR-mih-nul/ Go to Terminal B for your flight. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Gate /GAYT/ Your boarding gate is number twelve. Fingerspell GATE
4 Ticket /TIK-it/ Show your ticket at the gate before boarding. V-hand punches into opposite palm
5 Boarding pass /BOR-ding PAS/ The boarding pass shows your seat number. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Passport /PAS-port/ You need a passport to travel to another country. P-hand opens like small book
7 Visa /VEE-zuh/ A visa gives you permission to enter a foreign country. V-hand stamps opposite palm
8 Customs /KUS-tumz/ At customs, officers check what you bring into the country. C-hand stamps opposite palm
9 Immigration /im-ih-GRAY-shun/ Immigration officers check your passport at the border. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Luggage /LUG-ij/ Ronan places his luggage on the conveyor belt. Mime carrying suitcase (hand grips, arm curls)
11 Suitcase /SOOT-kays/ Pack your suitcase the night before the trip. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Carry-on /KAIR-ee-ON/ A carry-on bag fits under the seat or in the overhead bin. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Baggage claim /BAG-ij KLAYM/ Pick up your bags at the baggage claim area. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Security /sih-KYOOR-ih-tee/ Go through security before you reach the gates. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Check-in /CHEK-in/ Check-in at the airline counter or use the kiosk. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Departure /dih-PAR-chur/ The departure time is ten fifteen A.M. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Arrival /uh-RY-vul/ The arrival time is two thirty P.M. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Delay /dih-LAY/ The flight has a two-hour delay due to weather. Fingerspell DELAY
19 Flight /FLYT/ Flight number 247 is now boarding. Fingerspell FLIGHT
20 Pilot /PY-lut/ The pilot flies the airplane safely. Fingerspell PILOT
21 Flight attendant /FLYT uh-TEN-dunt/ The flight attendant shows you how to buckle your seatbelt. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Runway /RUN-way/ The airplane taxis down the runway before takeoff. Fingerspell RUNWAY
23 Takeoff /TAYK-off/ The airplane lifts up during takeoff. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Landing /LAND-ing/ The smooth landing means the flight is over. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Seat belt /SEET BELT/ Fasten your seat belt when the light turns on. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Overhead bin /OH-ver-hed BIN/ Store your carry-on in the overhead bin. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Aisle seat /EYL SEET/ Nerissa prefers the aisle seat for easy access. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Window seat /WIN-doh SEET/ Ronan picks the window seat to see the clouds. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Destination /des-tih-NAY-shun/ Their destination is two thousand miles away. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Round trip /ROWND TRIP/ A round trip ticket includes going and coming back. Fingerspell or sign each word
31 One-way /WUN-WAY/ A one-way ticket is for travel in one direction only. Fingerspell or sign each word
32 Connecting flight /kuh-NEK-ting FLYT/ A connecting flight requires a change of planes. Fingerspell or sign each word
33 Layover /LAY-oh-ver/ The layover is three hours in the middle city. Fingerspell or sign each word
34 Turbulence /TUR-byoo-lens/ Turbulence makes the plane shake and bump. Fingerspell or sign each word
35 International /in-ter-NASH-un-ul/ An international flight goes to another country. Fingerspell or sign each word
