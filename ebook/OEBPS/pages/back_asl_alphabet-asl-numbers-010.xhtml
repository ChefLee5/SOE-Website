# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Zero /ZEER-oh/ Zero means nothing or none. O-hand shape
2 One /WUN/ One is a single item. Index finger up
3 Two /TOO/ Two means a pair. Index and middle up
4 Three /THREE/ Three comes after two. Thumb, index, and middle up
5 Four /FOR/ Four fingers but no thumb. Four fingers up, thumb folded
6 Five /FYV/ Five is a whole hand. All five fingers spread open
7 Six /SIKS/ Six is five plus one. Pinky meets thumb, three fingers up
8 Seven /SEV-en/ Seven days in a week. Ring finger meets thumb, three up
9 Eight /AYT/ Eight legs on a spider. Middle finger meets thumb, three up
10 Nine /NYN/ Nine is almost ten. Index meets thumb, four fingers up
11 Ten /TEN/ Ten fingers, ten toes. A-hand (thumb up), shake
