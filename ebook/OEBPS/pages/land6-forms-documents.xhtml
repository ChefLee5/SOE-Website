Scene 11: Forms & Documents

Living in Sophia means knowing your paperwork. Ezra and Athena practice filling out the forms that matter most.

🖼️ Illustration: Forms & Documents
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Forms & Documents' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Form /FORM/ Fill out the form with your personal information. Fingerspell FORM
2 Signature /SIG-nuh-chur/ Sign your signature on the line at the bottom. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Social Security number /SOH-shul sih-KYOOR-ih-tee NUM-ber/ Your Social Security number is private — never share it. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Birth certificate /BURTH sur-TIF-ih-kit/ A birth certificate proves where and when you were born. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Driver's license /DRY-verz LY-sens/ A driver's license is a common form of photo ID. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 State ID /STAYT EYE-DEE/ A state ID works as identification if you don't drive. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Passport /PAS-port/ A passport is required for international travel. P-hand opens like small book
8 Green card /GREEN KARD/ A green card allows a person to live and work in the U.S. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Work permit /WURK PUR-mit/ A work permit gives you legal permission to be employed. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Tax return /TAKS rih-TURN/ File your tax return by April fifteenth. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 W-2 form /DUB-ul-yoo TOO FORM/ The W-2 form shows how much you earned last year. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Application /ap-lih-KAY-shun/ Fill out the application completely and neatly. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Notary /NOH-tuh-ree/ A notary stamps official documents to verify them. Fingerspell NOTARY
14 Photocopy /FOH-toh-kop-ee/ Make a photocopy of every important document. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Translate /TRANS-layt/ Ask someone to translate the form into your language. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Print /PRINT/ Print your name clearly in capital letters. Fingerspell PRINT
17 Date of birth /DAYT UV BURTH/ Write your date of birth as month, day, year. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Marital status /MAIR-ih-tul STAT-us/ Marital status means single, married, or divorced. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Emergency contact /ih-MUR-jen-see KON-takt/ List an emergency contact person and their phone number. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Legal name /LEE-gul NAYM/ Write your full legal name as it appears on your ID. Fingerspell or sign each word
📋

Ezra's Tip:

"Practice filling out forms! Write your full name, your address, your date of birth, and your phone number. These four things appear on EVERY form."
