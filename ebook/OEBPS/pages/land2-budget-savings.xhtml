# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Budget /BUJ-it/ Make a budget to track your money. B-hands on opposite palm (MONEY + PLAN)
2 Savings /SAY-vingz/ Put money in savings every month. Both hands scoop toward body (gathering)
3 Income /IN-kum/ Income is the money you earn. Hand scoops money toward body
4 Expenses /ek-SPEN-sez/ Rent and food are monthly expenses. Hand pushes money away from body
5 Afford /uh-FORD/ Can we afford a new computer? Fingerspell AFFORD
6 Borrow /BOR-oh/ She borrowed money to pay the bill. Fingerspell BORROW
7 Lend /LEND/ Will you lend me ten dollars? Fingerspell LEND
8 Owe /OH/ I owe you five dollars from lunch. Fingerspell OWE
9 Debt /DET/ Pay off your debt quickly. Fingerspell DEBT
10 Interest /IN-ter-est/ The bank pays interest on savings. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Invest /in-VEST/ Invest your money in stocks or bonds. Fingerspell INVEST
12 Profit /PROF-it/ The store made a thousand dollar profit. Fingerspell PROFIT
13 Loss /LOS/ The business had a loss last quarter. Fingerspell LOSS
14 Tax /TAKS/ Sales tax adds eight percent. T-hand or X-hand taps opposite palm
15 Tip /TIP/ Leave a tip for the waiter. Fingerspell TIP
16 Discount /DIS-kownt/ Use this coupon for ten percent discount. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Free /FREE/ Free admission on Sundays. F-hands cross, then spread apart
18 Charge /CHARJ/ Is there a charge for parking? Fingerspell CHARGE
19 Fee /FEE/ The doctor charges a co-pay fee. Fingerspell FEE
20 Rent /RENT/ Rent is due on the first. Fingerspell RENT
21 Bill /BIL/ Pay the electric bill on time. Fingerspell BILL
22 Receipt /rih-SEET/ Keep the receipt for tax records. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Wallet /WOL-it/ Put your money in your wallet. Fingerspell WALLET
24 Piggy bank /PIG-ee BANK/ Save coins in a piggy bank. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Goal /GOHL/ Set a savings goal and work toward it. Fingerspell GOAL
