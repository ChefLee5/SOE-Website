# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language

4 Turn /TURN/ Turn at the next corner. Fingerspell TURN

12 Near /NEER/ The restaurant is near the train station. Fingerspell NEAR

14 Above /uh-BUV/ The apartment is above the shop. Fingerspell ABOVE
15 Below /bih-LOH/ The parking garage is below the mall. Fingerspell BELOW
16 Inside /in-SYD/ Go inside the building and turn left. Fingerspell INSIDE
17 Outside /owt-SYD/ Wait outside the entrance for the group. Fingerspell or sign each word
