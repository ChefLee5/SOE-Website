# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Bus /BUS/ Take the bus to school every morning. B-hand mimes long vehicle
2 Subway /SUB-way/ The subway runs underground through the city. Y-hand slides under opposite flat palm
3 Train /TRAYN/ The train travels between cities. H-hands slide along each other (tracks)
4 Station /STAY-shun/ Wait at the station for the next train. S-hands outline building
5 Platform /PLAT-form/ Stand on the platform until the train stops. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Ticket /TIK-it/ Buy a ticket before boarding the train. V-hand punches into opposite palm
7 Fare /FAIR/ The bus fare is two dollars. Fingerspell FARE
8 Transfer /TRANS-fer/ Transfer to the blue line at Main Street. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Schedule /SKEJ-ool/ Check the bus schedule for departure times. 4-hand slides down, then across opposite palm
10 Route /ROOT/ This bus route goes to the hospital. Fingerspell ROUTE
11 Stop /STOP/ Pull the cord to request a stop. Flat hand chops down on opposite palm
12 Driver /DRY-ver/ The bus driver opens the doors. Fingerspell DRIVER
13 Passenger /PAS-en-jer/ Each passenger must pay the fare. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Seat /SEET/ Give your seat to an elderly passenger. Fingerspell SEAT
15 Standing room /STAN-ding ROOM/ The bus is full — standing room only. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Rush hour /RUSH OW-er/ Trains are crowded during rush hour. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Commute /kuh-MYOOT/ My daily commute takes forty-five minutes. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Metro /MET-roh/ The metro is fast and goes everywhere. Fingerspell METRO
19 Trolley /TROL-ee/ The trolley runs on tracks through downtown. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Ferry /FAIR-ee/ The ferry carries people across the river. F-hands carry across space
21 Terminal /TUR-mih-nul/ Meet me at the bus terminal downtown. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Pass /PAS/ Buy a monthly pass to save money. Fingerspell PASS
23 Delay /dih-LAY/ There is a ten-minute delay on the red line. Fingerspell DELAY
24 On time /ON TYM/ The bus arrived on time today. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Accessible /ak-SES-ih-bul/ All buses are accessible for wheelchairs. Fingerspell or sign each word
