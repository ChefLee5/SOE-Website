# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Playground /PLAY-grownd/ The playground has swings, slides, and monkey bars. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Swing /SWING/ Aiko pumps her legs high on the swing. Fingerspell SWING
3 Slide /SLYD/ Go down the slide feet-first for safety. Fingerspell SLIDE
4 Seesaw /SEE-saw/ Two friends go up and down on the seesaw. Fingerspell SEESAW
5 Monkey bars /MUN-kee BARZ/ Kenji crosses the monkey bars hand over hand. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Sandbox /SAND-boks/ The sandbox is for building castles and digging. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Jump rope /JUMP ROHP/ The girls jump rope and count to one hundred. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Hopscotch /HOP-skoch/ Draw the hopscotch squares with chalk. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Tag /TAG/ "You're it!" Kenji shouts as they play tag. Fingerspell TAG
10 Hide and seek /HYD and SEEK/ In hide and seek, one person counts while others hide. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Catch /KACH/ Throw the ball and catch it with both hands. Fingerspell CATCH
12 Throw /THROH/ Throw the ball gently to your partner. Fingerspell THROW
13 Kick /KIK/ Kick the soccer ball toward the goal. Fingerspell KICK
14 Run /RUN/ Run fast in the relay race! L-hands, index pulls on opposite thumb, move forward
15 Jump /JUMP/ Jump over the puddle so your shoes stay dry! Fingerspell JUMP
16 Climb /KLYM/ Climb the rock wall one hand at a time. Fingerspell CLIMB
17 Skip /SKIP/ Skip along the path and sing a song. Fingerspell SKIP
18 Race /RAYS/ Ready, set, go! It's a running race. Fingerspell RACE
19 Team /TEEM/ Pick your team and choose who plays first. Fingerspell TEAM
20 Fair /FAIR/ Play fair so everyone has a good time. Fingerspell FAIR
21 Winner /WIN-er/ The winner shakes hands with the other players. Fingerspell WINNER
22 Loser /LOO-zer/ A gracious loser says "good game" and tries again. Fingerspell LOSER
23 Score /SKOR/ The score is five to three — we're winning! Both hands tally in air
24 Whistle /WIS-ul/ The teacher blows the whistle to end recess. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Line up /LYN UP/ Line up quietly to go back inside. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Bench /BENCH/ Sit on the bench if you need to rest. Fingerspell BENCH
27 Shade /SHAYD/ Find the shade under the tree on hot days. Fingerspell SHADE
28 Water fountain /WAW-ter FOWN-tun/ Get a drink from the water fountain. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Recess /REE-ses/ Recess is everybody's favorite time of day. Fingerspell RECESS
30 Outdoor /OWT-dor/ Outdoor games are best on sunny days. Fingerspell or sign each word

Aiko's Tip:

"Name the ACTIONS you do at recess: 'I RUN. I JUMP. I CLIMB. I THROW.' Action words (verbs!) are the most powerful words in any language!"
