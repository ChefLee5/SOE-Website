Scene 7: The Scientific Method

In the Celestia Lab, Elias and Selene follow the steps of scientific thinking.

# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Observe /ob-ZURV/ Observe the world around you carefully. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Question /KWES-chun/ Ask a question about something you want to understand. Index finger draws question mark in air
3 Hypothesis /hy-POTH-ih-sis/ A hypothesis is your educated guess. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Experiment /ek-SPER-ih-ment/ Design an experiment to test your hypothesis. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Variable /VAIR-ee-uh-bul/ A variable is the thing you change in an experiment. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Control /kun-TROHL/ The control group stays the same for comparison. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Data /DAY-tuh/ Collect data by measuring and recording results. D-hand moves along opposite palm (reading data)
8 Results /rih-ZULTS/ The results show what happened in the experiment. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Conclusion /kun-KLOO-zhun/ The conclusion tells what you learned from the experiment. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Theory /THEER-ee/ A theory is a well-tested explanation backed by evidence. Fingerspell THEORY
11 Fact /FAKT/ A fact is something that has been proven true. Fingerspell FACT
12 Opinion /oh-PIN-yun/ An opinion is a personal belief, not a fact. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Evidence /EV-ih-dents/ Evidence supports your conclusion. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Measure /MEZH-er/ Measure the temperature every hour. Both Y-hands tap together (SAME SIZE)
15 Record /rih-KORD/ Record your observations in a science journal. Fingerspell RECORD
