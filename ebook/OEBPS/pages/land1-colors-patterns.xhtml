Scene 7: Colors & Patterns

The Harmonia Paint Studio is where Kenji and Aiko mix colors and design patterns. Art is a language too.

🖼️ Illustration: Colors & Patterns
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Colors & Patterns' with numbered callout markers (1–25) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Red /RED/ The red paint is as bright as a cherry. Index finger strokes down lips
2 Blue /BLOO/ The sky above Harmonia is a deep blue. B-hand shakes in space
3 Yellow /YEL-oh/ The yellow sunflower turns toward the light. Y-hand shakes in space
4 Green /GREEN/ Green is the color of fresh leaves and grass. G-hand shakes in space
5 Orange /OR-inj/ Orange is a mix of red and yellow. C-hand squeezes near chin (like squeezing orange)
6 Purple /PUR-pul/ Red and blue mixed together make purple. P-hand shakes in space
7 Pink /PINK/ The pink flowers bloom in Harmonia's garden. Middle finger strokes down chin (P-hand)
8 Brown /BROWN/ The tree trunk is brown and rough. B-hand slides down cheek
9 Black /BLAK/ Kenji writes with black ink in his journal. Index finger swipes across forehead
10 White /WYT/ The white paper is ready for your drawing. 5-hand on chest pulls away to flat O
11 Gray /GRAY/ The rainy sky turns a cloudy gray. 5-hands pass through each other back and forth
12 Gold /GOHLD/ The gold trophy shines on the shelf. Fingerspell GOLD
13 Silver /SIL-ver/ The silver bracelet sparkles in the light. Fingerspell SILVER
14 Beige /BAYZH/ The beige walls make the room feel calm. Fingerspell BEIGE
15 Turquoise /TUR-koyz/ The turquoise ocean water is clear and warm. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Maroon /mah-ROON/ The maroon curtains are dark red-brown. Fingerspell MAROON
17 Navy /NAY-vee/ Kenji's navy blue uniform looks sharp. Fingerspell NAVY
18 Light /LYT/ Light blue is softer and paler than dark blue. Both flat hands rise up easily
19 Dark /DARK/ Dark green looks almost like black. Both flat hands cross in front of face
20 Bright /BRYT/ The bright yellow paint stands out on the canvas. Both flat O-hands at chest open to 5-hands
21 Striped /STRYPD/ Aiko's striped shirt has blue and white lines. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Plaid /PLAD/ The plaid blanket has a crisscross pattern. Fingerspell PLAID
23 Polka dot /POHL-kuh DOT/ The polka dot dress has small round circles. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Solid /SOL-id/ A solid color means one color with no pattern. Fingerspell SOLID
25 Pattern /PAT-ern/ The pattern repeats over and over on the fabric. Fingerspell or sign each word
