# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Apple /AP-ul/ The red apple is crisp and sweet. X-knuckle twists on cheek
2 Banana /buh-NAN-uh/ Peel the yellow banana before eating it. Mime peeling banana from index finger
3 Orange /OR-inj/ Squeeze the orange to make fresh juice. C-hand squeezes near chin (like squeezing orange)
4 Grape /GRAYP/ A bunch of purple grapes hangs from the vine. Fingerspell GRAPE
5 Strawberry /STRAW-bair-ee/ The strawberry is small, red, and sweet. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Watermelon /WAW-ter-mel-un/ A slice of watermelon is perfect on a hot day. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Peach /PEECH/ The fuzzy peach is soft and juicy. Fingerspell PEACH

9 Lemon /LEM-un/ A lemon is sour and yellow. Fingerspell LEMON
10 Tomato /tuh-MAY-toh/ Is a tomato a fruit or a vegetable? Fingerspell TOMATO
11 Carrot /KAIR-ut/ The orange carrot grows underground. Fingerspell CARROT
12 Potato /puh-TAY-toh/ Bake the potato in the oven for one hour. Fingerspell POTATO
13 Onion /UN-yun/ Chopping an onion can make your eyes water. Fingerspell ONION
14 Broccoli /BROK-uh-lee/ Broccoli looks like a tiny green tree. Fingerspell or sign each word

24 Mushroom /MUSH-room/ Mushrooms grow in dark, damp places. Fingerspell or sign each word

26 Avocado /av-uh-KAH-doh/ An avocado is green inside with a big seed. Fingerspell or sign each word
