# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Tablespoon /TAY-bul-spoon/ Add one tablespoon of butter. Fingerspell T-B-S or sign BIG + SPOON
2 Teaspoon /TEE-spoon/ One teaspoon of salt is enough. Fingerspell T-S-P or sign SMALL + SPOON
3 Cup /KUP/ Use one cup of flour for the cake. C-hand taps opposite flat palm (cup on saucer)
4 Pint /PYNT/ A pint of milk equals two cups. Fingerspell PINT
5 Quart /KWORT/ A quart is four cups of liquid. Fingerspell QUART
6 Gallon /GAL-un/ Buy a gallon of water for camping. G-hand large volume shape
7 Ounce /OWNS/ The recipe calls for eight ounces. Fingerspell OUNCE
8 Pound /POWND/ Buy one pound of ground beef. P-hand drops firmly
9 Gram /GRAM/ One hundred grams of rice is one serving. Fingerspell GRAM
10 Kilogram /KIL-oh-gram/ A kilogram is more than two pounds. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Liter /LEE-ter/ Drink two liters of water daily. Fingerspell LITER
12 Milliliter /MIL-ih-lee-ter/ The bottle says twenty-five milliliters. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Inch /INCH/ The board is twelve inches long. Index and thumb pinch close together
14 Foot /FOOT/ The table is three feet tall. Point to foot
15 Yard /YARD/ The fabric costs five dollars per yard. Fingerspell YARD
16 Mile /MYL/ The school is two miles from home. M-hand moves forward
17 Centimeter /SEN-tih-mee-ter/ Measure the line in centimeters. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Meter /MEE-ter/ The pool is fifty meters long. Fingerspell METER
19 Kilometer /KIL-oh-mee-ter/ A kilometer is more than half a mile. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Temperature /TEM-per-uh-chur/ Check the temperature on the weather app. Index slides up/down opposite index (thermometer)
21 Fahrenheit /FAIR-en-hyt/ Water boils at two hundred twelve Fahrenheit. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Celsius /SEL-see-us/ Water boils at one hundred Celsius. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Ruler /ROO-ler/ Use a ruler to draw a straight line. R-hands outline ruler
24 Tape measure /TAYP MEZH-er/ A tape measure can measure curves. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Scale /SKAYL/ Step on the scale to see your weight. Both flat hands balance like scale
