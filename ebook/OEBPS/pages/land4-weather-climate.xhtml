Scene 7: Weather & Climate

In Aquaria, weather changes constantly as winds blow from across the ocean. Ronan and Nerissa track it all.

A richly detailed scene depicting 'Weather & Climate' with numbered callout markers (1–30) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Weather /WETH-er/ The weather changes from sunny to cloudy. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Sunny /SUN-ee/ A sunny day is bright and warm. Fingerspell SUNNY
3 Cloudy /KLOW-dee/ The cloudy sky hides the sun. Fingerspell CLOUDY
4 Rainy /RAY-nee/ Bring an umbrella on a rainy day. Fingerspell RAINY
5 Windy /WIN-dee/ The windy day blows leaves across the path. Fingerspell WINDY
6 Snowy /SNOH-ee/ The snowy morning covers everything in white. Fingerspell SNOWY
7 Foggy /FOG-ee/ The foggy harbor makes it hard to see the ships. Fingerspell FOGGY
8 Stormy /STORM-ee/ A stormy night brings thunder and lightning. Fingerspell STORMY
9 Thunder /THUN-der/ The loud thunder echoes across the sky. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Lightning /LYT-ning/ A flash of lightning lit up the dark sea. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Rain /RAYN/ The rain falls gently on the garden. Both 5-hands drop down repeatedly (raindrops)
12 Snow /SNOH/ Snow falls softly and covers the trees. 5-hands drift down with wiggling fingers
13 Hail /HAYL/ Small balls of hail bounce off the ground. Fingerspell HAIL
14 Ice /EYS/ Ice forms on the pond when it's very cold. Fingerspell ICE
15 Frost /FROST/ Frost covers the grass on early winter mornings. Fingerspell FROST
16 Humidity /hyoo-MID-ih-tee/ High humidity makes the air feel thick and damp. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Temperature /TEM-per-uh-chur/ The temperature today is seventy-five degrees. Index slides up/down opposite index (thermometer)
18 Forecast /FOR-kast/ The weather forecast predicts rain tomorrow. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Umbrella /um-BREL-uh/ Nerissa opens her umbrella when it starts to rain. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Rainbow /RAYN-boh/ A rainbow arches across the sky after the storm. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Drought /DROWT/ A long drought dries up the rivers. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Flood /FLUD/ The heavy rain caused a flood in the valley. W-hands rise up from below
23 Hurricane /HUR-ih-kayn/ A hurricane brings strong winds and heavy rain. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Tornado /tor-NAY-doh/ A tornado is a spinning column of wind. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Earthquake /URTH-kwayk/ The earthquake shook the buildings in the city. S-hands grab air and shake
26 Breeze /BREEZ/ A cool breeze blows in from the ocean. Fingerspell BREEZE
27 Cloud /KLOWD/ A big white cloud drifts slowly overhead. Both claw hands shape clouds, move across
28 Sunshine /SUN-shyn/ Warm sunshine makes the flowers bloom. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Overcast /OH-ver-kast/ The overcast sky looks gray from morning to night. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Climate /KLY-mit/ Climate describes the typical weather over many years. Fingerspell or sign each word
