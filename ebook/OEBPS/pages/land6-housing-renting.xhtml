Scene 12: Housing & Renting

Finding a place to live in Sophia means learning the language of landlords, leases, and home life.

🖼️ Illustration: Housing & Renting
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Housing & Renting' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Lease /LEES/ A lease is a contract to rent a home for a set time. Fingerspell LEASE
2 Tenant /TEN-unt/ A tenant is the person who rents the apartment. Fingerspell TENANT
3 Landlord /LAND-lord/ The landlord owns the building and collects rent. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Security deposit /sih-KYOOR-ih-tee dih-POZ-it/ The security deposit is returned when you move out. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Rent /RENT/ Rent is due on the first day of every month. Fingerspell RENT
6 Apartment /uh-PART-ment/ The apartment has two bedrooms and one bathroom. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Studio /STOO-dee-oh/ A studio apartment has one room for living and sleeping. Fingerspell STUDIO
8 Maintenance /MAYN-tuh-nuns/ Call maintenance if the sink is leaking. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Eviction /ih-VIK-shun/ An eviction is when a landlord forces you to move out. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Move in /MOOV IN/ Move in day is the first of September. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Move out /MOOV OWT/ Clean the apartment before you move out. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Utilities /yoo-TIL-ih-teez/ Utilities like water and gas may be included in rent. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Furnished /FUR-nisht/ A furnished apartment comes with beds and a couch. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Unfurnished /un-FUR-nisht/ An unfurnished apartment is empty — bring your own furniture. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Application fee /ap-lih-KAY-shun FEE/ The application fee is twenty-five dollars. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Credit check /KRED-it CHEK/ The landlord runs a credit check before approving you. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Roommate /ROOM-mayt/ A roommate shares the apartment and splits rent. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Neighborhood /NAY-ber-hood/ Choose a safe neighborhood close to school or work. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Repair /rih-PAIR/ The landlord must repair broken appliances. Fingerspell REPAIR
20 Inspection /in-SPEK-shun/ The inspection checks the apartment's condition. Fingerspell or sign each word
🏠

Athena's Tip:

"Know your renting words! Practice: 'I am a TENANT. I signed a LEASE. My RENT is due on the first. I call MAINTENANCE for repairs.' These sentences make you a confident renter."
