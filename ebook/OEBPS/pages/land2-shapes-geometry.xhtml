# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Circle /SUR-kul/ A circle is perfectly round with no corners. Fingerspell CIRCLE
2 Square /SKWAIR/ A square has four equal sides and four right angles. Fingerspell SQUARE
3 Rectangle /REK-tang-gul/ A rectangle is like a stretched-out square. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Triangle /TRY-ang-gul/ A triangle has three sides and three corners. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Oval /OH-vul/ An oval is shaped like an egg. Fingerspell OVAL
6 Diamond /DY-mund/ A diamond shape has four sides tilted on a point. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Star /STAR/ Kwame draws a five-pointed star. Both index fingers brush up alternately
8 Heart /HART/ A heart shape is a symbol of love. Middle finger taps left chest
9 Pentagon /PEN-tuh-gon/ A pentagon has five sides. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Hexagon /HEK-suh-gon/ A hexagon has six sides like a honeycomb cell. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Octagon /OK-tuh-gon/ A stop sign is an octagon with eight sides. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Sphere /SFEER/ A ball is a three-dimensional sphere. Fingerspell SPHERE
13 Cube /KYOOB/ A cube has six flat square faces. Fingerspell CUBE
14 Cylinder /SIL-in-der/ A can of soup is shaped like a cylinder. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Cone /KOHN/ An ice cream cone narrows to a point. Fingerspell CONE
16 Pyramid /PEER-uh-mid/ A pyramid has a flat base and triangular sides. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Angle /ANG-gul/ An angle is formed where two lines meet. Fingerspell ANGLE
18 Right angle /RYT ANG-gul/ A right angle is exactly 90 degrees. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Line /LYN/ A straight line goes on forever in both directions. Fingerspell LINE
20 Point /POYNT/ A point is a single exact location. Fingerspell POINT
21 Parallel /PAIR-uh-lel/ Parallel lines run side by side and never touch. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Perpendicular /pur-pen-DIK-yoo-ler/ Perpendicular lines meet at a right angle. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Symmetry /SIM-eh-tree/ A butterfly has perfect symmetry — both sides match. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Radius /RAY-dee-us/ The radius is the distance from the center to the edge of a circle. Fingerspell RADIUS
25 Diameter /dy-AM-ih-ter/ The diameter goes all the way across the circle. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Circumference /sur-KUM-fer-ens/ The circumference is the distance around the circle. Fingerspell or sign each word
27 Side /SYD/ A triangle has three sides. Fingerspell SIDE
28 Corner /KOR-ner/ A square has four corners. Fingerspell CORNER
29 Face /FAYS/ A cube has six flat faces. Index finger circles face
30 Edge /EJ/ A cube has twelve edges. Fingerspell EDGE
