# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Bicycle /BY-sih-kul/ Ride your bicycle to the park. S-fists pedal in circles
2 Helmet /HEL-met/ Always wear a helmet when riding a bike. Both C-hands come down on head
3 Pedal /PED-ul/ Pedal faster to go up the hill. Fingerspell PEDAL
4 Chain /CHAYN/ The chain connects the pedals to the wheel. Fingerspell CHAIN
5 Tire /TYR/ Pump air into the flat tire. Fingerspell TIRE
6 Handlebar /HAN-dul-bar/ Hold the handlebar to steer the bike. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Brake /BRAYK/ Squeeze the brake to slow down. Fingerspell BRAKE
8 Kickstand /KIK-stand/ Use the kickstand to park your bike. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Bike lane /BYK LAYN/ Ride in the bike lane for safety. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Lock /LOK/ Lock your bike to the rack. Fingerspell LOCK
11 Sidewalk /SYD-wawk/ Walk on the sidewalk not in the street. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Crosswalk /KROS-wawk/ Cross the street at the crosswalk. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Pedestrian /peh-DES-tree-un/ The pedestrian waits for the walk signal. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Jogging /JOG-ing/ She goes jogging in the park every morning. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Stroller /STROH-ler/ Push the baby in the stroller. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Wheelchair /WEEL-chair/ The ramp is for wheelchair access. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Scooter /SKOO-ter/ The electric scooter is fun to ride. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Skateboard /SKAYT-bord/ He does tricks on his skateboard. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Path /PATH/ Follow the path through the garden. Fingerspell PATH
20 Trail /TRAYL/ The hiking trail goes to the waterfall. Fingerspell TRAIL
21 Ramp /RAMP/ Use the ramp instead of the stairs. Fingerspell RAMP
22 Stairs /STAIRZ/ Climb the stairs to the second floor. Fingerspell STAIRS
23 Elevator /EL-eh-vay-ter/ Take the elevator to the top floor. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Escalator /ES-kuh-lay-ter/ The escalator moves you up automatically. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Shortcut /SHORT-kut/ Take the shortcut through the park. Fingerspell or sign each word
