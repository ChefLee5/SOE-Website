# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Brain /BRAYN/ The brain controls everything your body does. Tap temple with index
2 Heart /HART/ The heart pumps blood through the whole body. Middle finger taps left chest
3 Lungs /LUNGZ/ The lungs fill with air every time you breathe in. Fingerspell LUNGS
4 Stomach /STUM-uk/ Food goes to the stomach to be broken down. Pat stomach
5 Liver /LIV-er/ The liver filters toxins and helps digest food. Fingerspell LIVER
6 Kidneys /KID-neez/ The kidneys clean the blood and make urine. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Intestines /in-TES-tinz/ The intestines absorb nutrients from digested food. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Bone /BOHN/ The body has 206 bones that form the skeleton. Crossed arms tap shoulders (like skeleton)
9 Skeleton /SKEL-uh-tun/ The skeleton is the framework that holds the body up. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Muscle /MUS-ul/ The muscle gets stronger when you exercise. Flex bicep, point to it
11 Tendon /TEN-dun/ A tendon connects muscle to bone. Fingerspell TENDON
12 Ligament /LIG-uh-ment/ A ligament connects one bone to another bone at a joint. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Joint /JOYNT/ The knee is a joint that lets the leg bend. Fingerspell JOINT
14 Blood /BLUD/ Blood carries oxygen to every part of the body. Red wiggling fingers drip down opposite hand
15 Blood vessel /BLUD VES-ul/ Blood vessels are the tubes that carry blood. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Artery /AR-ter-ee/ An artery carries blood away from the heart. Fingerspell ARTERY
17 Vein /VAYN/ A vein carries blood back to the heart. Fingerspell VEIN
18 Nerve /NURV/ A nerve sends signals between the brain and the body. Fingerspell NERVE
19 Skull /SKUL/ The skull is the hard bone that protects the brain. Fingerspell SKULL
20 Rib cage /RIB KAYJ/ The rib cage protects the heart and lungs. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Pelvis /PEL-vis/ The pelvis is the wide bone at the base of the spine. Fingerspell PELVIS
22 Cartilage /KAR-tih-lij/ Cartilage is softer than bone and cushions the joints. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Diaphragm /DY-uh-fram/ The diaphragm is the muscle that helps you breathe. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Spleen /SPLEEN/ The spleen helps the body fight infections. Fingerspell SPLEEN
25 Bladder /BLAD-er/ The bladder stores urine until you use the restroom. Fingerspell or sign each word
26 Oxygen /OK-sih-jen/ We breathe in oxygen and breathe out carbon dioxide. Fingerspell OXYGEN
27 Cell /SEL/ Every living thing is made of tiny cells. Fingerspell CELL
28 Tissue /TISH-oo/ Muscle tissue is a group of similar cells working together. Fingerspell TISSUE
29 Organ /OR-gun/ The heart is an organ that pumps blood. Fingerspell ORGAN
30 Pulse /PULS/ Amara presses two fingers to her wrist to feel her pulse. Fingerspell PULSE
