# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Bandage /BAN-dij/ Wrap the bandage around the cut. Mime wrapping bandage around hand
2 Cut /KUT/ Clean the cut with soap and water. Fingerspell CUT
3 Bruise /BROOZ/ The bruise turned purple after the fall. Fingerspell BRUISE
4 Burn /BURN/ Run cold water over a minor burn. Fingerspell BURN
5 Blister /BLIS-ter/ A blister formed on her heel from new shoes. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Sprain /SPRAYN/ He sprained his ankle playing basketball. Fingerspell SPRAIN
7 Fracture /FRAK-chur/ A fracture is a broken bone. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Swelling /SWEL-ing/ Apply ice to reduce the swelling. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Ice pack /YS PAK/ Put an ice pack on the injury. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Splint /SPLINT/ A splint keeps the broken bone still. Fingerspell SPLINT
11 Wound /WOOND/ Keep the wound clean to prevent infection. Fingerspell WOUND
12 Sting /STING/ A bee sting hurts but usually heals quickly. Fingerspell STING
13 Rash /RASH/ The rash is red and itchy. Fingerspell RASH
14 Fever /FEE-ver/ A fever means your temperature is too high. Back of hand on forehead (checking temp)
15 Cough /KOF/ Cover your mouth when you cough. Fist thumps chest
16 Sneeze /SNEEZ/ Sneeze into your elbow, not your hand. Pinch nose, head jerks forward
17 Dizzy /DIZ-ee/ She felt dizzy and needed to sit down. Fingerspell DIZZY
18 Nausea /NAW-zee-uh/ Nausea means feeling sick to your stomach. Fingerspell NAUSEA
19 CPR /SEE-PEE-AR/ Learn CPR to help in an emergency. Fingerspell CPR
20 Poison /POY-zun/ Call poison control if a child swallows chemicals. Fingerspell POISON
21 Allergic reaction /uh-LUR-jik ree-AK-shun/ An allergic reaction can cause hives. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Ambulance /AM-byoo-lents/ Call an ambulance for serious injuries. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Emergency room /ee-MUR-jen-see ROOM/ Go to the emergency room for broken bones. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Stretcher /STRECH-er/ The paramedic placed the patient on a stretcher. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Recover /rih-KUV-er/ Rest and fluids help you recover faster. Fingerspell or sign each word
