# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Recipe /RES-ih-pee/ Follow the recipe step by step. Fingerspell RECIPE
2 Ingredient /in-GREE-dee-ent/ Flour is an important ingredient in bread. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Measure /MEZH-er/ Measure the sugar carefully before adding it. Both Y-hands tap together (SAME SIZE)
4 Cup /KUP/ Add one cup of milk to the batter. C-hand taps opposite flat palm (cup on saucer)
5 Tablespoon /TAY-bul-spoon/ Use two tablespoons of butter. Fingerspell T-B-S or sign BIG + SPOON
6 Teaspoon /TEE-spoon/ Add half a teaspoon of vanilla extract. Fingerspell T-S-P or sign SMALL + SPOON
7 Pinch /PINCH/ A pinch of salt is a tiny amount between two fingers. Fingerspell PINCH
8 Dash /DASH/ A dash of pepper adds flavor to the soup. Fingerspell DASH
9 Stir /STUR/ Stir the batter until it is smooth. Fingerspell STIR
10 Mix /MIKS/ Mix the dry ingredients together first. Fingerspell MIX
11 Pour /POR/ Pour the batter into the baking pan. Fingerspell POUR
12 Preheat /pree-HEET/ Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Bake /BAYK/ Bake the cookies for twelve minutes. Fingerspell BAKE
14 Cool /KOOL/ Let the cake cool before adding frosting. Both open hands fan at body
15 Serving /SUR-ving/ One serving of rice is one half cup. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Serving size /SUR-ving SYZ/ The serving size is listed on the label. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Yield /YEELD/ This recipe yields twenty-four cookies. Fingerspell YIELD
18 Double /DUB-ul/ Double the recipe to make enough for everyone. Fingerspell DOUBLE
19 Halve /HAV/ Halve the recipe if you only need a small batch. Fingerspell HALVE
20 Level /LEV-ul/ Use a level cup of flour, not a heaping one. Fingerspell LEVEL
21 Heaping /HEEP-ing/ A heaping tablespoon is piled above the rim. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Sift /SIFT/ Sift the flour to remove lumps. Fingerspell SIFT
23 Whisk /WISK/ Whisk the eggs until they are fluffy. Fingerspell WHISK
24 Grease /GREES/ Grease the pan so the cake doesn't stick. Fingerspell GREASE
25 Batch /BACH/ The first batch of cookies is ready! Fingerspell BATCH
26 Timer /TY-mer/ Set the timer so the bread doesn't burn. T-hand taps wrist
27 Degrees /dih-GREEZ/ Bake at three hundred fifty degrees Fahrenheit. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Measuring cup /MEZH-er-ing KUP/ Use a measuring cup for liquid ingredients. Fingerspell or sign each word
29 Measuring spoon /MEZH-er-ing SPOON/ The measuring spoon set has four sizes. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Spatula /SPACH-uh-luh/ Use a spatula to scrape the bowl clean. Fingerspell or sign each word

Octavia's Tip:

"Cooking IS math! When you MEASURE, you use fractions. When you DOUBLE a recipe, you multiply. When you HALVE it, you divide. Bake a cake and do math at the same time!"
