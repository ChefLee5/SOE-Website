# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 School /SKOOL/ Every neighborhood has a school for children. Clap hands twice
2 Library /LY-brair-ee/ The public library lends books for free. L-hand circles in air
3 Hospital /HOS-pih-tul/ Go to the hospital for emergency medical care. H-hand draws cross on upper arm
4 Fire station /FYR STAY-shun/ The fire station has trucks and brave firefighters. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Police station /puh-LEES STAY-shun/ Report a crime at the police station. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Post office /POHST OF-is/ Mail a letter or package at the post office. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Bank /BANGK/ The bank opens at nine in the morning. B-hand taps opposite palm repeatedly (stacking money)
8 Grocery store /GROH-ser-ee STOR/ Buy food and household items at the grocery store. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Restaurant /RES-tuh-rawnt/ The restaurant serves lunch from eleven to two. R-hand touches each side of chin
10 Pharmacy /FAR-muh-see/ The pharmacy fills prescriptions and sells medicine. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Gas station /GAS STAY-shun/ Buy fuel for your car at the gas station. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Laundromat /LAWN-droh-mat/ Wash your clothes at the laundromat. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Park /PARK/ Children play on the swings in the park. Modified P-hand taps S-hand on top
14 Playground /PLAY-grownd/ The playground has slides, swings, and monkey bars. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Community center /kuh-MYOO-nih-tee SEN-ter/ The community center hosts classes and events. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Place of worship /PLAYS uv WUR-ship/ A place of worship is where people gather for prayer. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 City hall /SIT-ee HAWL/ City hall is where the mayor works. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Courthouse /KORT-hows/ Legal cases are heard in the courthouse. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Museum /myoo-ZEE-um/ The museum has exhibits about history and art. M-hands draw building outline
20 Movie theater /MOO-vee THEE-uh-ter/ Watch a film at the movie theater. Fingerspell or sign each word
