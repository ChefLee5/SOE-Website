6 Strengths /STRENGTHS/ My strengths include teamwork and communication. Fingerspell or sign each word

8 Punctual /PUNK-choo-ul/ Be punctual and arrive five minutes early. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Professional /proh-FESH-un-ul/ Dress in professional clothes for the interview. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Handshake /HAND-shayk/ Give a firm handshake when you meet the interviewer. Clasp hands together, mime shaking

19 Part-time /PART-tym/ She works part-time while attending school. Fingerspell or sign each word

21 Background check /BAK-grownd CHEK/ The employer runs a background check. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Follow up /FOL-oh UP/ Follow up with a thank-you email. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Offer /OF-er/ She received a job offer on Friday. Fingerspell OFFER
24 Accept /ak-SEPT/ He decided to accept the position. Fingerspell ACCEPT
25 Decline /dih-KLYN/ She had to decline the offer. Fingerspell or sign each word
