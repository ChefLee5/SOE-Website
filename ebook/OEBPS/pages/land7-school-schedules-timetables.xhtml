22 Tardy /TAR-dee/ Three tardies equal one absence. Fingerspell TARDY
23 Absent /AB-sent/ Mark the student absent if they are not here. Fingerspell ABSENT
24 Present /PREZ-ent/ Raise your hand to be marked present. Fingerspell or sign each word
