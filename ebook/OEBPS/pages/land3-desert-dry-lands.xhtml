# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Desert /DEZ-ert/ The desert is hot and dry with very little rain. Both flat hands push outward (dry, vast)
2 Cactus /KAK-tus/ The cactus stores water inside its stem. Fingerspell CACTUS
3 Sand dune /SAND DOON/ Wind shapes the sand dune into hills. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Oasis /oh-AY-sis/ An oasis is a green spot with water. Fingerspell OASIS
5 Drought /DROWT/ A drought means no rain for a long time. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Scorpion /SKOR-pee-un/ Watch out for the scorpion under the rock. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Lizard /LIZ-erd/ The lizard basks in the sun on a rock. Fingerspell LIZARD
8 Rattlesnake /RAT-ul-snayk/ The rattlesnake shakes its tail as warning. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Vulture /VUL-chur/ The vulture circles high in the sky. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Tumbleweed /TUM-bul-weed/ A dry tumbleweed rolls across the road. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Canyon /KAN-yun/ The Grand Canyon is a mile deep. Fingerspell CANYON
12 Mesa /MAY-suh/ A mesa is a flat-topped desert hill. Fingerspell MESA
13 Mirage /mih-RAHJ/ A mirage looks like water but is hot air. Fingerspell MIRAGE
14 Arid /AIR-id/ Arid land has very little rainfall. Fingerspell ARID
15 Shade /SHAYD/ Rest in the shade to escape the sun. Fingerspell SHADE
16 Canteen /kan-TEEN/ Fill your canteen with water before hiking. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Compass /KUM-pus/ Use a compass to find north. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Fossil /FOS-ul/ Scientists found a dinosaur fossil. Fingerspell FOSSIL
19 Erosion /ee-ROH-zhun/ Wind erosion carved the rock. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Sandstorm /SAND-storm/ A sandstorm fills the air with sand. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Sunrise /SUN-ryz/ The desert sunrise paints the sky orange. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Sunset /SUN-set/ The sunset over the desert is breathtaking. Fingerspell SUNSET
23 Stars /STARZ/ You can see thousands of stars in the desert. Both index fingers brush up alternately (repeated)
24 Heat /HEET/ Desert heat can reach over one hundred degrees. Fingerspell HEAT
25 Survive /sur-VYV/ Desert animals survive with very little water. Fingerspell or sign each word
