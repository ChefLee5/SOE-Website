# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Hammer /HAM-er/ Use a hammer to drive nails into wood. Fingerspell HAMMER
2 Nail /NAYL/ The nail holds two boards together. Fingerspell NAIL
3 Screw /SKROO/ A screw twists into wood for a tight hold. Fingerspell SCREW
4 Screwdriver /SKROO-dry-ver/ Turn the screw with a screwdriver. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Wrench /RENCH/ The wrench tightens and loosens bolts. Fingerspell WRENCH
6 Pliers /PLY-erz/ Pliers grip and pull things with their jaws. Fingerspell PLIERS
7 Drill /DRIL/ The drill makes holes in wood, metal, or walls. Fingerspell DRILL
8 Saw /SAW/ Use the saw to cut boards to the right length. Fingerspell SAW
9 Level /LEV-ul/ The level checks if a surface is flat. Fingerspell LEVEL
10 Tape measure /TAYP MEZH-er/ Measure twice with the tape measure, cut once. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Ladder /LAD-er/ Climb the ladder to reach the high shelf. Fingerspell LADDER
12 Paint roller /PAYNT ROH-ler/ The paint roller covers large areas quickly. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Paintbrush /PAYNT-brush/ Use a paintbrush for edges and small details. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Bucket /BUK-it/ Fill the bucket with soapy water for cleaning. Fingerspell BUCKET
15 Shovel /SHUV-ul/ Use a shovel to dig a hole in the ground. Fingerspell SHOVEL
16 Rake /RAYK/ Rake the leaves into a pile in the fall. Fingerspell RAKE
17 Wheelbarrow /WEEL-bair-oh/ The wheelbarrow carries heavy loads of dirt. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Hard hat /HARD HAT/ Wear a hard hat on the construction site for safety. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Safety goggles /SAYF-tee GOG-ulz/ Safety goggles protect your eyes from dust and debris. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Work gloves /WURK GLUVZ/ Work gloves protect your hands from sharp objects. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Blueprint /BLOO-print/ The blueprint shows the plan for the new building. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Cement /sih-MENT/ Cement hardens into a strong, solid surface. Fingerspell CEMENT
23 Brick /BRIK/ Stack the bricks to build a wall. Fingerspell BRICK
24 Lumber /LUM-ber/ Lumber is wood cut into boards for building. Fingerspell LUMBER
25 Wire /WYR/ The electrician runs wire through the walls. Fingerspell WIRE
26 Pipe /PYP/ Water flows through a copper pipe. Fingerspell PIPE
27 Bolt /BOHLT/ Tighten the bolt with a wrench. Fingerspell BOLT
28 Nut /NUT/ The nut screws onto the bolt to hold it. Fingerspell NUT
29 Sandpaper /SAND-pay-per/ Sandpaper makes rough wood smooth. Fingerspell or sign each word
30 Toolbox /TOOL-boks/ Keep all your tools organized in the toolbox. Fingerspell or sign each word

Athena's Tip:

"Tools have FUN names! Say them aloud: HAMMER, DRILL, WRENCH, SANDPAPER. Now pick up an object near you and describe it: 'This is a ___. It is made of ___. We use it to ___."
