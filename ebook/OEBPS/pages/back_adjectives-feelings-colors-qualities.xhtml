# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Beautiful /BYOO-tih-ful/ The sunset is beautiful tonight. Circle face with 5-hand, close to flat O
2 Ugly /UG-lee/ The old building looks ugly but is strong. Fingerspell UGLY
3 Delicious /dih-LISH-us/ The homemade bread smells delicious. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Terrible /TAIR-ih-bul/ The storm last night was terrible. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Important /im-POR-tant/ Education is very important for your future. F-hands rise from center to top
6 Interesting /IN-ter-es-ting/ The book about space was very interesting. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Boring /BOR-ing/ The long meeting was boring. Fingerspell BORING
8 Tired /TYRD/ I am tired after a long day of work. Bent hands on chest, fall forward
9 Hungry /HUNG-gree/ The children are hungry after playing outside. Fingerspell HUNGRY
10 Thirsty /THUR-stee/ I am thirsty — may I have some water? Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Sick /SIK/ She stayed home because she was sick. Middle finger on forehead + stomach
12 Healthy /HEL-thee/ Eating vegetables keeps you healthy. Both claw hands on chest pull to fists (strong)
13 Strong /STRONG/ The strong man lifted the heavy box. Fingerspell STRONG
14 Weak /WEEK/ After being sick, he felt weak. Fingerspell WEAK
15 Brave /BRAYV/ The brave firefighter ran into the building. Claw hands at shoulders pull to fists at chest
16 Afraid /uh-FRAYD/ The child was afraid of the dark. Fingerspell AFRAID
17 Proud /PROWD/ Her parents are proud of her success. Thumb slides up chest
18 Nervous /NUR-vus/ I feel nervous before a job interview. Both 5-hands shake in front
19 Excited /ek-SY-ted/ The children are excited about the field trip. Middle fingers brush up chest alternately
20 Grateful /GRAYT-ful/ I am grateful for my family and friends. Flat hand from chin sweeps forward
21 Kind /KYND/ A kind word can change someone's day. Fingerspell KIND
22 Friendly /FREND-lee/ The new neighbor is very friendly. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Careful /KAIR-ful/ Be careful when crossing the street. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Ready /RED-ee/ Are you ready for the test? Fingerspell READY
25 Free /FREE/ The concert in the park is free. F-hands cross, then spread apart
