# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Ocean /OH-shun/ The ocean covers most of planet Earth. Both hands make waves, push forward
2 Wave /WAYV/ The waves crash on the sandy shore. Open hand waves side to side
3 Sand /SAND/ Build a sandcastle on the warm sand. Fingerspell SAND
4 Shell /SHEL/ Collect beautiful shells along the beach. Fingerspell SHELL
5 Tide /TYD/ The tide comes in and goes out twice a day. Fingerspell TIDE
6 Seaweed /SEE-weed/ Green seaweed washes up on the shore. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Coral /KOR-ul/ The coral reef is home to colorful fish. Fingerspell CORAL
8 Starfish /STAR-fish/ The starfish has five arms. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Jellyfish /JEL-ee-fish/ The jellyfish floats through the water. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Crab /KRAB/ The crab walks sideways on the sand. Both V-hands snap open and close (pincers)
11 Dolphin /DOL-fin/ Dolphins jump out of the sea. B-hand dips and rises forward
12 Whale /WAYL/ The blue whale is the largest animal on Earth. W-hand dips and rises (swimming)
13 Lighthouse /LYT-hows/ The lighthouse guides ships safely to shore. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Pier /PEER/ Walk to the end of the pier to watch the sunset. Fingerspell PIER
15 Surfboard /SURF-bord/ Stand on the surfboard and ride the wave. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Lifeguard /LYF-gard/ The lifeguard watches swimmers. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Sunscreen /SUN-skreen/ Put on sunscreen to protect your skin. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Towel /TOW-ul/ Dry off with a towel after swimming. Flat hands rub back and forth at sides
19 Sandcastle /SAND-kas-ul/ Decorate the sandcastle with shells. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Seagull /SEE-gul/ The seagull flies over the beach. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Horizon /hor-RY-zun/ The sun sets on the horizon. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Current /KUR-ent/ The current moves water in big circles. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Anchor /ANG-ker/ Drop the anchor to stop the boat. Fingerspell ANCHOR
24 Shore /SHOR/ The boat returned to shore. Fingerspell SHORE
25 Reef /REEF/ The reef is full of colorful sea creatures. Fingerspell REEF
