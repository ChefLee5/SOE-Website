Scene 20: Pets at Home

Kenji and Aiko visit the Harmonia Pet Shop. Caring for a pet teaches responsibility — and lots of vocabulary!

🖼️ Illustration: Pets at Home
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Pets at Home' with numbered callout markers (1–25) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Pet /PET/ A pet is an animal you keep at home. Stroke back of hand (petting motion)
2 Dog /DOG/ The dog wags its tail when it is happy. Snap fingers + pat thigh
3 Cat /KAT/ The cat purrs when you scratch behind its ears. F-hand pulls from cheek (whiskers)
4 Fish /FISH/ The goldfish swims in a round bowl. Flat hand swims forward like fish
5 Bird /BURD/ The bird sings a cheerful song every morning. G-hand opens and closes at mouth (beak)
6 Hamster /HAM-ster/ The hamster runs on its wheel at night. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Turtle /TUR-tul/ The turtle moves slowly but lives a long time. A-hand under opposite cupped hand, thumb peeks
8 Rabbit /RAB-it/ The rabbit hops around the yard eating clover. Both H-hands at head, wiggle (long ears)
9 Collar /KOL-er/ Put a collar and tag on your dog. Fingerspell COLLAR
10 Leash /LEESH/ Walk the dog on a leash for safety. Fingerspell LEASH
11 Bowl /BOHL/ Fill the water bowl for the cat. Both cupped hands form bowl shape
12 Cage /KAYJ/ The bird lives in a large cage with perches. Fingerspell CAGE
13 Veterinarian /vet-er-ih-NAIR-ee-un/ Take your pet to the veterinarian for checkups. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Feed /FEED/ Feed the fish twice a day. Fingerspell FEED
15 Groom /GROOM/ Groom the dog by brushing its fur. Fingerspell GROOM
16 Train /TRAYN/ Train the puppy to sit and stay. H-hands slide along each other (tracks)
17 Walk /WAWK/ Walk the dog around the block every morning. Both flat hands walk forward alternately
18 Adopt /uh-DOPT/ Adopt a pet from the animal shelter. Fingerspell ADOPT
19 Shelter /SHEL-ter/ The animal shelter cares for homeless pets. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Loyal /LOY-ul/ Dogs are loyal companions to their owners. Fingerspell LOYAL
21 Playful /PLAY-ful/ The playful kitten chases a ball of yarn. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Bark /BARK/ The dog barks when someone knocks on the door. Fingerspell BARK
23 Purr /PUR/ The cat purrs softly while sleeping on my lap. Fingerspell PURR
24 Fetch /FECH/ Throw the ball and the dog will fetch it. Fingerspell FETCH
25 Companion /kum-PAN-yun/ A pet is a loving companion for the whole family. Fingerspell or sign each word
