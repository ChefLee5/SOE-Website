# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 One /WUN/ One is the first number on the wall. Hold up index finger
2 Two /TOO/ Two socks make a pair. Hold up index + middle finger
3 Three /THREE/ A triangle has three sides. Hold up thumb + index + middle
4 Four /FOR/ A table has four legs. Hold up four fingers (no thumb)
5 Five /FYV/ Kwame holds up five fingers on one hand. Open 5-hand
6 Six /SIKS/ A cube has six flat sides. Fingerspell SIX
7 Seven /SEV-en/ There are seven days in a week. Fingerspell SEVEN
8 Eight /AYT/ An octopus has eight arms. Fingerspell EIGHT
9 Nine /NYN/ Nine is one less than ten. Fingerspell NINE
10 Ten /TEN/ Count to ten on your fingers. Thumb of A-hand shakes
11 Eleven /ih-LEV-en/ Eleven comes right after ten. Fingerspell ELEVEN
12 Twelve /TWELV/ There are twelve months in a year. Fingerspell TWELVE
13 Thirteen /thur-TEEN/ Thirteen is the first "teen" number. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Twenty /TWEN-tee/ Twenty is two groups of ten. Fingerspell TWENTY
15 Thirty /THUR-tee/ There are about thirty days in a month. Fingerspell THIRTY
16 Forty /FOR-tee/ Kwame counted forty stars in the sky. Fingerspell FORTY
17 Fifty /FIF-tee/ Fifty cents equals half a dollar. Fingerspell FIFTY
18 Hundred /HUN-dred/ One hundred pennies make one dollar. Sign 1 + C (one-C)
19 Thousand /THOW-zund/ A thousand is ten groups of one hundred. Fingertips of M-hand tap opposite palm
20 Million /MIL-yun/ There are millions of stars in the sky. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 First /FURST/ Kwame was the first to finish the puzzle. Fingerspell FIRST
22 Second /SEK-und/ Octavia was the second person in line. Index finger flicks behind opposite palm
23 Third /THURD/ The third floor is above the second floor. Fingerspell THIRD
24 Zero /ZEE-roh/ Zero means nothing, or the starting point. O-hand
25 Number /NUM-ber/ Write the number under each picture. Both flat O-hands twist together
26 Count /KOWNT/ Count the apples in the basket. Thumb and index slide up opposite palm
27 Pair /PAIR/ A pair means two of the same thing. Fingerspell PAIR
28 Dozen /DUZ-en/ A dozen means twelve. Buy a dozen eggs. Fingerspell DOZEN
29 Half /HAF/ Cut the pizza in half to share equally. Fingerspell HALF
30 Double /DUB-ul/ Double three is six. Fingerspell DOUBLE
31 Even /EE-ven/ Two, four, six, and eight are even numbers. Fingerspell EVEN
32 Odd /OD/ One, three, five, and seven are odd numbers. Fingerspell ODD
33 Digit /DIJ-it/ Each number from zero to nine is a single digit. Fingerspell DIGIT
34 Numeral /NOO-mer-ul/ A numeral is a symbol that represents a number. Fingerspell or sign each word
35 Sequence /SEE-kwens/ A sequence is numbers in a specific order. Fingerspell or sign each word
