# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Recycle /ree-SY-kul/ Recycle paper, plastic, and glass. R-hand circles (going around again)
2 Reuse /ree-YOOZ/ Reuse the bag instead of throwing it away. Fingerspell REUSE
3 Reduce /rih-DOOS/ Reduce waste by buying only what you need. Fingerspell REDUCE
4 Trash /TRASH/ Put trash in the trash can, not on the ground. Fingerspell TRASH
5 Garbage /GAR-bij/ Garbage trucks pick up the bins every week. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Litter /LIT-er/ Never litter — always find a proper bin. Fingerspell LITTER
7 Bin /BIN/ The blue bin is for recycling. Fingerspell BIN
8 Compost /KOM-pohst/ Compost turns food scraps into rich soil. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Landfill /LAND-fil/ Trash goes to the landfill when it can't be recycled. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Pollution /puh-LOO-shun/ Pollution makes the air and water dirty. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Clean energy /KLEEN EN-er-jee/ Solar and wind are forms of clean energy. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Solar panel /SOH-ler PAN-ul/ A solar panel turns sunlight into electricity. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Wind turbine /WIND TUR-byn/ The wind turbine spins to make electricity from wind. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Water bottle /WAW-ter BOT-ul/ Use a reusable water bottle instead of plastic. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Plastic /PLAS-tik/ Too much plastic ends up in the ocean. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Paper /PAY-per/ Recycle paper to save trees. Both flat hands brush past each other
17 Glass /GLAS/ Glass can be recycled again and again. C-hand rises from opposite palm (tall glass)
18 Aluminum /uh-LOO-mih-num/ Aluminum cans are easy to recycle. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Conserve /kun-SURV/ Conserve water by turning off the faucet. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Protect /proh-TEKT/ It is our job to protect the environment. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Ecosystem /EE-koh-sis-tem/ Every plant and animal is part of an ecosystem. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Biodegradable /by-oh-dee-GRAY-duh-bul/ Biodegradable materials break down naturally over time. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Emissions /ee-MISH-unz/ Cars and factories produce harmful emissions. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Habitat /HAB-ih-tat/ Protect the habitat so animals have safe homes. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 Volunteer /vol-un-TEER/ Volunteer to help clean up the park. Fingerspell or sign each word
