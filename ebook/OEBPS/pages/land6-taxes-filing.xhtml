# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Tax /TAKS/ Pay your income tax every year. T-hand or X-hand taps opposite palm
2 Tax return /TAKS rih-TURN/ File your tax return by April fifteenth. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Refund /REE-fund/ You may get a refund if you overpaid taxes. Fingerspell REFUND
4 W-2 /DUB-ul-yoo TOO/ Your employer sends a W-2 form each January. Fingerspell W-2
5 Income /IN-kum/ Report all income on your tax return. Hand scoops money toward body
6 Deduction /dih-DUK-shun/ A deduction reduces the tax you owe. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Dependent /dih-PEN-dent/ Claim your children as dependents. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Filing status /FY-ling STAT-us/ Choose your filing status: single or married. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Federal /FED-er-ul/ Federal taxes go to the national government. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 State tax /STAYT TAKS/ State tax rates vary by state. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Sales tax /SAYLZ TAKS/ Sales tax is added at the register. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Property tax /PROP-er-tee TAKS/ Property tax is based on home value. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Audit /AW-dit/ An audit reviews your tax records. Fingerspell AUDIT
14 Receipt /rih-SEET/ Keep receipts for tax deductions. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Accountant /uh-KOWN-tent/ Hire an accountant to prepare your taxes. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 IRS /EYE-AR-ES/ The IRS collects federal taxes. Fingerspell IRS
17 Extension /ek-STEN-shun/ File an extension if you need more time. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Withholding /with-HOHL-ding/ Withholding is tax taken from your paycheck. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Exempt /eg-ZEMPT/ Some items are exempt from sales tax. Fingerspell EXEMPT
20 Penalty /PEN-ul-tee/ A penalty is charged for late filing. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Gross pay /GROHS PAY/ Gross pay is your total earnings before taxes. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Net pay /NET PAY/ Net pay is what you take home after taxes. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Tax bracket /TAKS BRAK-it/ Your tax bracket determines your rate. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Volunteer tax help /VOL-un-TEER TAKS HELP/ Free volunteer tax help is available. Fingerspell or sign each word
25 E-file /EE-fyl/ E-file your tax return for faster processing. Fingerspell E-FILE
