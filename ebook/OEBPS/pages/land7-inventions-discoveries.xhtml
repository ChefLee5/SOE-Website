Scene 5: Inventions & Discoveries

The Celestia Museum of Innovation celebrates the greatest inventions and the people who made them.

🖼️ Illustration: Inventions & Discoveries
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Inventions & Discoveries' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Invention /in-VEN-shun/ An invention is something new that someone creates. 4-hand pulls from forehead (IDEA + MAKE)
2 Discovery /dis-KUV-er-ee/ A discovery is finding something for the first time. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Inventor /in-VEN-ter/ An inventor thinks of new ideas and builds them. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Scientist /SY-en-tist/ A scientist studies the natural world. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Experiment /ek-SPER-ih-ment/ An experiment tests an idea to see if it works. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Hypothesis /hy-POTH-ih-sis/ A hypothesis is a prediction based on what you know. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Laboratory /LAB-ruh-tor-ee/ Scientists work in a laboratory with special equipment. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Microscope /MY-kroh-skohp/ A microscope makes tiny things look big enough to study. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Telescope /TEL-ih-skohp/ A telescope makes faraway things look closer. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Electricity /ee-lek-TRIS-ih-tee/ Benjamin Franklin studied electricity with a kite. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Light bulb /LYT BULB/ Thomas Edison invented the practical light bulb. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Telephone /TEL-ih-fohn/ Alexander Graham Bell invented the telephone. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Airplane /AIR-playn/ The Wright Brothers built and flew the first airplane. Y-hand with index out, flies forward
14 Computer /kum-PYOO-ter/ Computers have changed how we work, learn, and communicate. C-hand bounces up opposite forearm
15 Internet /IN-ter-net/ The internet connects billions of people worldwide. 5-hands, middle fingers touch, twist
16 Printing press /PRIN-ting PRES/ The printing press made books available to everyone. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Vaccine /vak-SEEN/ The vaccine has saved millions of lives from disease. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Wheel /WEEL/ The wheel is one of the oldest and most important inventions. Fingerspell WHEEL
19 Steam engine /STEEM EN-jin/ The steam engine powered the Industrial Revolution. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Solar panel /SOH-ler PAN-ul/ A solar panel converts sunlight into electricity. Fingerspell or sign each word
