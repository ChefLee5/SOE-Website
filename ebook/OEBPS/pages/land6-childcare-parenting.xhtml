Scene 13: Childcare & Parenting

In Sophia, parents and caregivers learn the vocabulary of raising children — from infancy to school age.

🖼️ Illustration: Childcare & Parenting
A richly detailed scene depicting 'Childcare & Parenting' with numbered callout markers (1–20) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Daycare /DAY-kair/ Drop off the baby at daycare before going to work. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Babysitter /BAY-bee-sit-er/ The babysitter watches the children on Friday nights. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Diaper /DY-per/ Change the baby's diaper every two hours. Fingerspell DIAPER
4 Bottle /BOT-ul/ Warm the baby's bottle to the right temperature. Fingerspell BOTTLE
5 Formula /FOR-myoo-luh/ Baby formula provides nutrition when breastfeeding isn't possible. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Crib /KRIB/ The baby sleeps safely in the crib. Fingerspell CRIB
7 Stroller /STROH-ler/ Push the stroller through the park on sunny days. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Pacifier /PAS-ih-fy-er/ The pacifier soothes the baby when she cries. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Car seat /KAR SEET/ Every child must ride in a car seat until a certain age. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Highchair /HY-chair/ The baby eats in the highchair at the table. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Sippy cup /SIP-ee KUP/ The toddler drinks milk from a sippy cup. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Teething /TEE-thing/ Teething means the baby's first teeth are coming in. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Lullaby /LUL-uh-by/ Sing a soft lullaby to help the baby fall asleep. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Nap /NAP/ The toddler takes a nap after lunch. Fingerspell NAP
15 Potty training /POT-ee TRAY-ning/ Potty training teaches toddlers to use the toilet. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Pediatrician /pee-dee-uh-TRISH-un/ The pediatrician is a doctor for children. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Immunization /im-yoo-nih-ZAY-shun/ Immunization shots protect children from diseases. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Play date /PLAY DAYT/ The children have a play date at the park. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Preschool /PREE-skool/ Preschool prepares children for kindergarten. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Bedtime /BED-tym/ Bedtime is eight o'clock on school nights. Fingerspell or sign each word

Athena's Tip:
