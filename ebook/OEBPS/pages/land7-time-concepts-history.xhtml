# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Past /PAST/ The past is everything that already happened. Fingerspell PAST
2 Present /PREZ-ent/ The present is the time happening right now. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Future /FYOO-chur/ The future is the time that hasn't happened yet. Fingerspell FUTURE
4 Ancient /AYN-shunt/ Ancient civilizations lived thousands of years ago. S-hand pulls wisdom from chin repeatedly
5 Modern /MOD-ern/ Modern technology includes smartphones and computers. M-hand flicks forward from chin (NEW)
6 Century /SEN-chur-ee/ A century is one hundred years. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Decade /DEK-ayd/ A decade is ten years. Fingerspell DECADE
8 Era /EER-uh/ The space era began in the 1960s. E-hand circles in time motion
9 Timeline /TYM-lyn/ A timeline shows events in the order they happened. Fingerspell or sign each word
10 Cause /KAWZ/ Every event in history has a cause. Fingerspell CAUSE
11 Effect /ih-FEKT/ The effect of the invention was a changed world. Fingerspell EFFECT
12 Revolution /rev-oh-LOO-shun/ A revolution is a big, sudden change. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Independence /in-dih-PEN-dents/ Independence Day celebrates a nation's freedom. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 War /WOR/ War brings conflict and suffering. Fingerspell WAR
15 Peace /PEES/ Peace is the absence of war and conflict. Fingerspell PEACE
16 Treaty /TREE-tee/ A treaty is an agreement between nations. Fingerspell TREATY
17 Culture /KUL-chur/ Culture includes traditions, art, food, and language. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Civilization /siv-ih-lih-ZAY-shun/ A civilization is a complex society with its own culture. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Heritage /HAIR-ih-tij/ Heritage is the history and traditions passed down. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Legacy /LEG-uh-see/ Every person leaves a legacy for the next generation. Fingerspell LEGACY
