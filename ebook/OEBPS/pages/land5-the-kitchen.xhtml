Scene 8: The Kitchen

Back home, Amara and Felix prepare a healthy meal from scratch. Every tool in the kitchen has a name and a job.

🖼️ Illustration: The Kitchen
A richly detailed scene depicting 'The Kitchen' with numbered callout markers (1–40) indicating each vocabulary item's location in the scene.
# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Stove /STOHV/ Amara turns on the stove to heat the pot. S-hands mime turning burner knobs
2 Oven /UV-en/ The oven preheats to three hundred and fifty degrees. Flat hand slides into opposite hand (putting in oven)
3 Pot /POT/ The large pot holds soup for the whole family. P-hands mime stirring in pot
4 Pan /PAN/ Felix cracks an egg into the hot frying pan. Mime holding pan handle, flip
5 Skillet /SKIL-it/ The cast-iron skillet cooks vegetables evenly. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Spatula /SPACH-oo-luh/ Use the spatula to flip the pancake. Fingerspell or sign each word
7 Whisk /WISK/ Amara uses the whisk to mix the batter smooth. Fingerspell WHISK
8 Ladle /LAY-dul/ The ladle scoops soup into each bowl. Fingerspell LADLE
9 Tongs /TONGZ/ Tongs grip hot food without burning your fingers. Fingerspell TONGS
10 Cutting board /KUT-ing BORD/ Felix chops vegetables on the wooden cutting board. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Knife /NYF/ A sharp knife slices the tomato neatly. Index finger slides along opposite index (cutting)
12 Peeler /PEE-ler/ The peeler removes the skin from the potato. Fingerspell PEELER
13 Grater /GRAY-ter/ Amara grates cheese on top of the pasta. Fingerspell GRATER
14 Colander /KOL-un-der/ The colander drains hot water from the pasta. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Measuring cup /MEZH-er-ing KUP/ The measuring cup shows exactly one cup of flour. Fingerspell or sign each word
16 Measuring spoon /MEZH-er-ing SPOON/ One teaspoon of salt is all you need. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Mixing bowl /MIK-sing BOHL/ Combine the ingredients in the big mixing bowl. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Blender /BLEN-der/ The blender makes fruit smoothies in seconds. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Toaster /TOH-ster/ The bread pops up golden from the toaster. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Can opener /KAN OH-pen-er/ The can opener turns around the edge of the can. Fingerspell or sign each word
21 Rolling pin /ROH-ling PIN/ Amara rolls out dough flat with the rolling pin. Fingerspell or sign each word
22 Baking sheet /BAY-king SHEET/ Place the cookies on the baking sheet. Fingerspell or sign each word
23 Pot holder /POT HOHL-der/ Always use a pot holder to pick up something hot. Fingerspell or sign each word
24 Apron /AY-prun/ Felix ties his apron before he starts cooking. Fingerspell APRON
25 Timer /TY-mer/ Set the timer for fifteen minutes. T-hand taps wrist
26 Recipe /RES-ih-pee/ The recipe says to add two eggs and one cup of sugar. Fingerspell RECIPE
27 Ingredient /in-GREE-dee-unt/ Each ingredient is measured before mixing. Fingerspell or sign each word
28 Boil /BOYL/ Bring the water to a boil before adding the pasta. Fingerspell BOIL
29 Simmer /SIM-er/ Let the sauce simmer on low heat for twenty minutes. Fingerspell SIMMER
30 Stir /STUR/ Stir the soup slowly so it doesn't stick to the bottom. Fingerspell STIR
31 Chop /CHOP/ Chop the onion into small, even pieces. Fingerspell CHOP
32 Dice /DYS/ Dice the tomatoes into tiny cubes. Fingerspell DICE
33 Slice /SLYS/ Slice the bread into thick pieces for sandwiches. Fingerspell SLICE
34 Grill /GRIL/ Felix grills chicken outside on the barbecue. Fingerspell GRILL
35 Bake /BAYK/ Bake the muffins at three hundred and seventy-five degrees. Fingerspell BAKE
36 Fry /FRY/ Fry the plantains in a pan with a little oil. Fingerspell FRY
37 Steam /STEEM/ Steam the broccoli to keep it bright green and crunchy. Fingerspell STEAM
38 Roast /ROHST/ Roast the vegetables in the oven until golden brown. Fingerspell ROAST
39 Season /SEE-zun/ Season the food with salt, pepper, and herbs. Fingerspell SEASON
40 Serve /SURV/ Amara serves dinner on colorful plates. Fingerspell SERVE
