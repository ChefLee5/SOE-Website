# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Water cycle /WAW-ter SY-kul/ The water cycle recycles Earth's water endlessly. Fingerspell or sign each word
2 Evaporation /ih-vap-or-AY-shun/ Evaporation lifts water from lakes into the air. Fingerspell or sign each word
3 Condensation /kon-den-SAY-shun/ Condensation forms clouds from water vapor. Fingerspell or sign each word
4 Precipitation /prih-sip-ih-TAY-shun/ Precipitation falls as rain, snow, sleet, or hail. Fingerspell or sign each word
5 Collection /kuh-LEK-shun/ Collection is when water gathers in rivers, lakes, and oceans. Fingerspell or sign each word
6 Cloud /KLOWD/ White clouds drift slowly across the blue sky. Both claw hands shape clouds, move across
7 Cumulus /KYOO-myoo-lus/ Cumulus clouds are big, white, and puffy. Fingerspell or sign each word
8 Stratus /STRAT-us/ Gray stratus clouds cover the sky like a blanket. Fingerspell or sign each word
9 Cirrus /SEER-us/ Thin, wispy cirrus clouds form high in the sky. Fingerspell CIRRUS
10 Thunderstorm /THUN-der-storm/ A thunderstorm brings lightning, thunder, and heavy rain. Fingerspell or sign each word
11 Blizzard /BLIZ-erd/ A blizzard is a severe snowstorm with strong winds. Fingerspell or sign each word
12 Hurricane /HUR-ih-kayn/ A hurricane forms over warm ocean water. Fingerspell or sign each word
13 Tornado /tor-NAY-doh/ A tornado spins in a funnel from the clouds to the ground. Fingerspell or sign each word
14 Drought /DROWT/ A long drought causes water shortages. Fingerspell or sign each word
15 Flood /FLUD/ A flood covers dry land with water. W-hands rise up from below
16 Barometer /buh-ROM-ih-ter/ A barometer measures air pressure. Fingerspell or sign each word
17 Wind vane /WIND VAYN/ A wind vane shows which direction the wind is blowing. Fingerspell or sign each word
18 Anemometer /an-eh-MOM-ih-ter/ An anemometer measures wind speed. Fingerspell or sign each word
19 Rain gauge /RAYN GAYJ/ A rain gauge measures how much rain has fallen. Fingerspell or sign each word
20 Meteorologist /mee-tee-or-OL-oh-jist/ A meteorologist studies and predicts the weather. Fingerspell or sign each word
