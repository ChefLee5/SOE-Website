# Word Pronunciation In the Story… ASL Sign 🤟 My Language
1 Hello /heh-LOH/ Say hello to everyone you meet. Flat hand salutes from forehead outward
2 Goodbye /good-BY/ Wave goodbye when you leave. Open hand waves fingers down
3 Please /PLEEZ/ Always say please when you ask. Flat hand circles on chest clockwise
4 Thank you /THANK yoo/ Show gratitude with thank you. Flat hand from chin moves outward
5 Sorry /SOR-ee/ Say sorry when you make a mistake. A-fist circles on chest
6 Yes /YES/ Nod yes with your hand. S-fist nods up and down (like head nodding)
7 No /NOH/ Shake no with two fingers. Index and middle snap to thumb
8 Help /HELP/ Ask for help when you need it. A-fist on flat palm, both rise up
9 More /MOR/ Sign more when you want extra. Both flat-O hands tap fingertips together
10 Done /DUN/ Sign done when you finish. Both 5-hands flip from palms-in to palms-out
11 Stop /STOP/ Sign stop to set a boundary. Flat hand chops down on opposite flat palm
12 Wait /WAYT/ Sign wait to be patient. Both 5-hands face up, wiggle fingers
13 Again /uh-GEN/ Sign again to repeat something. Curved hand flips into opposite flat palm
14 Understand /un-der-STAND/ Do you understand? Flick index finger up near temple
15 Don't understand /DOHNT/ I don't understand yet. Index at temple, flick down (negative)
16 Name /NAYM/ What is your name? H-fingers tap crosswise
17 Nice /NYS/ Nice to meet you! Flat hand slides across opposite palm
18 Friend /FREND/ You are my friend. Hook index fingers, then swap positions
19 Love /LUV/ I love you. Cross arms over chest, squeeze
20 I love you /EYE LUV YOO/ The universal ILY sign! Thumb, index, pinky up (ILY handshape)
21 Good /GOOD/ That is very good! Flat hand from chin to opposite palm
22 Bad /BAD/ That was bad. Flat hand from chin, flip palm down
23 Welcome /WEL-kum/ You are welcome here. Open hand sweeps inward toward body
24 Excuse me /ek-SKYOOZ/ Excuse me, please. Fingertips brush forward on opposite palm
25 Congratulations /kun-GRACH/ Congratulations on your success! Both hands clap, then shake fists
